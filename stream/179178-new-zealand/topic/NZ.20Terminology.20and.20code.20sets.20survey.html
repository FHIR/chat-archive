Topic: NZ Terminology and code sets survey

Daniel Thomson (Dec 03 2020 at 01:32):

Hi all, as part of the development of NZ national terminology services we're doing a survey. It is designed to capture a National view of current challenges in managing or consuming health terminologies and code sets, and requirements and opportunities for providing terminology services across the Health and Disability System. We welcome any feedback you can provide as it will contribute to the development of a nationally managed terminologies and code sets service for the Health and Disability System.

Live Survey Link: https://consult.health.govt.nz/data-and-digital/terminology-services
