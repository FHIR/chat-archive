Peter Jordan (Sep 12 2020 at 21:54):

I noted that the time for deploying the NHI and HPI Web Services has been pushed out to April 2021, with the specs being published in January 2021.

The 'Terminology Service' will be interesting...

"We will introduce a national terminology service and value set authority, and supply implementers with a free and open source terminology server in-a-box solution, packaged with our latest SNOMED release."

Given the absence of any procurement process or community engagement, what this will be is a matter of speculation. My guess is that it could be an instance of the SNOMED International Snowstorm (on FHIR?) Server, but we'll have to see what the API is and which terminologies it supports.
