Michael Lawley (Nov 20 2018 at 03:54):

Here's the query that we developed for Health Pathways that finds codes "relevant to" injury of ankle:

curl -X GET \
  'https://ontoserver.csiro.au/stu3-latest/ValueSet/$expand?url=http://snomed.info/sct?fhir_vs=ecl/%28%3E%3E%20%28125603006%29%20OR%20%3C%20%28125603006%29%20OR%20%28%2A%20:%20%2A%20=%20%3C%3C%28125603006%20.%20363698007%29%29%29' \
  -H 'Postman-Token: fb28e8b1-b131-4cef-a0fc-792951313583' \
  -H 'cache-control: no-cache'

Michael Lawley (Nov 20 2018 at 03:56):

This uses a pattern of the form:

https://ontoserver.csiro.au/stu3-latest/ValueSet/$expand?url=http://snomed.info/sct?fhir_vs=ecl/(>> ({{code}}) OR < ({{code}}) OR (* : * = <<({{code}} . 363698007)))

where {{code}} is replaced by your code of interest (in te above, this was 125603006 |Injury of ankle|
