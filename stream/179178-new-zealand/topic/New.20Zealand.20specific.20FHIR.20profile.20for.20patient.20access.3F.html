Hi @Isaac Vetter - we haven't defined any profiles yet in the NZ Base IG. The first release only has extensions, identifiers and terminology. We'll be starting on national profiles in the next release. However, the issue of patient access is most likely to be tackled in a new project that's just started to create an NZ derivation of the IPS and hopefully that will consider the IPA project.
