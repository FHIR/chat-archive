42 CFR 493.1291(c)(2) “The name and address of the laboratory location where the test was performed.”
a. The actual name of the laboratory as indicated on the CLIA certificate [OBX-23].
b. The actual physical location of the laboratory facility or location within the facility (including room, suite, floor as applicable) where testing is performed, as indicated on the CLIA certificate [OBX-24].
