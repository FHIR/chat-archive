Lloyd McKenzie (Feb 28 2019 at 15:47):

Encounters are an aggregator for healthcare events. That aggregation can be useful irrespective of whether it's used to drive the claims process. Whether it's useful in your environment or commonly done by systems in your environment, you'd need to investigate. FHIR certainly doesn't require the use of encounters if there isn't a specific reason to use them.
