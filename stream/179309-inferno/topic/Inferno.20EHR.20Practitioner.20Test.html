Robert Scanlon (Aug 14 2020 at 13:33):

We use the term 'EHR Practitioner Launch' somewhat colloquially, because that is generally how it is used. But technically it is about allowing 3rd party apps to extend your application, and be accessed seamlessly within normal use of your application (launched and then given credentials to access data that user has access to through the US Core API). Setting aside the terms 'EHR' and 'Practitioner', does doing that make sense for your application?

Cooper Thompson (Aug 14 2020 at 15:15):

I don't know how well the Program Edition will fit your needs for testing CMS Patient Access API requirements. There is some overlap, but I don't think CMS Patient Access requires the part of the ONC Cures certification that relate to the provider/practitioner or bulk data access.
