Robert Scanlon (Mar 31 2021 at 16:13):

Hi @Lakshmi Bhamidipati -- you are right, in a handful of cases we require seeing at least 2 values for an element, instead of just one, to properly verify the 'multipleOr' search requirement. This is one of those places. If there is a good reason why an ONC certified system would only support one value here, then we can loosen the restrictions on this test. We chose to lean on the strict side with our interpretation to start because in practice once something is optional nobody will support it.

James Dinsmore (Aug 25 2021 at 20:37):

We recently added an intent of "plan" to MedicationRequest (where previously we only included an intent of "order"). At one point the MedicationRequest Tests checked for all four intent codes (which included an intent of "order") but now it is only testing for an intent of "proposal" and "plan." So for us it is skipping USCMR-13 (which causes a failure). We'd prefer not needing to introduce another intent (since we now support two of them). Can all of the possible intent codes (including "order") please be added back to USCMR-01?
