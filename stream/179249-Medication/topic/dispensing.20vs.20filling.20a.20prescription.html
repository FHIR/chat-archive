Lin Zhang (Mar 27 2021 at 10:56):

Is there any difference between difference dispensing and filling a prescription ? HL7 EHR-S Functional Model seems to distinguish them. But I don't know why. Thanks.

Lloyd McKenzie (Mar 27 2021 at 14:44):

The dispense action often consists of two parts - preparing the dispense and actually supplying the med. In the community setting, the first doesn't always imply the latter. The first step involves processing the insurance, counting the pills, sticking them in a bottle, printing the label, reviewing the order to ensure it's right & complete, and putting the meds in a bag. The second involves receiving payment and handing the pills to the patient. If a patient doesn't show up, then you need to reverse some of the first set of steps.
