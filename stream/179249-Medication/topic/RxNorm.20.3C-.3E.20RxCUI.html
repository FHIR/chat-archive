Jose Costa Teixeira (Jan 15 2021 at 10:25):

Does anyone know of a RxCUI to RxNorm Crosswalk?

Lin Zhang (Jan 15 2021 at 13:12):

A key component of RxNorm is the Concept Unique Identifier (RxCUI). The RxCUI is a unique, unambiguous identifier that is assigned to an individual drug entity in RxNorm and used to relate to all things associated with that drug.

Lin Zhang (Jan 15 2021 at 13:13):

RxNorm concept unique identifier (RXCUI): This RXCUI always designates the same concept, no matter the form of the name and no matter in what table it is found. Drugs whose names map to the same RXCUI are taken to be the same drug.
