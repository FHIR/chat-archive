Lloyd McKenzie (Apr 30 2021 at 13:29):

I don't think 'unknown' makes any sense. I also think 'completed' is going to be a confusing name - because 'completed' meant something completely different in MedicationUsage. My leaning would be just having two statuses - "recorded" and "entered-in-error". Default status is 'recorded'. If you don't have evidence that something is 'entered-in-error', then you always presume it's accurate information (or at least "as accurate as usual").
