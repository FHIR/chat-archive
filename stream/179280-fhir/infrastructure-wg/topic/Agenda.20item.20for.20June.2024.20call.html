Eric Haas (Jun 20 2019 at 17:40):

Structured Documents has agreed in principle to transfer ownership of DocumentReference to OO. However, due to its broad scope OO thinks that FHIR-I may be a more appropriate home for it.

  • If FHIR-I decides that DocRef fits better in the OO domain, then OO will vote to assume ownership of DocRef and follow up with FMG.
  • On the other hand, if FHIR-I decides DocRef fits better in the FHIR-I domain, then OO will inform SD so they can reopen the issue to transfer ownership to FHIR-I.
