Lloyd McKenzie (Oct 13 2021 at 02:25):

Identifier is actually owned by MnM, so OO approving the request is actually out-of-order. I've re-opened on that basis.

UDI is an identifier "system" - i.e. it is already a unique kind of identifier. Identifier.type is used to establish generic classes of identifiers that could come from multiple systems. We specifically (and intentionally) excluded all of the v2 codes that referred to identifier systems.
