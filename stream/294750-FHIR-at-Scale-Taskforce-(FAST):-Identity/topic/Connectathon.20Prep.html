Julie Maas (Aug 10 2021 at 15:34):

In profiling the onlyCertainMatches parameter within the operation $match, we had discussed using count=1 to require that at most one match should be returned in a patient access workflow, however in the cross-organization match use case we're concerned with, there may be an EMPI service that is returning results from multiple sources. In this case should more than one match be permitted and do we want to just increase the permitted count to represent that, or should count=1 be clarified as meaning "1 result per source"? The latter seems more in line with what we intend.
