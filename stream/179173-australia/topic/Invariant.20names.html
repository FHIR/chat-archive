Topic: Invariant names

Richard Townley-O'Neill (Apr 03 2018 at 06:07):

In some HL7 AU resource profiles invariant names start with "inv-", e.g. au-device and in others they do not, e.g. au-address.
This is not followed in core FHIR or US Core.
It seems redundant to me.
I propose that we stop putting "inv-" at the start of the names of our invariants.
