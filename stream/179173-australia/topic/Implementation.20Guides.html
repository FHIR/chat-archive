Stephen Royce (Sep 01 2016 at 02:50):

Folks, I've been wondering about building instances of ImplementationGuide, and I'm trying to figure out what level of granularity we would expect IGs built by the Agency to sit at. Currently, all the IGs that I've seen appear to be organisation-wide (both DAF and Argonaut have a single IG). However, it seems to me that we might like to have something more granular. The choices are broadly

  • one IG for the Agency as already described;
  • having modular IGs, e.g. one for continuity of care, one for medications, one for Medicare-sourced data, &c.; or
  • one for each information exchange, i.e. a Discharge Summary IG, a Prescription IG, and Organ Donor IG, &c.

Does anyone have any thoughts or suggestions on the matter?
