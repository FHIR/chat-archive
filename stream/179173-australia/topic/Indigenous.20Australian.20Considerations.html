Topic: Indigenous Australian Considerations

Grahame Grieve (Aug 14 2019 at 08:25):

I attended a workshop on Indigenous Informatics at HIC today, and then had a discussion with Ray from CSIRO Herston who's working on Indigenous health issues.

Coming out of those discussions, I have the following proposal for consideration:

We should add an Indigenous Australian affairs appendix to the Australian Base FHIR IG. It would contain:

  • the Name status extension
  • the Australian language value set that covers native languages
  • A standard social determinents of Health questionnaire developed with an appropriate indigenous community
  • a note about how to tag resources, to mark that they contain information derived from or pertinent to indigenous communities (support various custody and provenance concerns that a strong in the existing community)

Grahame Grieve (Aug 14 2019 at 08:25):

Note that the first 2 things already exist; we're not adding them.

Grahame Grieve (Aug 14 2019 at 08:27):

the questionnaire part depends on finding an appropriate community to leverage existing work (e.g. 715). The rough idea is that we would work towards a common representation of a common SDoH questionnaire which would lead to more consistent data reporting infrastructure, which is clearly an operational challenge now. We have an opportunity to build community that will make a real difference here

Grahame Grieve (Aug 14 2019 at 08:28):

note that there's plenty of work to do on this proposal on several fronts, so I'm just raising the idea at the moment

Grahame Grieve (Aug 14 2019 at 08:45):
