Genevieve Luensman (Jan 18 2022 at 23:23):

We would like to have a robust conversation about all aspects of Occupational Data for Health (ODH) during the Public Health WG Thu Q3 session. Several ODH data elements are up for consideration for USCDI. We have multiple ODH-related products, including a Guide to Collection for EHR developers https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/docs/2022-101/ and a Functional Profile http://www.hl7.org/implement/standards/product_brief.cfm?product_id=498 plus the interoperability templates (FHIR is here: http://hl7.org/fhir/us/odh/STU1/ ) and an information model https://doi.org/10.1093/jamia/ocaa070 . We are also starting an ODH DAM do build on the existing model. We would especially appreciate discussion with EHR vendors, but welcome all feedback and conversation.
