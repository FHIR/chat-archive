Frank McKinney (May 11 2021 at 23:39):

Hi @Kate Dech . The IG is intended to help stakeholders that participate in fulfilling (broadly defined) a patient's specialty medication to obtain information they need. We envisioned the primary data recipients to be specialty pharmacies and HUBs, though the guide doesn't restrict to those.

I'm not very familar with SaveonSP or PrudentRx... If their relationship with the payer includes facilitating PA, their needs might be similar to a HUB's. A fair amount of the patient info that HUBs and specialty pharmacies get from the practice is used to obtain authorization from the patient's insurance.

Hope this helps. @Maggie Buchinger : Please chip in if you have any other thoughts

Maggie Buchinger (May 12 2021 at 13:21):

Hi @Kate Dech - to add just a bit on to that, one of the use cases specifically defined in the guide is supporting specialty enrollment for programs or services, which includes copay assistance or copay coupon services. It sounds like the Specialty Medication Enrollment implementation guide should be able to meet those needs. Happy to discuss further!
