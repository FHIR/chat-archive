In ISiK Stufe 1 it appears as if _has isn't mandated. This is however something that's quite often used to e.g. find Patients who had an encounter in a specified timeframe, or to find patients who have a particular Concern or responsible clinician. I could image that one would not wish to allow for nested _has constructs, but one level of _has would go a long way.
I imagine this has been discussed at some time in the past, is there a reason for not including support for _has ? Or have I simply looked at the wrong documentation pages ?

_has is definitely as mandatory on the following page: https://simplifier.net/guide/implementierungsleitfadenisik-basismodul/UebergreifendeFestlegungenSuchparameter

Alexander Zautke (Mar 04 2022 at 17:33):

Note that in this version of ISiK it is only mandatory for a limited set of references. Stufe 2 will make it mandatory for any reference marked as must-support.
