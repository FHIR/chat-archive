Michael Osborne (Apr 28 2017 at 09:01):

In the US they recommend (mandate) LOINC codes for ordering Pathology/Lab tests (I assume this is what you mean by screening). If you are after a set of SNOMED CT concepts for ordering you could check out the Australian work on this, where they mandate SNOMED CT for orders, and have a process for creating new concepts in the Australian extension.
https://www.healthterminologies.gov.au/ncts/#/access Looks like you require an account on the Australian NCTS site for access now. If you just want to play with a subset of Lab Procedures - this will get you there with no authentication:
https://ontoserver.csiro.au/stu3-latest/ValueSet/$expand?identifier=http%3A%2F%2Fsnomed.info%2Fsct%2F32506021000036107%2Fversion%2F20170331%3Ffhir_vs%3Drefset%2F1021000036104
