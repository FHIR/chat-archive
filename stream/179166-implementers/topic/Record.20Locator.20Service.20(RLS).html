John Moehrke (Jan 28 2021 at 17:02):

@Brendan Keeler I think you might be confusing MHD with MHDS. MHD is an API to many document sharing exchanges including (MHDS, XDS, XCA, XDR, etc). Where as MHDS is a full-stack solution for a document sharing exchange (HIE). MHDS is a profile of profiles and pulls in MHD, PDQm, PIXm, PMIR, SVCM, ATNA, mCSD, and IUA.

John Moehrke (Jan 28 2021 at 17:03):

More details on these topics are explained in the HIE-Whitepaper https://profiles.ihe.net/ITI/HIE-Whitepaper/index.html
