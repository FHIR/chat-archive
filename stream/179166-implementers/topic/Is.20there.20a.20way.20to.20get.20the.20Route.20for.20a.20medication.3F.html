Topic: Is there a way to get the Route for a medication?

John Josef (May 20 2019 at 14:56):

During a Medication search, the resource does not seem to allow for a Route, is this only available on MedicationRequest and MedicationStatement?

Lloyd McKenzie (May 20 2019 at 14:58):

The Medication represents the 'substance'. Some substance formulation can be administered via a variety of routes. You can use MedicationKnowledge if you're interested in recommended routes (and doses) when using the substance to treat different types of indications for different types of patients.
