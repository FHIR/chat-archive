Michelle (Moseman) Miller (Jun 02 2021 at 18:22):

There has been some ongoing discussion in Patient Care and Learning Health on how to handle this - most recent suggestion resulted in a change request, J#29730, to expand CareTeam.participant.onBehalfOf to be applicable when a given delegate or proxy is working on behalf of someone else (e.g. allow a Practitioner to work on behalf of another Practitioner). Patient Care has not voted on this change request yet, so comments are welcome and/or you can join any of our Thursday 5pm Eastern conference calls.
