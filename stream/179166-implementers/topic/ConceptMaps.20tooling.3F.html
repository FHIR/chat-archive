Michael Lawley (Nov 18 2016 at 01:51):

It should be borne in mind that Snapper's prime goal is not to be a general purpose or complete editing tool. It's instead intended to cover the simple case (where people may not even care/know about FHIR) and base cases of FHIR CodeSystem, ValueSet, and ConceptMap authoring. It's also very much a work-in-progress
