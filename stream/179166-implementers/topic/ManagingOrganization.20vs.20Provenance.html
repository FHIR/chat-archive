Lloyd McKenzie (Oct 06 2021 at 15:58):

Author is who performs a particular operation. The organization that the patient is a "patient of" might be the only organization allowed to update the Patient instance, but that's not a given. (Some organizations might allow external participants, including the patient themselves to update certain demographic information). Also, the author might be captured at a level of detail that is more precise than just the organization. E.g. "what practitioner changed this phone number".
