Topic: Languages spoke at a location

Cooper Thompson (Jul 08 2020 at 20:12):

Do any systems out there support tracking languages spoke at a location? Something akin to the signs you see in windows or advertisements (at least in the US) that say "Se habla español". FHIR already supports the concept of a schedulable interpreter service via the communication property on HealthCareService. However the "Se habla español" case seems more like you are marketing to the demographic that speaks that language. Asking for a friend (FHIR#25302).

Lloyd McKenzie (Jul 08 2020 at 22:19):

@Brian Postlethwaite ? Seems reasonable to have a standard extension, though whether it should be Location or HealthcareService (or both), I'm not so clear on.

Brian Postlethwaite (Jul 08 2020 at 22:20):

Yes, we have the property on HealthcareService already, this was the first request on Location - and was looking for support in the community to determine if it should be an extension and stay that way, or move to core for it.
