Daniel Venton (Feb 04 2022 at 14:53):

What about the Condition.meta.source attribute?

Lloyd McKenzie (Feb 04 2022 at 14:58):

Provenance is intended for detailed metadata about how a particular resource (or resource version) came to be.

Shamil Nizamov (Feb 04 2022 at 14:58):

You may use an associated Provenance resource.
