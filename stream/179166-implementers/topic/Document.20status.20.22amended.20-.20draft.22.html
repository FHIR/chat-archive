Topic: Document status "amended - draft"

Jose Costa Teixeira (Apr 12 2021 at 11:58):

How can we express the status of a document that, after being "final", is now being amended but the changes have not yet been validated, i.e. it is in draft amended mode?

Vassil Peytchev (Apr 12 2021 at 21:55):

The cleanest approach is to not use a document's "amended" status, but instead to create a new document, and use DocumentReference.relatesTo to express the relationship (replaces if the new document is the previous one with amendments applied, or appends if it just contains the amendment.)
