Kenny Blanchette (Dec 06 2018 at 20:23):

How are others modeling imaging results that a patient reports having been completed?
e.g. "I had a mammogram on 1/1/2018 that was negative." Based on the Boundaries sections and a few previous Zulip threads, we are considering using DiagnosticReport, Observation, or some combination of the two linked through DiagnosticReport.result (in addition to Communication).

Lloyd McKenzie (Dec 10 2018 at 23:58):

The question is where you'd expect it to show up in the EHR. I doubt most systems would include that sort of information alongside other DiagnosticReports. You might see it alongside other observations. Most typically you'd probably see it captured as a "note" - which EHRs are currently planning to represent using DocumentReference.
