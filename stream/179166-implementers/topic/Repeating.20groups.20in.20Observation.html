Eric Browne (Oct 20 2016 at 21:40):

@Grahame Grieve the two RCPA mapping scripts have some recursive issues. They are manifested in resource profiles and instances that have leaf nodes ( Observations with values) that also have a "has-member" relationships pointing to themselves. These errors also corrupt the hierarchical display of the profiles. Colorectal has 28 such errors. Prostate has 2. Search for "obs.related" in the mapping scripts.
