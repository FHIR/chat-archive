Topic: Standards for AI results, like NLP NER

Sam Schifman (Nov 04 2021 at 14:23):

Hi, My name is Sam Schifman, Chief Architect at Diameter Health, I have been doing some research into AI, particularly around NLP, and have noticed a lack of standards around the results coming from these solutions. For example, an InferredEntity and InferredRelationship resource types could capture a great deal of what these solutions output. Also, adding to the Meta tag we could link resource inferred or supported by InferredEntities to those resources and represent the confidence so that they can be used with full knowledge of their inferred nature. This could greatly improve the interoperability of this data and unlock its potential be used in multiple use cases. I was wondering if anyone else in the FHIR community is already thinking about this and, if so, how I could connect with them?
