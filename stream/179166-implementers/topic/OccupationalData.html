Lloyd McKenzie (Sep 12 2018 at 14:18):

Excuse my ignorance - does this mean the occupationalData will only available as a resource to use in the US? The elements within it look laregly transferable (am UK based).

@Jon Behr The OccupationalData resource won't be available for use anywhere. It was a proposed resource that has been removed. It will not be in any official FHIR release. The OccupationalData implementation guide has been balloted as U.S. specific because the project that's driving it was U.S. sponsored and we didn't have others at the table. If there's international interest, a generified version (without U.S.-specific bindings and rules) could be published, with the U.S.-specific IG defined as a subset. Ideally we'd want at least 2-3 other countries at the table for that.
