Hi FHIR Folks!

I just wanted to pop in here and pick the brains of anyone familiar with a utilization of the R4 "List" resource, and even more specifically the use of a List resource for ordering screens to patients wherein those screens are R4 Questionnaire resources?

I understand the general structure of the List resource and which are the functional fields for using it as a list of record items. What I was hoping for some insight on, was the formatting of the "coding" field inside the "Flag" field inside each Entry item. I see there is a specific one used in the examples for medication orders, etc. but I don't see any examples dealing with Questionnaires or screening orders.

Would love any and all advice, thoughts, feelings, ideas...!

Lloyd McKenzie (Apr 30 2020 at 22:31):

List isn't really for ordering anything. If you want to order someone to complete a form, you'd use either Task or ServiceRequest. You might have a managed List of Tasks representing someone's to-do list potentially.
