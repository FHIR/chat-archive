John Silva (Dec 07 2021 at 18:51):

Also for any tools that have automated the FHIR version conversion process using the FML will have problems. I guess the FML is not yet prescriptive enough and there are many corner cases that it doesn't handle that a "tool author" has to work around anyway. Oh well.
