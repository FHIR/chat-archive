Lloyd McKenzie (May 02 2019 at 13:58):

FHIR is a standard, not a pre-configured application, so you can't really "install" it. You can, however, deploy one of a wide variety of existing open source FHIR implementations - or you can create one of your own. A list of existing open source FHIR servers can be found here: http://wiki.hl7.org/index.php?title=Open_Source_FHIR_implementations
