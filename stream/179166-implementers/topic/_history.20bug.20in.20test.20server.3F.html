Topic: _history bug in test server?

Steve Munini (Nov 15 2017 at 02:32):

I may have found a bug in the http://test.fhir.org/r3 test server related to deletion of resources - they are showing up as POSTs in history when they should be DELETE. Steps to reproduce:

POST http://test.fhir.org/r3/Patient - create a patient
PUT http://test.fhir.org/r3/Patient/262510 - update the patient
PUT http://test.fhir.org/r3/Patient/262510 - change something, and update the patient again
DELETE http://test.fhir.org/r3/Patient/262510 - delete the resource
GET http://test.fhir.org/r3/Patient/262510/_history - get the history

The top most <entry> is showing a POST and not a DELETE. Also, the <url> does not have the resource id in the path.

<entry>
<link>
<relation value="audit"/>
<url value="AuditEvent/131883"/>
</link>
<link>
<relation value="audit"/>
<url value="AuditEvent/131883"/>
</link>
<fullUrl value="http://test.fhir.org/r3/Patient/262510"/>
<request>
<method value="POST"/>
<url value="http://test.fhir.org/r3/Patient"/>
</request>
<response>
<etag value="W/4"/>
<lastModified value="2017-11-15T02:16:10.Z"/>
</response>
</entry>
