Topic: Are Claim.Location and Encounter.Location different?

Bo Pritchard (Aug 03 2018 at 18:08):

Are Claim.Location and Encounter.Location signifying different resources.

Lloyd McKenzie (Aug 03 2018 at 20:37):

The granularity could be different. For example, Encounter.location could be XYZ hospital emergency, while Claim.location might just be XYZ Hospital (or vice versa). There might be other use-cases for them to differ if the claim submission requirements and clinical workflow requirements differ. Also, there might not always be an Encounter for the Claim - e.g. capitation-based claims, provider-to-provider consults where there's no associated patient encounter, etc.
