Marc de Graauw (Mar 24 2020 at 09:12):

I know here EA XMI's are being converted to ART-DECOR datamodels which can be exported as FHIR Logical Models. So it's doable - I don't expect this to work out of the box for other environments. @Alexander Henket

Alexander Henket (Mar 24 2020 at 09:19):

Those XMIs conform to very specific rules enforced by DCM-like modeling. The DCM plugin from Results4Care is used in EA, and a couple of additional rules have been applied. @Michael van der Zel would be in the know of the EA side. The import scripting aims to transform into ART-DECOR only. ART-DECOR can then export into logical models. Michael has also been working exporting Loigcal Models from EA directly. Not sure where he landed with that.
