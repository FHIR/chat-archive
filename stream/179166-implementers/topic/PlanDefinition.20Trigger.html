<trigger>
    <type value="data-changed"/>
    <data>
        <type value="Condition"/>
        <codeFilter>
            <path value="code"/>
            <valueSet value="http://cts.nlm.nih.gov/fhir/ValueSet/2.16.840.1.113883.3.117.1.7.1.279"/>
        </codeFilter>
    </data>
</trigger>

Bryn Rhodes (Nov 01 2019 at 18:43):
