  • question about the use of additional flags around extensible bindings GF#16333)
  • remove url as an option for ElementDefinition.binding.valueSet? (GF#16055)
  • make ElementDefinition.constraint.expression option?
  • move Bundle.signature to an extension? (GF#16392)
  • questions about Bundle.securityContext (GF#14563)
