Robert Dieterle (Apr 19 2021 at 16:59):

As far as I am aware, the CMS Interoperability Final Rule does not have any Payer reporting requirements. The proposed reducing burden rule required specific reporting on the patient access API and Prior Authorization. Since this proposed rule was not published in the Federal Register, the reporting requirements are not currently part of regulation.
