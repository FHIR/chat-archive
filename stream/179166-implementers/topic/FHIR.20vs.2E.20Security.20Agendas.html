Lloyd McKenzie (Jan 15 2019 at 21:04):

I do the best I can with what's available when I get to prepping the agenda (which this time was Friday). I filter by what I recognize as being definitely or at least likely FHIR related and I invite anyone who knows better/more to update. It's actually quite common for different WGs to identify different topics for the same joint session - or one WG to indicate a plan for a joint that the other WG appears to know nothing about. (And I generally send emails when it looks like there's going to be confusion.) However, if agendas change after compliation, there's nothing I can do if no one tells me (or better yet, updates the wiki themselves). I have a dream of moving all WGM agendas into Jira so that there's a single source of truth for all agendas that everyone can look at, filter, monitor, indicate planned attendance, etc. But first, Jira balloting... :)
