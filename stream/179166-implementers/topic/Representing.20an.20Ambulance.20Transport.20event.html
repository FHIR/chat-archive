John Moehrke (Jun 01 2021 at 15:48):

Paramedicine Care Summary maps the flow of the patient information from the ambulance patient record, commonly known as the electronic Patient Care Record (ePCR), to the hospital Electronic Medical Record (EMR) https://wiki.ihe.net/index.php/Paramedicine_Care_Summary

John Moehrke (Jun 01 2021 at 15:49):

Routine Interfacility Patient Transport supports interoperability between systems used on transport vehicles between healthcare facilities. https://wiki.ihe.net/index.php/Routine_Interfacility_Patient_Transport
