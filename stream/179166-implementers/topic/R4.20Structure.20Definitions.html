Richard Townley-O'Neill (Apr 24 2019 at 00:13):

On http://hl7.org/fhir/R4/resourcelist.html choose the resource you are interested in then look for the text

See the Profiles & Extensions and the alternate definitions: Master Definition XML + JSON, XML Schema/Schematron + JSON Schema, ShEx (for Turtle) + see the extensions & the dependency analysis

and choose the link to the xml at "Master Definition XML"
