Topic: Registering implementations?

Tilo Christ (Nov 22 2021 at 23:09):

I was wondering how my implementations of some low-maturity (FMM0 + 1) specifications can contribute to bumping up the maturity level of these (mostly extensions in the SDC realm, such as usageMode). I am aware of the description at https://www.hl7.org/fhir/versions.html#maturity and the following:
The maturity model is significantly influenced by the degree and type of implementation activity using an artifact. For this reason, we encourage implementers to register their implementations . A detailed analysis of the basis for the maturity metrics for FHIR artifacts can be found here .

However, both the registration link and the maturity metrics link are broken. Are implementations still being tracked and is this the way how to mature a spec? Or would I need to participate in a Connectathon and show it off?

Lloyd McKenzie (Nov 23 2021 at 02:53):

We're just starting to track maturity at the implementation guide level. For the pending release of SDC, most content will be marked as level 2, with net new things marked as 0 (draft). One of my objectives for next year's release is to gather more information about usage so we can nudge the maturity of somethings up to the 4/5 level.
