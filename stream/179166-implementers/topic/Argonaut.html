Venkat (Jun 05 2018 at 07:30):

What is the main difference b/w the FHIR Specification and Argonaut ?

Kevin Shekleton (Jun 05 2018 at 08:15):

Argonaut defines a set of profiles of the FHIR specification.

Lloyd McKenzie (Jun 05 2018 at 13:07):

The FHIR Specification is international in scope. Argonaut constrains FHIR for on U.S.-specific implementation for human patients and focuses on the needs of clinical sharing for primary care EHRs (rather than public health, research and other use-cases)
