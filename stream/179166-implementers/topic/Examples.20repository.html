Lloyd McKenzie (Jan 03 2022 at 05:02):

If you think there are examples that would be helpful to include in the core specification (because they help highlight useful aspects of the resource or usages implementers might not necessarily understand from the current examples, you can also use the 'propose a change' link and submit a change request, including your proposed example as an attachment. (Be sure to explain in the change request why you feel the additional example would be helpful/useful - and of course make sure that the example data is data that can be freely published without violating privacy or IP.)
