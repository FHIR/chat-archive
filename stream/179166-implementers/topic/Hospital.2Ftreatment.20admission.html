Brian Postlethwaite (Apr 26 2018 at 07:02):

This may be captured using a Quesitonnaire (as a form) / QuestionnaireResponse, but ultimately will be other resources such the ones you've mentioned (Obs/Reports...). It depends on you admission scope/workflows/forms.

Lloyd McKenzie (Apr 26 2018 at 17:24):

For high-risk things that you want flagged to everyone interacting with the patient, consider Flag - it's intended to essentially provide the equivalent of orange stickies on the front of the patient chart - high-priority/low-volume. More detail on something like mobility, falling risk, Hep B would be captured as Condition - because they're something that can evolve and are tracked over time. A formal DNR might be expressed as Consent or Contract.
