Are there any profiles or groups working on wound care in FHIR? We've had a few folks ask about how wound data should be represented in FHIR - the first resource that came to mind was observation or some type of related group of observations..

Bryn Rhodes (Jun 11 2018 at 21:25):

The CIMI group has done extensive modeling of wound assessment, but I'm not sure whether they're available as FHIR profiles yet. Certainly a good place to start though. Do you know @Susan Matney ?

Danielle Friend (Jun 11 2018 at 21:41):

Thanks @Bryn Rhodes - are those published somewhere I could take a look?

Bryn Rhodes (Jun 12 2018 at 03:17):

@Danielle Friend , they are published as Clinical Element Models here: http://www.opencem.org/#/20171114/Intermountain/WoundAssert, work to represent them as FHIR profiles is apparently underway, though I don't know what the timeframe is. The work is very detailed and has apparently been validated by national wound experts, so should be a great place to start.
