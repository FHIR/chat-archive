Topic: Observation.category for blood glucose

Michael Christensen (May 23 2017 at 13:01):

What would the Observation.category be for a blood glucose measurement (performed by the patient)? None of the existing codes (http://hl7.org/fhir/codesystem-observation-category.html) are a perfect match to me.

Eric Haas (May 23 2017 at 16:39):

laboratory

  • this is a ''preferred" binding in R3 so you can use your own category code or have more that one. like patient reported

Jim Kreth (May 23 2017 at 19:25):

In particular for glucose results, we use 2 categories: "laboratory" and "point-of-care". The first is glucose results typically returned as part of a panel and the second is where PoC meters are used and recorded.
