Bryn Rhodes (Dec 10 2017 at 18:43):

Measure calculation can be performed for a specific practitioner (there is a practitioner parameter to the $evaluate-measure operation), but it could also be performed for other granularities from a single patient up through an entire population. In the case that a measure is evaluated for all the patients for a single practitioner, it would make sense for the practitioner to appear on the MeasureReport, but so far we've only needed reportingOrganization. I'd be happy to see a tracker to add practitioner though.
