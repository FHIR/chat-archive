Lloyd McKenzie (Jul 08 2021 at 14:13):

Historically, they've been dealt with when one of the committers had time/decided they were high enough priority. We're trying to move to a process where there's more community vetting, at least for changes that will impact author or reader experience. That process is just getting off the ground though. If it's a bug and it's having a significant impact on you, raising it on #IG creation and waving your hands at Grahame or Mark Iantorno is the best way to accelerate resolution.
