Lloyd McKenzie (Sep 28 2019 at 04:31):

I don't think any of our IGs follow all the best practices yet. (And the best practices continue to evolve). Not sure what you mean by "All the implementation guides". Given that anyone who wants can create one and there's no requirement to register them anywhere, there's no list of all. The HL7 registry is here: http://www.fhir.org/guides/registry/
The continuous integration build ones are here: https://fhir.github.io/auto-ig-builder/builds.html (considerable overlap with the previous list)
Simplifier has a whole lot more: https://simplifier.net/

Grahame Grieve (Sep 28 2019 at 10:53):

Ward Weistra (Sep 30 2019 at 09:41):

Indeed, Simplifier guides are here: https://simplifier.net/guides (It has no manual curation like the HL7 list, so there are some test IGs in there, even while they are not linked to a test project.)
And as Grahame mentions we eventually want to role it into https://registry.fhir.org to give you one place to find all registered IGs.
Of course people are still free to not register IGs, keeping them just unlisted or private.
