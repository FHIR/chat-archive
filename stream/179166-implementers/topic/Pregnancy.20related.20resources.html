Lloyd McKenzie (Feb 18 2021 at 20:17):

Right. Gestational age would be an Observation. Most of the data in a pregnancy record will be Observations. However, there'll be some MedicationAdministrations, Immunizations, Procedures and other things too. Systems can (though not many do) link all information related to a particular Pregnancy using EpisodeOfCare and the extension that allows linking various resources to it.
