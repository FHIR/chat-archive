Eric Haas (Oct 24 2019 at 16:18):

We had polled the Argonaut community earlier this year and nobody had implemented at that time. We have discussed updating the status of Argonaut guides to indicate the level of community support.

Cooper Thompson (Oct 25 2019 at 15:02):

Epic has implemented the provider-based scheduling use case from that IG. We've implemented both the client and server roles. Currently, it's only Epic-to-Epic since to our knowledge nobody else has implemented it, but our intent was to build something that could be used Epic to non-Epic once other implementations exist.
