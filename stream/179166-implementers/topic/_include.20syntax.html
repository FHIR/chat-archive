We're seeing a FHIR server which supports _includes in the form of Observation?_include=Observation:patient,Observation:practitioner (note the comma separated list) instead of Observation?_include=Observation:patient&_include=Observation:practitioner . It seems they were reading something in http://build.fhir.org/search.html#revinclude which inspired this usage - nevertheless, it's wrong and not the intent of the standard. Have we seen this syntax implemented anywhere else ? (if so, some clarification in the standard may be in order)
