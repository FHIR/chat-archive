Topic: Forge 18.2.1 for STU3 - HIMSS 2018 Edition hotfix

Michel Rutten (Mar 08 2018 at 14:51):

We've published a Forge STU3 hotfix that fixes an annoying issue reported by the community, where Forge would not clear the dirty flag after saving a profile to disk with the "Generate Snapshot Component" option enabled. Forge will automatically detect and install this update during startup.

Change log: https://simplifier.net/ui/OrganizationNews/Show?id=45
Download from: https://simplifier.net/forge/

@sai charan, @Giorgio Cangioli and @Mounika thank you for reporting this issue!

If you find another issue or would like to submit a feature request, please contact us at forge@fire.ly.
