Paul Knapp (Mar 17 2017 at 11:42):

Regarding DSTU2 - the resources are draft and incomplete, use STU3, the final should be published next week, consult the Current Build until then. Note that STU are STU - for trial use and it is entirely possible that breaking changes will be made between now and Release 4 in about the summer of 2018.
I'm not sure what you mean by 'capturing all the fields in the same resource (claims). Wherever you see Claim.element->Name the 'Name' is a resource of type Name (Resources begin with a capital letter). In your example above Practitioner is the name of the resource which holders the name of the referring provider, it is referenced in the resource ReferralRequest which is itself referenced in the Claim resource.
We can of course also discuss whether I mapped the right elements.
