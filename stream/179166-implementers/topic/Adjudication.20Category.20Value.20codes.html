Paul Knapp (Jul 18 2019 at 16:02):

@Mona O Typically the adjudication sates what the insurer will pay (benefit amount) and may state the copay, deductible, eligible amount (max billed amount to be considered) - in general the patient is responsible for the amount billed less the benefit and may state a patient responsibility amount but in fact an insurer cannot really determine the patient responsibility as the patient may have other insurances which will cover some or all of that amount.
