Ward Weistra (May 14 2020 at 14:13):

Hi @Lauri Karppinen, on Simplifier.net we render on the first tab for:

  • Profiles: An interactive tree-view of the profile (diff/hybrid/snapshot)
  • Examples/FHIR Instances: The narrative from the resource

Looking at other DiagnosticReport instances the narrative seems to render (example STU3 / example R4).
When a narrative is empty, we will show: "The narrative of this resource is empty." (example).
When the narrative tab shows "The resource cannot be rendered." generally there are major issues with the profile. You should be able to hit the Validate button on the resource page and see those errors listed. See this one for example.

Does your resource have any validation errors? If you can share a link, I could have a look at it.
