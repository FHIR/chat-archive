Lloyd McKenzie (Dec 05 2016 at 16:50):

@Naminder Soorma Examples are part of the quality criteria. So for a resource to hit FMM level 3, it must have examples such that at least one example exercises every data element in the resource, examples are all clinically appropriate and show the full intended scope of the resource. So all resources should get to that point. But for low maturity resources, we don't expect as much - because the resources are still subject to significant change and because writing good examples is hard.
