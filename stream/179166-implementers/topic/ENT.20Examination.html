Lloyd McKenzie (Nov 01 2017 at 15:01):

The guidance is the same as usual - what information can typically be looked at independently and what needs to be looked at together - that drives whether you're dealing with one Observation or multiple. In terms of one profile vs. multiple, I suspect you can get away with a single profile that covers left and right. Whether you need to create separate profiles for different aspects depends on whether the constraints are separate.
