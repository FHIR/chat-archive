Topic: Looking for a volunteer for patient managed sharing

Grahame Grieve (Jul 09 2017 at 20:50):

In San Diego, we'll be trailing a connectathon stream around patient managed sharing of information. One of the key aspects of this connectathon stream is that the patient organises for 2 EHRs to communicate about them, without the patient being in the loop. See http://wiki.hl7.org/index.php?title=201709_Consumer_Centered_Data_Exchange for details.

Grahame Grieve (Jul 09 2017 at 20:52):

One of the steps in this connectathon stream is that the patient authorizes two EHRs to communicate with each other, and then the EHRs have to decide that they trust each other. (this is different to where the patient simply directs the EHR to share data with any application, and the EHR need not decide for itself whether it trusts the application, since the patient does. That's a useful scenario, but this scenario is different, because the EHRs can recognise and trust each other as authoritative)

Grahame Grieve (Jul 09 2017 at 20:53):

We are working with Aaron Seib from Nate Trust on the trust verification step, and he's looking for someone to volunteer to implement a web service for the connectathon that fronts for the verification step

Grahame Grieve (Jul 09 2017 at 20:55):

as the volunteer, you'd probably already be planning to attend the connectathon, and you'd be interested in a patient centered healthcare system, and you'd be able to work with Aaron to prototype a simple but useful service for the connectathon.

Grahame Grieve (Jul 09 2017 at 20:55):

if you're interested in this role, PM me directly, or email me, and I'll hook you up with Aaron.

Grahame Grieve (Jul 09 2017 at 20:55):
