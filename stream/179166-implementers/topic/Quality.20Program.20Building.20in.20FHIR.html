Aaron Seib (Oct 29 2018 at 02:25):

Hi - I stumbled upon this thread while I was thinking through a use case that I think may fit in this stream:

A value based payment program needs the clinical cancer staging information for each patient under the care of an APM participant. To calculate relevant quality measures, an extract of clinical cancer staging information is required bi-annually from the EHRs of participating providers for their attributed patients. Having successfully extracted the data from CEHRT calculations of measures are performed and reported back to the participants and the program administration.
