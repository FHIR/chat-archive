Patrick Werner (May 16 2017 at 18:47):

DRGs are the "result" of a Diagnosis, Procedure, characteristics of the patient like age, gender, and a episoed of care/ encounter. Therfore it isn't just a resource but a combination of many resources. As the main purpose is reembursement this will lead to a claim in the end.
To capture the necessary combination of data - a profiled composition would be a possible way to go.

Lloyd McKenzie (May 17 2017 at 16:27):

DRG is derived from a bunch of things, but in the end it's still a single coded assertion. Thus far, it's only been identified as a requirement on the claims side. If you think it needs to be on Encounter too, submit a change request.
