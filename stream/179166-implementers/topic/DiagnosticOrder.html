Lloyd McKenzie (Jan 02 2017 at 18:24):

@Grahame Grieve With the workflow changes, the status of a request/order represents only the status of the authorization as determined by the placing system. It does not reflect the progress of the workflow. Workflow progress is always managed using Task. (The reason being that the same request/order can be associated with multiple workflows - an order might be rejected by one lab, cancelled at another lab and fulfilled by a third - yet it's still the same requisition.)

Lloyd McKenzie (Jan 02 2017 at 18:25):

Also, it's problematic for an order (which might have a digitial signature) to be updated by the filling system to reflect its agreement to say "accepted", "in progress", etc.
