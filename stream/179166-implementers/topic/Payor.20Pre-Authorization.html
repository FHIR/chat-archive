@Bo Pritchard Not at the account level as a patient may have multiple coverages the benefits of which would accrue to the account.
A pre-authorization is a use on the Claim Resource which represents a claim, preauthorization or a predetermination. Please see the Claim and ClaimResponse as well as examples and join the Financial Management WG calls, Tuesday at 11 Eastern for discussions.
