Lloyd McKenzie (Apr 21 2016 at 20:53):

There's a large set of different types of devices, equipment and general products that may have various manufacturing and/or configuration parameters that will vary by device type. Creating separate resources for each doesn't make a lot of sense to me. While the basic optical characteristics for vision prescriptions haven't changed much over the years, there are some new things (contacts rather than glasses, tinting, etc.) and there'll likely be more new things in the future. Having a generic structure makes more sense to me.
