Brian Pech (Mar 31 2016 at 21:08):

The FHIR management group is pleased to announce that registration for FHIR Connectathon 12 is now opened. This event will be held on the Saturday and Sunday (May 7-8) prior to the May WGM in Montreal, Quebec.

Information about the upcoming Connectathon can be found at :
http://wiki.hl7.org/index.php?title=FHIR_Connectathon_12
At this site, there is general logistic information as well as descriptions of the proposed tracks that will be taking place at the Connectathon. This site also provides links to pages that provide details about each track and information about track leads.

Registration for the Connectathon and the HL7 Working Group Meeting is available at: http://www.hl7.org/events/wgm052016/.

FMG would also request that Connectathon participants submit information on a tracking spreadsheet which will allow us to assure sufficient allocation of space and resources for the various tracks.
https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1gAnNmyurFDopLYJakQOtXWYGxvuoiDn49n8qRI5mlb4/
