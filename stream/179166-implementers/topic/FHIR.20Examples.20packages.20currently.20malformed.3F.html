Lloyd McKenzie (Aug 24 2017 at 19:19):

There isn't a requirement that references resolve. We do raise warnings in the build process when this happens, but work groups aren't required to address those warnings until their resource hits FMM 3.

Lloyd McKenzie (Aug 24 2017 at 19:19):

(Creating and maintaining examples is a large amount of work, so we don't impose overly tight expectations when resources are still undergoing significant change.)
