Hi @Ramandeep Dhanoa, if you publish your profiles to Simplifier, the platform will render your profiles, similar to the HL7 FHIR spec. Simplifier also provides a user-friendly online Implementation Guide editor with integrated profile rendering. For example:
https://simplifier.net/guide/Basisprofilerno-basis/no-basis-Patient

Lloyd McKenzie (Apr 03 2019 at 19:50):

To host it under hl7.org, there are three possibilities - you can ballot it through HL7, you can have your local HL7 affiliate ask to have it hosted there or you can do it through fhir.org (though you need to be a member).

Ramandeep Dhanoa (Apr 03 2019 at 19:54):

@Lloyd McKenzie Do you guys have any documentation around these steps?

Lloyd McKenzie (Apr 03 2019 at 22:31):

There's tonnes of documentation around balloting, but really you just need to do the first step which is to find a work group that wants to sponsor your implementation guide. You can see a list of work groups on the HL7 home page by clicking on "resources". If you're going through an affiliate, your affiliate will have processes. When they get to the point where they're ready to publish, they should send an email to fmgcontact@hl7.org. If you want to do it through fhir.org, reach out to @Grahame Grieve
