John Moehrke (Dec 11 2019 at 19:00):

This functionality would be specific to your transport. Thus you need to identify your transport. In the case of the IHE protocols, the intended recipient is an element of the DocumentManifest. This is the manifest of a communication, like a packing slip in a shipping container.
