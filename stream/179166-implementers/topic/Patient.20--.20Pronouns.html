Daniel Tam (Jan 27 2022 at 05:00):

Are any providers here recording patient pronouns? We don't see this as a field in the Patient US Core profile. We're thinking about creating our own extension to record this, but are wondering what others are doing.

Note that this is separate from the Patient.gender field. That field maps to Administrative gender (male, female, other, unknown)

The pronouns would be how the patient wishes to be addressed i.e.:
he/him/his
she/her/hers
they/them/their
Ze/hir/hirs, ze/zir/zirs

https://www.health.nd.gov/sites/www/files/documents/Files/HSC/HEO/Pronouns.pdf
