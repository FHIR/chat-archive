Nathan Hall (Nov 01 2018 at 14:58):

If I am a user of a FHIR server, and I want to hit an endpoint to get Duplicate Drug Therapy (sending a payload of medications and potentially patient conditions) , what endpoint would I hit? As the server, what resources might I send back?

Lloyd McKenzie (Nov 01 2018 at 15:41):

A few options. You could define a custom operation. The endpoint would be whatever name you define for your operation. E.g. [base]/Patient/foo/$dupdrugs or something like that. The response could be a Bundle of DetectedIssue instances. Alternatively, you could use CDSHooks with their medication-prescribe hook and you'd get back a bunch of cards (and possibly DetectedIssue instances too, though not sure how many EHRs support those).
