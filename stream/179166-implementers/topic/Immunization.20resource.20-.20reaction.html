Lloyd McKenzie (Nov 15 2021 at 05:25):

I think it's legitimate to say it could be either Observation or Condition. There's a fair bit of flex in terms of where information goes depending on the system and organizational (and individual) preference. If a clinician thinks the patient's state is something they want on the problem list and will manage as it evolves, then it can be Condition. Even something like "sore arm" could, in theory, be captured as a Condition and managed if someone really wanted to.
