Michele Mottini (Feb 09 2022 at 16:02):

I assume you are talking about the US core profile (https://www.hl7.org/fhir/us/core/StructureDefinition-us-core-diagnosticreport-note.html), the answer is yes, that binding is extensible so if you have other categories you can use them

Lloyd McKenzie (Feb 09 2022 at 17:24):

More specifically, if you have other categories that don't fall within one of the existing categories, you can use your own. If they're sub-categories of one of these 3 codes, you must specify the 'standard' codes in addition to your own.

Lloyd McKenzie (Feb 09 2022 at 19:28):

You're allowed to use additional codes, but you MUST use those codes if they apply. I.e. if you're dealing with some kind of radiology report, you must send LP29684-5. You're free to send additional codes though.
