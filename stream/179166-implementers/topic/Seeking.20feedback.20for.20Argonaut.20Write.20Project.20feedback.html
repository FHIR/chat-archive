Eric Haas (Apr 19 2021 at 18:51):

A new Argonaut project for 2021 is to provide guidance for supporting FHIR “write” use cases to an EHR. We have agreed to include the following Patient Generated Data categories as in scope use cases:

  • Vitals (e.g. : Weight, Blood Pressure)
  • Activity (e.g. Steps)
  • At home diagnostics (Blood Glucose, Covid 19 Test)
  • Sleep
  • Medications
  • PROS/Assessments

We would like feedback from the FHIR Implementer community for additional categories that we may have omitted.

Note that the Patient Empowerment Work Group is currently working on a Patient Corrections IG and things like Demographic Updates are out of scope.

Tilo Christ (Apr 21 2021 at 16:25):

The photograph piece is becoming more and more requested in "early discharge" and "hospital at home" scenarios. You discharge patients a few days earlier and let them document the progress of their healing through a camera. This has accelerated quite a bit due to the pandemic along with many other patient-facing virtual care use-cases. So the goal is not to let patients randomly send in pictures, but rather to agree with them that every day they would take a picture, take their temperature, and document symptoms and pain. This will then be looked at by nursing staff.
