Lloyd McKenzie (Nov 02 2017 at 16:43):

Whether racial/ethnicity information is captured for demographic purposes and what sort of information is relevant tends to be highly country-specific. In most countries it's not done (or is even illegal). Where it is done, it's driven by the historical background and sensitivities for the specific country, thus its use as an extension. If you're interested in ancestry information for clinical reasons (e.g. Ashkenazi Jewish heritage), that information is best captured as an Observation.
