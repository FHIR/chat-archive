John Moehrke (Dec 29 2020 at 13:29):

I agree that this is an important new perspective and a different perspective than before. However I don't think that it is a fundamental change to the modeling. I would express it more as an expansion of the model. In that everything that an EHR does today is still useful, and often for inpatient they are plan focused. But for out-patient or chronic-care; this is where the EHR tends to fail. The EHR could encompass these other domains, and some EHR vendors seem to want to do that. But the patients and other forces in the market are demanding movement far faster. Thus I say it is an expansion of the model, not a totally new model. One where the EHR is a participant, but so too are others in the plan, and so too is the patient (etc).
