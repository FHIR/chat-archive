Lloyd McKenzie (Sep 02 2021 at 15:49):

If a search parameter isn't recognized, it'll generally be ignored. The search parameters actually used will be reflected in the 'self' link in the search response

If you search simply http://fhir.hl7fundamentals.org/r4/Observation what do you get back? Is it the same result you get from http://fhir.hl7fundamentals.org/r4/Observation?_subject=500 ?

Jean Duteau (Sep 02 2021 at 17:04):

It is ignoring the _subject. The first query is only returning observations with a subject of Patient/500. The second query is returning all observations.
