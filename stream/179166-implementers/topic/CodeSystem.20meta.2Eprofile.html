Katarina (Jan 29 2020 at 13:47):

Hi, can I define a CodeSystem without meta.profile? Or do I have to use http://hl7.org/fhir/StructureDefinition/shareablecodesystem for meta.profile?

Yunwei Wang (Jan 29 2020 at 15:00):

Why not? meta.profile is not required.

Katarina (Jan 29 2020 at 15:44):

When I do not define meta.profile, the CodeSystem will be validated against http://hl7.org/fhir/StructureDefinition/CodeSystem?

Lloyd McKenzie (Jan 29 2020 at 16:28):

You never have to declare meta.profile. Declaring a profile doesn't mean it'll be validated against, and systems can choose to validate against profiles even if they're not declared in the instance.
