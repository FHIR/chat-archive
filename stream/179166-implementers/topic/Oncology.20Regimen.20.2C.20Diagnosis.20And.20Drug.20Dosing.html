In particular, I led a development effort to create an Order Template authoring tool which could emit their Chemotherapy Order Templates (https://www.nccn.org/professionals/OrderTemplates/Default.aspx) in FHIR format, using the http://hl7.org/fhir/stu3/plandefinition.html resource. I'm happy to discuss informally, but I'd encourage you to reach out to NCCN for the latest on that.
