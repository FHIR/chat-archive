Lloyd McKenzie (Mar 24 2020 at 23:18):

There's two options. You could just include the jurisdictional health number as a Patient.identifier and the existence of that identifier alone is sufficient for systems to infer the existence of coverage. Alternatively, you can use Coverage. Whether the financial responsibility is managed by the state or by a commercial enterprise shouldn't much matter to the applicability of the resource. (If you find a situation where that's not true, please holler :) )

Sean O'Quinn (Mar 25 2020 at 01:04):
