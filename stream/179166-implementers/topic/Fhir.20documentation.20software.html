Hi all, does anyone know what software I can use to document my implementation of fhir resources; e.g the extensions I am adding.

Lloyd McKenzie (Mar 16 2018 at 16:34):

You're looking for two sets of tooling - profile authoring tools (e.g. Forge or Trifolia) and publication tools (e.g. the FHIR IGPublisher or Simplifier. I'd recommend starting with Simplifier as it's the most straightforward. If you need something more sophisticated, you can explore the other options.

Igor Sirkovich (Mar 16 2018 at 18:25):
