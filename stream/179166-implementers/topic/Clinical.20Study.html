Lloyd McKenzie (Feb 27 2016 at 23:55):

We'd love to develop resources in the clinical trials space but have been waiting for implementers to come forward. The relevant work group at HL7 is RCRIM (Regulated Clinical Research Information Management). To move this forward, you'll need to engage with that group. I'm happy to support you as you walk through the process.
