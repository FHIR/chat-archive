Morten Ernebjerg (May 27 2021 at 07:08):

You can choose to map the answers to other FHIR resources before transferring them (often, that would be Observation resources). For instance, if one yes/no questions is "Do you smoke?", you can map that to an Observation resource capturing the smoking status.
