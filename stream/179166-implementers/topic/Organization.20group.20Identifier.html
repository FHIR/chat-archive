Topic: Organization group Identifier

Kimberly Kenna (Mar 30 2021 at 14:05):

Typically Identifiers for resources are unique for that resource in context of the system + value combination. Is it also appropriate to place a group identifier for a resource's Identifier field? For example, an Organization is a member of a group that has a unique group ID. Can that group ID go into the Organization.identifier field also?

Lloyd McKenzie (Mar 30 2021 at 14:27):

The requirement is that system + value are globally unique. Identifier doesn't support conveying combinations of elements which, together, foster uniqueness. If you're referring to insurance coverage, 'group' goes into Coverage.class.value.

Kimberly Kenna (Mar 30 2021 at 14:39):

That's what I expected. Thanks for confirming!
