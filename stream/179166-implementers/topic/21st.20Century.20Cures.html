Josh Mandel (May 22 2020 at 21:35):

IANAL, but the EHI definitions in ONC's final rule are aligned with the HIPAA Designated Record Set definitions:

We have defined EHI (§ 171.102) to mean electronic protected health information (ePHI) as the term is defined for HIPAA in 45 CFR 160.103 to the extent that the ePHI would be included in a designated record set as defined in 45 CFR 164.501 (other than psychotherapy notes as defined in 45 CFR 164.501 or information compiled in reasonable anticipation of, or for use in, a civil, criminal, or administrative action or proceeding),
