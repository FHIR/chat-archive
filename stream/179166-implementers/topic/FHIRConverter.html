Hi @Ananya Choudhury, there's a few implementations of the FHIR Mapping Language, besides the one by Firely: https://confluence.hl7.org/pages/viewpage.action?pageId=76158820#UsingtheFHIRMappingLanguage-MappingEngineImplementations
As I understand it, they generally work with modelling your source data model (which could be a CSV file) in FHIR logical models, converting your own data in any way to XML/JSON representations conforming to that and next using the mapping language to perform the conversion.

I'm not sure if @David Hay would have an update to his blog post on this: https://fhirblog.com/2019/03/25/importing-csv-data-into-a-fhir-server/
