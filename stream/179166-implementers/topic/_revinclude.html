Lloyd McKenzie (Mar 07 2017 at 18:00):

Your understanding is correct. I believe the healthintersections.com.au server works (be sure you're testing the STU 3 version). That said, the query you're making is a dangerous one. A given patient may have thousands or tens of thousands of observations. So if you execute the search and set a page size of 50 patients, you could easily get back 500k Observations in your first page. (And you may find that some servers will therefore refuse to perform the query)
