Nata Samarina (Nov 19 2021 at 13:21):

Hello. What resource is better to use for storing planned dates and dates of attendance at dispensary observation?
Dispensary observation is the observation of a patient who has a chronic disease. Observation can be lifelong. For the dispensary observation map, we use the EpisodeOfCare resource. We don't really like the idea of storing planned dates and attendance dates in EpisodeOfCare.
