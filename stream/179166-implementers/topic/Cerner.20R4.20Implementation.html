Grahame Grieve (Apr 09 2019 at 22:16):

it is possible to put a facade of one version in front of another version. There are a number of disparate issues to address:
- conversion of content between versions - you'll need bi-directional transforms, and the ones that exists are of variable quality. But they do exist, and good testing will be needed
- renaming of resources (particularly Conformance->CapabilityStatement) and search parameters between versions. We do not publish a map of search parameters between versions, though it's possible that we could - but the semantics can change subtly
- changing mime types between R2 and R3
- additional features made available in later versions - .e.g operations
