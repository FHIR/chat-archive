Abbie Watson (Jun 02 2017 at 14:42):

We have a veterinary prototype using Patient (it also uses Practitioner, RelatedPerson, Person, Organization, and Medication). It started off as an app modeling horse races, and uses data from the Kentucky Derby (they have 100+ years of veterinary and performance data). I wouldn't exactly call it production ready, and we've never found another app to exchange veterinary data with via REST API; but the FHIR resources handled the internal data modeling just fine; and we were able to exchange data via database to our other FHIR compliant apps (i.e. we loaded up the horses from the Derby Demo into our ProviderDirectory demo).
