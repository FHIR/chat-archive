Paul Lynch (Aug 22 2019 at 20:08):

We have a FHIR server at https://lforms-fhir.nlm.nih.gov/baseR4/Questionnaire which has LOINC panels as (unofficial) Questionnaires, plus some made up Questionnaires.

Noemi Deppenwiese (Aug 23 2019 at 08:27):

https://medical-data-models.org/?lang=en also has a "download as FHIR" option.

Daniel Vreeman (Sep 14 2019 at 18:13):

We've been doing some pilot work to create a FHIR Questionnaire representation of LOINC panels and forms based on the Argonaut profile. They're available for testing, but not yet part of the official release/distribution. With a loinc.org account (auth required), you can access them here https://fhir-dev.loinc.org/Questionnaire/. Just a warning that this is a temp server. But one you can try out this weekend.
