Lloyd McKenzie (Sep 09 2017 at 19:22):

@Fan Lin Servers can look at an instance, validate it against the profiles they know of and tag the instance with those profiles it happens to comply with. However, there's no expectation that servers will do so and given that doing so is quite costly in terms of processing effort, it's unlikely to be common. It'll be more typical for profiles to be asserted by the author of the instance prior to submitting to the server.
