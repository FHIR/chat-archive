Gino Canessa (Nov 05 2019 at 19:17):

Argonaut and DaVinci are both HL7 Accelerator Programs (among others).

Functionally they have similar goals (advancing standards and adoption), but they are different organizations with different approaches, policies, and membership. Depending on which projects either is working on at a given time there may be little or a lot of overlap in concerns.
