Brian Postlethwaite (Jun 05 2017 at 12:49):

If you refer to the adminstrative module page showing the linkages between these resources http://hl7.org/fhir/administration-module.html#clinical-reg
you can see that the encounter can link to account(s), and that a patient can have multiple accounts.
It is up to your business rules which accounts should be used, and who is going to cover the charges.
For example some charges may go to a government program, others to an insurer, and others to the patient themselves.
