All of the continuous integration builds got wiped if they hadn't changed recently. The will only come back if/when someone runs a continuous build for the relevant IG. All of the 'official' releases should still be there. We've changed processes to ensure this doesn't happen to the main builds of HL7-published IGs going forward. It will still happen to IGs published by others though unless they've communicated with HL7 that they want their builds protected as well.
