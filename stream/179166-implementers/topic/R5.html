Ward Weistra (Jun 24 2021 at 12:53):

Also replied by email, but for completeness:

With our Firely FHIR tooling we try to follow the official FHIR releases as closely as possible:

  • Currently the latest stable FHIR release is FHIR R4
  • The version FHIR R4B is its final stages of being released, mainly making changes in the medication and evidence-based medicine space of the specification
  • The stable release of FHIR R5 is not expected until Q1-2 2022

As such the current stable versions of Forge (https://simplifier.net/forge) include STU3 and R4 and the R5 version will remain a preview release until R5 is officially released.
