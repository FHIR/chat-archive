Jason Walonoski (Sep 30 2016 at 13:46):

SyntheticMass is now up and running and publicly accessible (http://syntheticmass.mitre.org/). Add fhir/metadata to get the FHIR endpoint.

It currently has about 1 million synthetic patients (some dead) who can have diabetes, opioid addiction, heart disease, and injuries. Alzheimer's and lung cancer will be added soon.

The goal is to have 7 million patients with the most prevalent conditions modeled.

All code is on github https://github.com/synthetichealth
