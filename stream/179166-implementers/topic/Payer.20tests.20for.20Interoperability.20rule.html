Carie Hammond (Jun 08 2021 at 23:54):

Touchstone has the EOB tests for C4BB: https://touchstone.aegis.net/touchstone/testdefinitions?selectedTestGrp=/FHIRSandbox/CARIN&activeOnly=true&contentEntry=TEST_SCRIPTS
It's available with a free subscription - you just need to register. You can get there to register at touchstone.com and the instructions for registering and running tests are at our online documentation: https://touchstone.aegis.net/touchstone/userguide/html/registration/index.html

That said, we are always around to answer questions and you can reach us here by tagging #Touchstone or email us at touchstone_support@aegis.net and we'll be happy to help you along if you have any questions. Happy Testing!
