ravi.kuchi (Nov 03 2021 at 11:14):

Hi @Friis80 , we (at Fresenius) have used FHIR for messaging in our FHIR ecosystem. We sync data from our EHR (which does not speak FHIR yet) to HealthCloud (Salesforce) via FHIR resources. OfCourse there is transformation involved from EHR -> FHIR -> HealthCloud but we are doing EHR -> FHIR already for other applications so we leveraged the same canonical model to supply data to HealthCloud. We transmit to the volume of 20M- 40M messages per day and we use kafka for the streaming. We have been in production for over 4 years and have had no issues. Notice that we are on STU3 currently.
