Lloyd McKenzie (Aug 01 2019 at 18:10):

Observation would be used to measure inputs and outputs. (And also for calculations based on the comparison of those two things.)

Siddharth Chamarthy (Aug 01 2019 at 19:49):

Are there any open or secured sandboxes that have such data? I want to assess the frequency of these output measurements

Lloyd McKenzie (Aug 01 2019 at 19:52):

A sandbox wouldn't necessarily reflect reality in terms of frequency. (Frequency would also vary by patient state and institutional practice.)

Siddharth Chamarthy (Aug 01 2019 at 19:57):

Okay I understand! So I could expect the Urine Output data to be available in the Observation Resource using a loinc code that corresponds to Urine output!

Lloyd McKenzie (Aug 01 2019 at 20:00):

In the U.S., most likely. In other countries, they might choose to adopt a different code system. (And in some places, they wouldn't use standardized codes at all...)
