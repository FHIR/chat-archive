Elliot Silver (Jun 25 2021 at 20:10):

I'd suggest tying them together with an EpisodeOfCare. There is a standard extension you can use on most resources to tie them to the EpisodeOfCare, although I'm not sure it can be used for medications/theraputic agents.

There is also ResearchStudy, and a similar extension allowing you to associate most resources with a particular study.
