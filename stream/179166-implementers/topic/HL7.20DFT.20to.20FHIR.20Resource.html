Does anyone know code/solution/resource to convert HL7 DFT-Detail Financial Transaction to FHIR resource?

Craig Newman (Jun 28 2019 at 12:57):

There is a v2-to-FHIR project but we haven't tackled DFT yet. General details are available at https://confluence.hl7.org/display/OO/2-To-FHIR+Project or on the v2-to-FHIR Zulip stream. We're in the process of mapping some of the DFT segments (PID, PV1, etc) but haven't gotten to all of them yet. If you're working on a DFT transformation we'd happily take your input and add it to the larger project.
