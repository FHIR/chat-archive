Lloyd McKenzie (Jan 27 2017 at 22:49):

The Excel spreadsheets are intended for internal HL7 use only. As the StructureDefinition resource evolves, it's much less work to update the extract code than it is to update all of the spreadsheets. I don't think there's any plans to increase consistency. And there is an intention to retire the use of spreadsheets for anything other than resource definition. That doesn't mean it couldn't happen, just that it's not terribly high priority
