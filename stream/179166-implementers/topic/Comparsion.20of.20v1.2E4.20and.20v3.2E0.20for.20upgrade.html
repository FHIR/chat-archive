Lloyd McKenzie (May 26 2017 at 15:11):

There's a tab in each resource showing differences between DSTU2 and STU3. That's probably a reasonable place to start, though obviously there will be some differences that are already reflected in 2016May and there may have been some changes in 2016May that got reversed or further changed before STU3 was published.

Jenni Syed (May 26 2017 at 16:08):

If you want to drink from the firehose,http://hl7.org/fhir/stu3/history.html#3.0.1 gives a high level view and http://hl7.org/fhir/stu3/diff.html is detailed to resource. As Lloyd said, it's hard to tell what was changed at that level from 2016May
