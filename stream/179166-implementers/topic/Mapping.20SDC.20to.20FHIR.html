Brian Postlethwaite (Jun 05 2019 at 10:52):

Rooms and beds would be locations.
The spec has examples on what could be in both
http://hl7.org/fhir/location.html#example
http://hl7.org/fhir/organization.html#example
Sometimes things aren't that straight forward in the wild, and we find that there are some points in hierarchies where an item might be created as both an organization and a location for different reasons.
And you are likely to only map the levels that you actually need to track/care about.

Brian Postlethwaite (Jun 05 2019 at 10:52):
