Michelle (Moseman) Miller (Sep 01 2016 at 20:02):

To elaborate on the (unresolved) debate:
The Patient Care work group discussion included: "Can a dictated H&P (all text) be Composition? Composition is never the primary source of information. Purpose of Composition is to define a table of contents."
http://wiki.hl7.org/index.php?title=2016-02-04_Patient_Care_FHIR_Call#Define_boundaries_between_ClinicalImpression.2C_Observation.2C_and_a_new_ClinicalNote_resource
http://wiki.hl7.org/index.php?title=2016-02-18_Patient_Care_FHIR_Call#ClinicalNote
http://wiki.hl7.org/index.php?title=2016-02-11_Patient_Care_FHIR_Call#Note_vs_ClinicalImpression_Boundaries

The Composition resource says "Composition defines the structure, it does not actually contain the content: rather the full content of a document is contained in a Bundle, of which the Composition is the first resource contained."
http://hl7-fhir.github.io/composition.html
