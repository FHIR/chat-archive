Elliot Silver (Nov 23 2018 at 17:40):

Under STU3 and R4, you'd have to use extensions to correlate images referenced in ImagingStudy to Binary resources. The images wouldn't be attachments to the ImagingStudy. You could use multiple Media resources instead of ImagingStudy, or in conjunction with ImagingStudy, but I don't think that gets you there either. We purposely designed ImagingStudy for use with a DICOM archive.

Nick Hatt (Nov 23 2018 at 22:05):

Depending on what you are trying to accomplish, you might want to connect to the PACS system instead of Epic/The EHR. EHRs and RIS systems hold documentation, and I think Epic has enough data to say, hydrate an ImagingStudy, but the PACS will hold the actual binary DICOM file.
