Topic: ✔ Patient to Practitioner Relationships

Josh Haskins (Aug 30 2021 at 21:17):

Hi. One of our source systems allows the defining of Patient to Practitioner relationships outside of an Encounter. These relationships can be Case Manager, Dentist, Psychiatrist, and around 25 more.

So far I have come up with the following FHIR resources to put this information:

  • RelatedPerson
    • Not meant for health care relationships.
  • Patient.generalPractitioner
    • Only good for the general practitioner, not for all the different types of relationships we have.
  • CareTeam
    • It’s not really a care team, as they operate individually of one another.

What would be the optimal way of representing these relationships?

Thank you.

Lloyd McKenzie (Aug 30 2021 at 21:24):

I'd go with CareTeam. It may be that the CareTeam members don't act like much of a team, but from a wholistic health perspective, they are one whether they recognize it or not. And it would be a CareTeam 'managed' by the patient themselves, in that they'd define who's in which role.
