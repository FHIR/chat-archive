Christiaan Knaap (Oct 16 2017 at 10:03):

We implemented this very recently, and a first check reveals that we don't account for it. If you have an Observation with valueQuantity:
- _elements=value: works, you get the valueQuantity in the result.
- _elements=valueQuantity (or any other value<ElementType>): does not work.
Indeed the spec could be clearer about this.
