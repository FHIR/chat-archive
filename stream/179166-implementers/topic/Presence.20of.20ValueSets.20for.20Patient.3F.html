Topic: Presence of ValueSets for Patient?

Paul Lynch (Jun 04 2020 at 16:58):

Is it expected that a FHIR server with Patient resources will also have the ValueSets associated with Patient (e.g. http://hl7.org/fhir/ValueSet/administrative-gender)?

Vassil Peytchev (Jun 04 2020 at 17:13):

"have the ValueSet" - do you mean would they have a FHIR endpoint where you can retrieve the ValueSet?

Paul Lynch (Jun 04 2020 at 17:45):

Yes. So, for example, if [base URL]/Patient works and returns something, could we expect [base URL]/ValueSet/administrative-gender would return that definition and that [base URL]/ValueSet/administrative-gender/$expand would return the expansion?

Vassil Peytchev (Jun 04 2020 at 17:47):

I don't think so, but it is just an un-informed opinion.

Rob Hausam (Jun 04 2020 at 18:07):

I agree with Vassil. I don't think that would be an expectation (and certainly not a promise) for a general FHIR server. If the server also provides at least some basic terminology service capabilities (as declared in its capability statement) then that could be done, and certainly might be a "nice to have", but I think that's as far as it would go.
