René Spronk (Oct 07 2021 at 10:48):

The mapping shown is to the HL7v3 RIM, not to CCDA. Such mappings are mere informational and not intended as (the basis for) implementations. If you're looking for a mapping from CCDA to FHIR, you should look elsewhere, e.g. https://github.com/HL7/ccda-to-fhir/ , http://www.hl7.org/fhir/us/ccda/ or others that the US-based FHIR community would care to suggest. Note that tehre is a #cda to fhir stream on this forum where you're likely to find others working on similar issues.
