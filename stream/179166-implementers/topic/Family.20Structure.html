Lloyd McKenzie (Oct 20 2020 at 15:29):

Coverage allows you to indicate both the policyHolder, subscriber and beneficiary. That's generally as deep as a hierarchy gets. Beneficiaries need to have a relationship to the subscriber. Individuals with relationships to beneficiaries don't really matter - it's the relationship to the subscriber that counts. If you query all Coverages for a given subscriber, you can find the collection of covered individuals.
