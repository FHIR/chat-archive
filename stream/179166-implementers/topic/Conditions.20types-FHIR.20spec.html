Josh Mandel (Oct 07 2021 at 12:59):

Some of these have other homes in FHIR, but not necessarily exclusively. For example, you'll want to review

  • Observation with a category of "social-history" and "exam" (for what you labeled as "socialHistory" and "cognitiveStatus")
  • AllergyIntolerance (for what you labeled as "reaction")

In some cases, if you're observing something and tracking it on a problem list, FHIR would say that you may need two resources represent it (I'm curious how common this is in practice).
