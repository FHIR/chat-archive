Lloyd McKenzie (May 30 2018 at 03:34):

Creating a standard generally requires a community of people who want to design something - and implement the results. So it'll take more than you, but one person/organization can certainly get the ball rolling. In terms of making money, there are lots of business models. It could be with an independent product. It could be with an app or service that plugs into existing systems. It could be from a knowledge data base. It could be from services and/or training.
