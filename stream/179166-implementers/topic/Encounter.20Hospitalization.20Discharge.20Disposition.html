Michele Mottini (Oct 22 2021 at 18:11):

'Must Support' does not mean that you have always to populate it - it means (as a server) that if you have that value you have to make it available in the FHIR resource.

Michele Mottini (Oct 22 2021 at 18:12):

So if you do not have a discharge disposition for the outpatient encounters in your system it is fine just not to populate FHIR Encounter.hospitalization.dischargeDisposition

Michele Mottini (Oct 22 2021 at 18:14):

(I'd say that outpatient encounter probably would not have the entire hospitalization element - and this is fine for US Core)

Michele Mottini (Oct 22 2021 at 18:15):

and especially: do not try to default or invent a value if you do not have anything in your system
