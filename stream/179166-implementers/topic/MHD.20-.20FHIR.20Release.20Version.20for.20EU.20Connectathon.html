I would like to know which FHIR release version is relevant for MHD at this year's Europe Connectathon. Is it R3 or R4? John Moehrke (Jan 16 2019 at 15:39):

IHE Connectathon next week will be testing the STU3 version of all of the IHE Profiles that leverage FHIR. There is an oustanding Public Comment ballot on a small first set of profiles to align with FHIR R4. Once that is approved then IHE Connectathon will move to those specified profiles.
That said, you might be able to find peer-to-peer partners willing to test R4...

John Moehrke (Jan 24 2019 at 21:15):

A bit of an update... Given that MHD and the other priority set, will be finishing the Public Comment resolution at very best March 1; the time difference between that and EU Connectathon is too short. So EU Connectathon will formally be testing the STU3 flavor of ALL of the IHE profiles. Anyone bringing a R4 flavor will likely just be doing cross-testing with peers also supporting R4.
