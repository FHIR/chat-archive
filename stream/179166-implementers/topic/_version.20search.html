John Moehrke (Oct 03 2018 at 13:47):

The meaning of Delete is very local policy specific. Some forms of delete will erase all versions, some will just mark the status of the Resource, others might delete and leave behind a Provenance of that delete action. So the answer on searching for deleted resources is use-case specific and policy specific.
