Lloyd McKenzie (Aug 02 2019 at 14:25):

@Smita Hastak @Rik Smithies This sounds like participating location. Participating location seems like it should be an Organization as it's about responsibility, not about physical walls. If a clinic was participating and they moved across the street, they'd still be the same "participating location" - they'd just have a new address. I.e. the Organization would be the same even though the Location would be a distinct instance.
