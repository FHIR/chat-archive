Topic: New IG templates - needs latest publisher

Lloyd McKenzie (May 16 2021 at 23:38):

If your IG is building with the 'current' template, I've just pushed a change that means you'll need the most recent IG publisher (i.e. 1.1.72). This new release does a few things:

  • adds next/previous and top/bottom links to every page in the IG
  • adds links to CSV and Excel representations of all structure definitions
  • adds support for a 'dragon' class for divs which will allow you to put 'here be dragons' sections in your IGs in the same manner we have them in the core spec
  • ensures that Provenance artifacts that are included in the IG to provide history don't show up in the artifacts list or table of contents

If you have any issues, please make sure you're running 1.1.72 or newer before reporting them.
