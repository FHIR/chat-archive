Christof Gessner (Aug 10 2020 at 09:43):

EU uses ISO 3166 (except for Greece EL). UK and GB are no longer in the list of EU codes https://publications.europa.eu/code/pdf/370000en.htm
UK is actually an official ISO 3166 code, albeit with a status of "exceptionally reserved", according to https://www.iso.org/obp/ui/#iso:code:3166:UK it refers to the United Kingdom. So it is a synonym of GB https://www.iso.org/obp/ui/#iso:code:3166:GB
