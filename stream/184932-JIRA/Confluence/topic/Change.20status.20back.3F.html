Lloyd McKenzie (May 27 2020 at 19:08):

Triaged is a function that Melva normally does for all issues - Work groups aren't expected to look at non-triaged items (and in many cases, the work group isn't even assigned). Work-group-specific triage is generally handled by filling in 'grouping' (ready-for-vote, fit-for-teleconference, etc.)

Lloyd McKenzie (May 27 2020 at 19:10):

There's no way to go back to Submitted from Triaged because all that Triaged means is that the issue has a work group filled in and seems to be expressed in a sufficiently coherent way that a work group could resolve it. (If the latter test fails, it gets assigned back to the submitter for clarification).
