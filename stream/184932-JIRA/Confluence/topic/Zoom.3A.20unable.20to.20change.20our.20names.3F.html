David Johnson (Aug 03 2020 at 20:03):

How can I prevent participants from changing their names?

The meeting host (host) selects Manage Participants from the basic menu at the bottom of the window.

Select ” Details ” at the bottom right and uncheck ” Allow participants to change their name “.
