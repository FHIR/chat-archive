Lloyd McKenzie (May 13 2021 at 04:06):

@Lynn Laakso note on the issue with Column B. The value must be "2021-May - HL7 FHIR IG SMART APP LAUNCH R2 STU" - i.e. with spaces on either side of the dash following the time-period. An exact match is needed
Agree that column I issue should go away when B is fixed.
The 'submitter' in the spreadsheet is always the person voting. onBehalfOf is never specified if you're referencing an existing Jira item. If you're referencing a new item to be created, it's the person who actually has the comment - and should be the 'reporter' of the issue when it gets created in Jira. This must be someone who has a Jira account. Everyone you're gathering comments from in your organization needs to have a Jira account for you to be able to submit their comments correctly.
