Sarah Gaunt (Apr 20 2021 at 23:13):

And if not, could they be added to the fields that can be edited in a bulk edit? @Lloyd McKenzie

Lloyd McKenzie (Apr 21 2021 at 15:06):

You can bulk change them. However, the change is "replace", not "add" or "remove". I.e. if there are 3 groupings, there's no bulk change that could drop one and leave the others unchanged.

Lloyd McKenzie (Apr 21 2021 at 15:06):

You would use the 'transition' option and the "adjust fields" or "adjust groupings" (depending on whether the issue had been resolved or not).
