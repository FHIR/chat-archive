Joshua Procious (May 05 2021 at 15:49):

@Paul Denning Generally speaking it has to do with the Characters in the title of the page. Confluence will use "pretty" format with the space/page-name unless the title of the page has special characters from this list: % & ? / \ ; " § + Additionally, there are tiny urls available for copy/paste from the page properties. If someone copies that it will not convert it and you'll see something like: https://confluence.hl7.org/x/OmT9Aw
