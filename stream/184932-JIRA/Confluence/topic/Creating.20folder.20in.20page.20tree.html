Lloyd McKenzie (Feb 25 2021 at 23:26):

You can organize pages in a hierarchy. Just create a page for the year and nest your meeting minutes under the appropriate year. There's a special control you can put on the 'year' page that will give you an ordered list. You can see it here: https://confluence.hl7.org/display/FHIRI/FHIR+SDC+meeting+minutes+2021

Lloyd McKenzie (Feb 25 2021 at 23:26):

To see the page hierarchy, click on the ... and say "View in Hierarchy"
