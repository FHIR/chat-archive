Corey Spears (Feb 01 2022 at 17:03):

Wouldn't it be nice to have a full dashboard for managing ballot results and reconciliation? Lets's make that happen. I am collecting feature ideas here.

I had put together some dashboards for some January balloted IGs and after talking to Lloyd, found out there was an interest in creating ballot dashboards across all ballots.
Here is what I threw together:
https://jira.hl7.org/secure/Dashboard.jspa?selectPageId=14005
image.png

But f we can make this official, there are a lot more backend development options.

Some initial feature thoughts (made available through selectable pre-developed and configurable widgets):

  • Graphical voting statistics
  • Heatmap of reporters (reporters' organizations)
  • Tickets submitted during ballot period without ballot votes
  • Tickets by most votes - Find the tickets with the biggest bang or most number of interested parties
  • Tickets by in-person requesters - aiding with in-person planning
  • Block voting management workflow (with notifications to in-person requesters, reporters, and voters)

Much of this is available to the Savvy Jira user, but the idea is to make this more widely available for the many that aren't. I would also unlock capabilities not generally available through regular dashboard creation.

Other ideas? Throw them in here.
