Melva Peters (Jun 03 2021 at 21:17):

This is a required step when you are transitioning issues from "submitted" to "triaged" if the comment submitter has not included the responsible Work Group. If you are doing this in one step (ie proposing disposition) on an issue that has a status of "submitted", then you will have to add the responsible Work Group. Typically the Work Group is added when the issue is triaged.
