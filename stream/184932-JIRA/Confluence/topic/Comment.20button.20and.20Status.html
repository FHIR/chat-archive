Topic: Comment button and Status

Paul Denning (Nov 22 2019 at 17:25):

Seems like the comment button is disabled if the status is Applied (and others). What is the rule that determines if the comment button is enabled?

Lloyd McKenzie (Nov 22 2019 at 18:50):

Hi @Paul Denning. We don't allow comments to be attached to anything that's been fully actioned because, typically, no one ever looks at those tracker items again. If you have a concern/question/correction, it should either be raised on chat.fhir.org or some other discussion forum, or - if you think further changes are needed - by raising a new change request and including a pointer to the original. While people who are subscribed to monitor that particular task could receive an email because your comment was added, it's not guaranteed anyone would. And even if they did, such emails often get lost in a blizzard and never seen/acted upon.
