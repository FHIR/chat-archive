Stream: Helios Accelerator

Topic: 2022 Priority Areas

Craig Newman (Apr 08 2022 at 12:58):

The Helios Interim Steering Committee has identified three priority areas for 2022. 

Make Data in Public Health Information Systems Accessible in Bulk (Starting with Immunization Information Systems)
Align and Optimize Public Health Data Sharing
Deliver Aggregate Information to Public Health

For more details on the priority areas, please visit the Helios 2022 Use Cases Confluence page (https://confluence.hl7.org/display/PH/2022+Use+Cases).

In the next several weeks, the Helios Interim Steering Committee will be recruiting and standing up an Operating Committee who will work to further refine the scope of the three priority areas into use cases that can be implemented within a year.  Project Teams are also being assembled to achieve the objectives in the priority areas.

Sign-up now to participate in the Helios Project Teams and help Helios advance public health interoperability!  Contact Us: Helios@hl7.org
