To get an understanding of the eCR Now FHIR App,
Is it trying to send CDA document to the CDC?

Brian Gugerty (May 13 2020 at 20:58):

#Vishak OS - I may not be the best person to answer your question, but my understanding is that the eCR Now FHIR App packages up CDA document and send it the the AIMS Platform, which is hosted by APHL. The AIMS platform routes it to the appropriate local public health jurisdiction, e.g. a state dept. of health or a City health department. De-identified data also goes to CDC.

John Loonsk (May 13 2020 at 22:24):

States are the ones who send some, de-identified data to the CDC as part of the NNDSS. They do not send eICRs. And, yes, so that it can connect to the rest of the infrasturucture at AIMS and in public health agencies, the eCR Now app uses FHIR on the EHR side and CDA eICR 1.1 on the public health side.
