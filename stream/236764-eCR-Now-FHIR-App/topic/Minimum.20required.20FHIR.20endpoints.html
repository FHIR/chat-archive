Santosh Jami (Feb 03 2021 at 10:28):

@Jay Rankin

  1. First step is to integrate the SMART on FHIR on your FHIR server
  2. Provide the right client credentials into the eCR Now App
  3. If you launch with only PatientId, eCR Now will review the Patient reosurce and retrieve all the relevant FHIR resources associated with that Patient namely: Encounter, DiagnosticReport, Observation, ServiceRequest, Immunization, Document, MedicationRequest, Pracitioner, Organization - all of these will be utilized to publish the eICR.

As mentioned, if the Step #1 and #2 are done the right way, we need not worry which FHIR resources eCR Now will be reviewing. Thanks
