Michelle (Moseman) Miller (Apr 19 2016 at 13:30):

One possible way of articulating the relationship, as documented in the Argonaut implementation guide (http://argonautwiki.hl7.org/index.php?title=Implementation_Guide), is "The Argonaut data element query is based on the HL7 U.S. Data Access Framework (DAF) FHIR Implementation Guide. However, the Argonaut use case and requirements per resource are a subset of those of the DAF implementation guide". CC: @Brett Marquard
