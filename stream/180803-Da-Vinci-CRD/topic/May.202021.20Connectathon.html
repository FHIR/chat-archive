I'm not sure I understand the question. Access to the EHR by a CDS service is always a 'potential' part of the CDS Hook workflow. Lloyd McKenzie (May 19 2021 at 03:20):

The SMART app approach could certainly be used by others, the issue is whether they'd be able to serve up the information a payer would need to make the decision on whether prior authorization would be required. The SMART app basically passes in the draft order and (ideally) the pre-fetch information. If that's enough to make an automated decision on prior authorization (or give any other guidance), then that could work
