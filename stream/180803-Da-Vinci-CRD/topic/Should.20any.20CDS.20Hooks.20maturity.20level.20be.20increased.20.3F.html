Isaac Vetter (Nov 13 2019 at 17:11):

Should any CDS Hooks mature from use of hooks in CRD?

Isaac Vetter (Nov 13 2019 at 17:11):

CDS Hooks defines a Hook Maturity Model (similar to the FHIR maturity model), which indicates the amount of consensus and maturity of each individual hook -- resulting in a ballot and then normative status for each hook. The CDS WG is in the process of balloting the patient-view hook. Should additional hooks be considered for a greater maturity per the maturity model?
