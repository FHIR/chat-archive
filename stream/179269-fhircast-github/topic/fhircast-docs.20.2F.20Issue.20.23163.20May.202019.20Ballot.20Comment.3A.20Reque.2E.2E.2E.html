isaacvetter commented on Issue #163

Resolution: Persuasive with Mod

Clarify meaning of the user-logout event to indicate that the user has been logged out, either through a time-out or a logout or other reasons and in the case of a user-specific session, the hub SHALL also send a hub.mode=denied event.
