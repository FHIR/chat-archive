Github Notifications (FHIRcast) (May 07 2019 at 23:31):

NiklasSvenzen commented on Issue #226

During conversation at the Montreal, May 2019 working group meeting, @wdvr, @wmaethner, @NiklasSvenzen and myself talked through this issue and would like to propose:

Proposed resolution: Persusasive with mod
Remove "driving application" and reword.

Existing wording: The app MUST either be launched from the driving application following the SMART on FHIR EHR launch flow or the app may initiate the launch following the SMART on FHIR standalone launch. In either case, the app MUST request and be granted the fhircast OAuth2.0 scope.

Proposed wording: Following a SMART on FHIR EHR launch or SMART standalone launch, the app SHALL request and if authorized, SHALL be granted the fhircast OAuth 2.0 scope.
