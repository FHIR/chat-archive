Topic: IG for open source CovApp COVID-19 assessment app

Morten Ernebjerg (May 07 2020 at 06:12):

We have released a FHIR R4 IG with the questionnaire used in the open-source COVID-19 assessment web app CovApp. The questionnaire captures basic information like age and contact to known cases etc., as well as current symptom and basic risk factor information. The app was co-developed by the Charité University Hospital in Berlin and Data4Life and is in use in several countries, so I thought this might be interesting for the international community.

  • CovApp FHIR IG: https://simplifier.net/guide/covid-19pre-testpatientassessment/home
  • CovApp open-source code: https://github.com/d4l-data4life/covapp
  • One running instance of CovApp: https://covapp.data4life.care/

Thanks to @Julian Sass , @Patrick Werner , @Peter Osburg , @Sylvia Thun , @dr Kai U. Heitmann, and Valerie Kirchberger for their contributions that helped make this possible!
