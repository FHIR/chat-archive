Topic: SANER Project feedback from a provider

David Pyke (Apr 03 2020 at 17:29):

Through one of the projects I'm on we received some feed back on SANER that they would like us all to know:

  • This data is more prized than PHI
  • Integrity of the data is highly suspect – systems don’t differentiate virtual versus real/physical beds, create double the numbers because of cleaning, same issues with ventilator numbers
  • Also having an issue because they're converting beds in real time to ICU beds and COVID beds further throwing off the numbers
  • They are currently building enterprise dashboards for the info – only have 20 people with access at Sutter – limited it to command center leaders and physicians

  • If you share this data it would be better to share the actual occupancy numbers to ICU, bed, ventilator – licensed beds or manually reported number of beds, but calculating the denominator to get total beds available is unwise
    ** Should coordinate with the ADT teams from Epic, Cerner, Meditech
    ** Data that would be helpful to share
    *** Tracking of PPE – personal protective equipment number – not tracking usage right now but would be incredibly helpful to share so folks know who to contact when they have shortages
    *** Availability of testing supplies

  • Limit data sharing to state agency officials only – worried that other hospitals will send patients because they think there are beds when there aren’t

  • Also concerned about sharing with CDC since those numbers would go to the WH even though they aren't accurate
