Topic: HHS lab reporting updates

Andrea Pitkus, PhD, MLS(ASCP)CM, CSM (Jun 06 2020 at 14:28):

HHS has updated reporting requirements this week: https://www.hhs.gov/about/news/2020/06/04/hhs-announces-new-laboratory-data-reporting-guidance-for-covid-19-testing.html Shouldn't be too much of a change if folks were already reporting ELR, such as hospital labs for MU. For non hospital labs (those excluded from MU incentives and requirements), and testing/collection locations such as drive up/through testing locations, more might be needed such as upgrading to 2015 certified versions of information systems as recommended or using standards such as LOINC and SNOMED CT and HL7 v 2.51. It should help nudge labs to the same level of standards adoption so more results can be computer actionable.

Keith Boone (Aug 31 2020 at 15:56):

The FEMA measure is still available for testing in this connectathon (# of tests, tests cumulative, positive tests, and cumulative positive tests), so you could accept V2 data and produce measures. We have nothing in SANER that talks about how you would do the compute from V2, that's really out of scope at this stage.
