Topic: Joint CMG/SDWG Meetings

Lisa Nelson (Dec 03 2021 at 23:23):

There will be a joint CMG/Structured Documents meeting next Thursday 12/9 from 10am – 11am ET.

Call-in Details are posted in the HL7 Conference Call Center under the Structured Documents Work Group's calls.

The first 30 minutes will be devoted to a discussion with the Focus Team from the HL7 Reimaging Initiative.

Following that topic, we will be discussing a brief status update on the Web IG Publishing Project to hear about the progress with the C-CDA Web IG publishing pilot and to discuss what the name for this new format of the C-CDA R2.1 specification should be called.

CMG Members, please RSVP to Lisa or Linda if you can’t attend.
