Topic: HL7 Jira Specification Feedback for all CDA specifications/i

Linda (Feb 11 2021 at 20:24):

As of February 10th, 2021, HL7 Jira Specification Feedback has been implemented to support the capture of comments on all CDA documents. 

The link for entering comments is: https://jira.hl7.org/projects/CDA

For instructions on how to use the Jira system, please go here, Specification Feedback, https://confluence.hl7.org/display/HL7/Specification+Feedback
