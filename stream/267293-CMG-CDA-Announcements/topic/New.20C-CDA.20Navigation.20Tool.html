Lisa Nelson (Jan 26 2021 at 16:39):

New C-CDA Navigation Tool Offers Easier Access to C-CDA R2.1 Specification and Companion Guide

On January 25, 2021, CMG announced publication of a new tool for navigating the C-CDA R2.1 specification and companion guide on the web. "We think the CDA Community is going to be very pleased," CMG Co-Chair Linda Michaelsen said.

The new website for navigating C-CDA and its Companion Guide resulted from a creative group within the CDA Community brainstorming ideas for how to make C-CDA more usable and easier to access. "We appreciate the efforts of those to pitched in to move this work from a possibility to published pages in just a couple weeks," added CMG Co-Chair Lisa Nelson, "and we especially thank John D'Amore from Diameter Health who took a leadership role in making this happen."

Check out the new C-CDA Navigation Tool. You have to see it to believe it. hl7.me/viewccda.
