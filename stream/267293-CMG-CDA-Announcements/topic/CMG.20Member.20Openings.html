Lisa Nelson (Nov 16 2021 at 17:02):

CMG Opening for EHR Stakeholder and International Representation

The CDA Management Group is seeking an additional member from the Inpatient EHR Community to join our team. We also have an opening for a member representing an international perspective for the CDA community. If you are in a position to represent the interests and perspectives of either of these stakeholder groups and you would like to learn more about what it means to serve as a representative on the CDA Management Group, please reach out to @Lisa Nelson or @Linda Michaelsen.

Desired skill set for a member of CDA-MG:

  • Good verbal and written communication skills are necessary.
  • Experience with HL7 CDA standards is necessary, as is a record of active involvement with HL7 international committees and/or active participation on the listservs.
  • Interest or experience in standards management processes.

Responsibilities/Expectations:

  • CDA-MG members MUST be able to routinely attend 1 hour weekly teleconferences.
  • CDA-MG members MUST be able to attend at least two WGMs per year; preferably all three.
  • CDA-MG members must be active participants in HL7 CDA standards development.
