Saul Kravitz (Nov 04 2020 at 23:19):

See the communication field and communicationproficiency extension in https://build.fhir.org/ig/HL7/davinci-pdex-plan-net/StructureDefinition-plannet-Practitioner.html. They are MS 0..1. If you have 'em, you are supposed to provide 'em.

Saul Kravitz (Nov 05 2020 at 15:56):

The only place I see that addresses cultural competence is the accessibility extension to Plannet-Location, that has an accessibility code for 'cultural competence'.

Robert Dieterle (Nov 11 2020 at 11:41):

Quote from the CMS final rule
"The MA plan’s network of contracted providers, including names, addresses, phone numbers, and specialties, updated no later than 30 calendar days after the MA organizations receives provider directory information or updates to provider directory information; and
For an MA organization that offers an MA-PD plan, the MA-PD’s pharmacy directory, including the pharmacy name, address, phone number, number of pharmacies in the network, and mix (specifically the type of pharmacy, such as “retail pharmacy”) updated no later than 30 calendar days after the MA organization receives pharmacy directory information or updates to pharmacy directory information."

No mention of cultural competence -- the IG should pass CMS rules as is.

BTW: The use of cultural competency at the location level is appropriate because in many/most cases this may involve more than just the capability of a single provider.
