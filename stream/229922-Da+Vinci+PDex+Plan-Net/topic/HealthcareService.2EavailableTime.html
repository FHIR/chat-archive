Saul Kravitz (Apr 15 2021 at 19:51):

@Chetana Nayyar The HealthcareService describes a service. There are three codes that characterize the service: category, type (the one you mention above) and specialty. Irrespective of the service, the profile provides the fields to support a description of when the service itself is available, and, independently, when the contact point is available.

So, if the service is a pain clinic, one might expect that the category would be 'outpat', the type might be 'pain', and the specialty could be Pain Medicine (Physical Medicine & Rehabilitation) Physician. They might have a 24 hour contact phone number (contact-availabletime) but the clinic might be open only 2-4pm on Wednesdays.
