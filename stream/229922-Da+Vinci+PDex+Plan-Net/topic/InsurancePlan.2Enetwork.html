Saul Kravitz (Mar 02 2021 at 19:47):

@Chetana Nayyar -- good question.
There is an invariant that requires that an InsurancePlan define network(s) at either the product level, or at the plan level.
It is possible that both could be populated, in which case the (implicit, not explicit) interpretation would be the union of the two for each plan.
