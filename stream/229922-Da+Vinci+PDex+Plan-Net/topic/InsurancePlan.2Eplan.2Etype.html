Dave Hill (Aug 06 2021 at 16:44):

Hi Joseph, we discussed this issue on the PDex use case call and have the following question: Do the premium/select/standard refer to different cost-sharing options on the same benefits or do these tiers have different benefits? If the tiers have different benefits, they would be considered different products. The plan refers to the different cost-sharing options within the same product (same benefits). Thanks!
