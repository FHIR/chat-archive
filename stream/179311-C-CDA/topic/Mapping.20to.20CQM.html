Lisa Nelson (Aug 20 2019 at 01:45):

Hi Kurt. We recently created an example for recording that a depression screening assessment was done. We agreed to record assessments in a related section for the associated type of assessment, or the most general Assessments Section, or if no better place is available to use the Results Section. We also agreed to use the Assessment Scale Observation template for recording the assessment information. Recording the results implies the assessment was done. Here's the sample http://cdasearch.hl7.org/examples/view/302a998a-6d91-4f6e-8354-13c7777bdf4b Let me know if this helps with what you are looking for. Lisa
