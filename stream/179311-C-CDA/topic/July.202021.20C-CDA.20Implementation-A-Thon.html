Registration Now Open for C-CDA IAT
Wednesday, July 28, 2021 | 10AM – 6PM EDT

Save your spot now for the next C-CDA Implementation-A-Thon (IAT)!

The focus for July is “accelerating advances through collaboration and teamwork”.

Topics will include:
• a greater understanding about the International Patient Summary implementation guide,
• how to utilize the support processes within the CDA Community to address a challenge, and
• what is happening with a new initiative at HL7 to develop mapping guidance for C-CDA to and from FHIR.
Always a crowd favorite, the "Ask the ONC" session will focus on the released version of USCDI V2 and new guidance about the ongoing evolution of the USCDI process.

Expect a summer field-day feel with team-building exercises that focus on building skills, knowledge and an appetite for collaboration that will help the CDA Community improve faster.

Pricing is $99 for HL7 members and $199 for non-members.
