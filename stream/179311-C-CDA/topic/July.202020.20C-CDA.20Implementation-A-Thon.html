Lisa Nelson (Jul 21 2020 at 14:30):

Zoom Into the C-CDA Implementation-A-Thon July 29th, 2020

Register today! http://www.hl7.org/events/c-cda/implementation/2020/07/

If you or your customers use HL7 C-CDA documents to exchange data, you won’t want to miss the upcoming virtual C-CDA Implementation-A-Thon, Wednesday, July 29th from 10am – 6pm ET.

This virtual event promises to be packed with useful information you need to manage your product roadmap for C-CDA support. We’ll be covering some of today’s most urgent implementer needs. Topics will be presented by C-CDA implementers including: Allscripts, Cerner, the CommonWell/CareQuality communities, Diameter Health, Optum, and others.

Attend the July 29th C-CDA IAT and you will:
• Learn how to document of death, death reason and birth/pregnancy
• Get the latest on representing a patient’s health insurance information in the Payer section
• Hear about a new identifier extension for CDA R2.0 that aligns with identifiers used in FHIR
• Stay abreast of what the CommonWell/Carequality community is doing to advance sharing of C-CDA Encounter Summaries and Patient Summaries
• See how USCI Clinical Notes, annotated comments and unstructured documents are being used to expand information exchange via C-CDA
• Clarify how to USCDI information maps from C-CDA templates to FHIR profiles and the FHIR Provenance Resource
• Discover how illustrative use cases can be used to accelerate interoperability for C-CDA documents
Check out the full agenda: 2020.07.29 Virtual IAT Track Agenda
Register today! http://www.hl7.org/events/c-cda/implementation/2020/07/
VIRTUAL EVENT!! $99 for HL7 members ($199 for non-members)
