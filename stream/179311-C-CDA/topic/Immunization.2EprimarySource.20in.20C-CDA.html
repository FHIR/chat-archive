Lisa Nelson (Jul 31 2020 at 11:40):

@Tushar Nair Immunization.primarySource is an element in a FHIR Resource. It is not a CDA element.

Definition from FHIR.
Element Id Immunization.primarySource
Definition
An indication that the content of the record is based on information from the person who administered the vaccine. This reflects the context under which the data was originally recorded.

My advice: 1. check the guidance and constraints supplied for Immunization in the US Core Profile on this resource. If no mapping guidance is supplied, then you'll have to reason through this by looking at the definitions of the participations in CDA. My advice would be to take a look at Immunization/performer in CDA to see if that sheds light on thngs. Another approach would be to see if the low order cardinality remains 0 in US Core Immunization. If the field is not required and you don't have the right information to determine how it should be populated, then don't use that part of the FHIR structure.
