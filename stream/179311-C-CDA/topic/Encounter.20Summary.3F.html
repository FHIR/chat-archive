Joe Lamy (Oct 21 2020 at 15:45):

Dave, if you saw a reference to "Encounter Summary", from what I understand, that term was introduced by the CommonWell-Carequality Content Guide, to encompass the document types that can be used to capture an encounter. The guide currently emphasizes use of Discharge Summary for inpatient and Progress note for outpatient. And while it notes the use of CCD document type used historically for encounters (and called out by rules), it urges these more appropriate formats for encounters over the CCD.
