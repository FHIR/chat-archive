Natasha Kreisle (Jul 21 2021 at 14:34):

HL7 C-CDA Implementation-A-Thon
Wednesday, July 28, 2021 | 10AM – 6PM EDT

One week away! The focus for this event is “accelerating advances through collaboration and teamwork”.

Expect a summer field-day feel with team-building exercises that focus on building skills, knowledge and an appetite for collaboration that will help the CDA Community improve faster.

C-CDA Implementation-A-Thon (IAT) topics will include:

a greater understanding about the International Patient Summary implementation guide,
how to utilize the support processes within the CDA Community to address a challenge,
what is happening with a new initiative at HL7 to develop mapping guidance for C-CDA to and from FHIR, and
the popular "Ask the ONC" session with focus on the released version of USCDI V2 and new guidance about the ongoing evolution of the USCDI process.
Cost is $99 for HL7 members and $199 for non-members.
