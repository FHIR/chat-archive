Please help set priorities for HL7's FHIR Standard for Trial Use 3, with a target release by end of 2016!

For STU3 resources you care about, the FHIR Management Group invites you to indicate the FHIR Maturity Model (FMM) level you'd like to see them at when FHIR STU3 is published.

Due date: 5/5 @ 5:55p ET
Action item: http://bit.ly/stu3-fmm-survey

Why? The results of this survey will guide workgroups in allocating effort and priorities for the next release of FHIR.
