David Hay (Mar 10 2017 at 20:49):

Hi Guys,

Well, it's coming up to that time again - the next Working Group Meeting is approaching us, and with it a FHIR Connectathon! The event will be held in Madrid (Spain) over the weekend of May 6/7 (immediately preceding the WGM – here’s a link to WGM details: http://www.hl7.org/events/working_group_meeting/2017/05/ )

It will be based on the STU3 version of FHIR that is almost ready to roll out (And a big thanks to all that are helping with this - especially our QA reviewers!)

We'll be sending out more details as we get closer to the event, but right now we're focussed on finalizing the tracks that will be supported, which means that the track co-ordinators need to get the proposals in by Tuesday March 21 (Sorry that we're a bit slow in sending out the reminder - the STU-3 candidate has been a lot of work!).

I've set up the pages:

Wiki - http://wiki.hl7.org/index.php?title=FHIR
Connectathon page: http://wiki.hl7.org/index.php?title=FHIR_Connectathon_15
Track proposals: http://wiki.hl7.org/index.php?title=Category:201704_FHIR_Connectathon_Track_Proposals

Again, sorry for the short timeframe...

David Hay & Lloyd McKenzie
Co-chairs FMG
