Grahame Grieve (Feb 25 2022 at 05:43):

It's been a long journey for all of us since we last met and we're excited to invite you to our next online FHIR® meetup.

Date: March 3, 2022
Time: 12:00 PM - 2:30 PM PT

Large terminologies like SNOMED CT, RxNorm and LOINC are a foundational part of the FHIR applications but there’re still problems with distribution, versioning, updates and management of terminologies. During this meetup we’re going to overview the current state of terminology support in FHIR, discuss challenges that implementers come across when developing and using their solutions.

Lord of the Terms
Speaker: @Grahame Grieve , FHIR product director for HL7 International

Practical approach to FHIR Terminology Services
Speaker: @nicola (RIO/SS) , CTO for Health Samurai

FHIR Terminology Services with Ontoserver
Speaker: @Michael Lawley who leads the Health Informatics Group at the Australian e-Health Research Centre, part of CSIRO, Australia’s National Science Agency.

Platform: Zoom. We want to make it really interactive.

Registration: https://bit.ly/3LBJAK9

See you soon!
