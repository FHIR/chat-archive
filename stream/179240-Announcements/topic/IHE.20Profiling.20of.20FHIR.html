John Moehrke (Jun 03 2016 at 13:56):

The IHE IT Infrastructure Technical Committee has published the following Technical Framework Supplements for trial implementation as of June 2, 2016:

Appendix Z on HL7 FHIR
Mobile Access to Health Documents (MHD)
Patient Demographic Query for Mobile (PDQm)
Patient Identifier Cross-reference for Mobile (PIXm)
These profiles may be available for testing at subsequent IHE Connectathons. The documents are available for download at http://ihe.net/Technical_Frameworks. Comments on all documents are invited at any time and can be submitted at ITI Public Comments.
