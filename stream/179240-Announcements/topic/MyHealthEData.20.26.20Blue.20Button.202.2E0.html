John Silva (Mar 07 2018 at 10:49):

FYI: the "Blue Button" concept began in the VA back in 2010 for US Veterans: https://www.va.gov/bluebutton/ From the press release it sounds like this Blue Button 2.0 is expanding the reach beyond veterans to Medicare beneficiaries.

And here is the official page for "Blue Button 2.0": https://bluebutton.cms.gov/ which specifically says that it uses FHIR and OAuth 2.0.

Interesting info on "Blue Button 2.0" page; you can even sign up for a sandbox demo account to try it out. It also talks about the FHIR Model it uses and the coding and identifiers they have used/mapped.

"We have mapped over 1,300 fields from the CMS claims data warehouse into FHIR. These fields are surfaced across the Patient, Coverage and Explanation of Benefits FHIR resources."

Josh Mandel (Mar 08 2018 at 00:11):
