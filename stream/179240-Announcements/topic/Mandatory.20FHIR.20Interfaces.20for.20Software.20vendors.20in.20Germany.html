Topic: Mandatory FHIR Interfaces for Software vendors in Germany

Simone Heckmann (Jul 03 2018 at 14:23):

The German National Association of Statutory Health Insurance Physicians (KBV) makes FHIR Interfaces mandatory by 2020 to integrate prescription software apps/modules with primary systems: http://www.kbv.de/html/2018_35667.php (in german)
Link to specification: http://www.kbv.de/media/sp/KBV_ITA_VGEX_Verordnungssoftware_Schnittstellenfestlegung.pdf
Please join the German stream (https://chat.fhir.org/#narrow/stream/27-german-(d-a-ch)/topic/KBV.20setzt.20auf.20FHIR) for comments, questions and discussion.
