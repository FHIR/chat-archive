Topic: AMIA & HL7 FHIR App Competition

Patricia Guerra (Jul 20 2021 at 19:41):

If you are working on FHIR® and have an app to show, we would like to hear from you! Consider submitting by AUGUST 2 to the competition, which is a fun, fast-paced kaleidoscope of practical and innovative FHIR® apps. We are interested in health-related FHIR® apps that:
• are deployed in a real-world setting, and in use with clinicians, caregivers, patients or people;
• address a real-world need or problem in an innovative, efficient and effective way; and
• provide clinical value. If you have one or more evaluation studies, so much the better!
More information is here: https://www.amia.org/amia2021/fhir-app-competition
