Features I'd like to highlight include:

  • You can now update your e-mail address and other account settings from https://chat.fhir.org/#settings/your-account
  • Extensive new keyboard shortcuts including + to respond with and n to cycle through unread streams/topics
  • Support for push messages on android (should be working by Monday) and, later on, iOS
  • Performance enhancements that should make our user sidebar snappier by limiting to users with recent activity
