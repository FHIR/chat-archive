Topic: FHIR Training this Fall

Sadhana Alangar (Oct 08 2018 at 18:48):

HL7 has online FHIR training opportunities this fall for those new to the standard and those at an intermediate level, all led by FHIR experts.

The Fall FHIR Intermediate Series starts next week (Oct 16). The series includes 5 new classes: HAPI on FHIR, FHIR Profiling, FHIR Terminology, SMART on FHIR and IHE on FHIR. You can take 1 class or all 5 for a discounted subscription price. Register here: http://www.hl7.org/events/webinar/fhir/intermediate/

The FHIR Fundamentals Course is a 4-week introductory workshop starting Nov 1. This course is the first step on the path to getting FHIR certified. This is one of our most popular courses and is filling fast. Register here: http://www.hl7.org/events/fhir_fun.cfm

We do offer discounts for HL7 members, students and lower and middle income countries. Please reach out to me if you have any questions. Our next FHIR training opportunities won’t be until 2019!
