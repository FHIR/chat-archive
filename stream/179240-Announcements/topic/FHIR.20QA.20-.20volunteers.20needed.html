Topic: FHIR QA - volunteers needed

David Hay (Nov 08 2018 at 17:55):

As we're about to publish the next version of the ballot, we need volunteers to do 'Quality Assurance' on the contents of the specification. This QA is purely grammatical and spelling - it's not about content. What we do is to download the spec, convert the pages to a number of MSWord documents which volunteers then spell check. These are returned to the editors for updating the spec. Takes about half an hour (if we have enough people!)

If you're interested in helping out (and the warm fuzzy feeling that you get from doing that - regrettably we can't offer fame and fortune) then send me an email at david.hay25@gmail.com. The QA period begins 11th November and ends 25th November. Volunteers will be recognized in the chat though...

Your help is much appreciated!
