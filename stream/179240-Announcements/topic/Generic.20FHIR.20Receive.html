James Walker (Jan 15 2020 at 12:22):

NHS Digital have developed a Generic FHIR Receive capability. This is to share data on three workstreams: i) Transfer of Care, discharge summaries from Acute, A&Eand Mental Health Trusts for discharge summaries & outpatient clinic letters to GPs ii) GP Connect, to share encounter at Federated GP practice to patients registered GP practice iii) Digital Medicines, to share flu vaccine data with patients registered GP practice, soon to be followed by Emergency Medication issueGeneric FHIR Receive
