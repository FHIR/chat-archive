Topic: Survey on Gender Identity values

Robert McClure (Jun 23 2020 at 13:49):

As many of you know, Vocab is running a HL7 Gender Harmony project. The project page has lots of information here.
One aspect of our deliverables is to improve the standardization of sex and gender data collection and exchange in health care systems. As part of this work we ask for your input on our proposed set of values for when recording Gender Identity in clinical systems. Feel free to share this survey with others. We are very interested in international input.

The survey is here: https://www.surveymonkey.ca/r/D3CBXH9

Many thanks for any help you can provide.
