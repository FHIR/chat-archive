Lynn Laakso (Aug 12 2021 at 20:24):

Due to weather-related power (and HQ -based systems) outages on Thursday August 12 in the Ann Arbor HL7 HQ office, with initial restoration estimates through Friday the 13th, some users had difficulty signing up for consensus groups. Since many users frequently enroll on the last day, two more days have been added to the sign-up period and the ballot voting period postponed by the same period to accommodate all users wishing to enroll.

Consensus Group Sign-Up Open Date: Tuesday, July 12, 2021

Consensus Group Sign-Up Close Date: new Saturday, August 14, 2021

Important Note: Consensus group signup closes when ballot voting begins.

Consensus group enrollment will be available from a date at least four weeks preceding the ballot vote opening date and will continue until the opening of voting. While the exact dates are dependent upon individual ballot open and close dates, in general the consensus group signup period dates are as follows:

Ballot Open Date: Sunday, August 15, 2021

Ballot Close Date: Wednesday, September 15, 2021
