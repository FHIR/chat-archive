The Cancer Electronic Pathology Reporting recently updated it’s approved project scope statement (PSS) to reflect changes based on consensus group decisions. This PSS was approved using the PSS Workflow process in Jira and therefore the update is being announced via Zulip. If you have comments about the updated scope, please review the latest comment entered on the PSS-1688 (https://jira.hl7.org/browse/PSS-1688) for Cancer Electronic Pathology Reporting in JIRA here
Scroll to the last comment entered on 5/13/2021. Click on the link in the comment that takes you to the Cancer ePathology Reporting Confluence Page . At the top of the confluence page, you will see a link to “PSS-1688-Draft-05032021.docx”. Please open the document with the track changes and review these changes with your work group. Add a comment to the Jira issue indicating that you have comments. Please record your comments by June 4, 2021. @Zabrina Gonzaga
