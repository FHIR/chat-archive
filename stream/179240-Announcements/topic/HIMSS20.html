Grahame Grieve (Mar 03 2020 at 02:21):

Dear HL7 Community,

We have been closely monitoring the developments of the global outbreak of COVID-19. Due to the concerns and risks related to the current outbreak of COVID-19 (Coronavirus) and out of an abundance of caution, HL7 International will not be staffing or hosting presentations in our booth at HIMSS20.

While we are disappointed, this is the right decision for our organization.

We are working to make presentations that were scheduled for our booth theater available at a later date. If you would like to be notified of the schedule, please send an email to boothpresentations@HL7.org. In addition, HL7 literature developed for distribution at HIMSS20 will be available for download at HL7.org/HIMSS in the coming days.
