Topic: Forge license

Rien Wertheim (Oct 03 2019 at 09:33):

On January 1st, 2020, Firely will issue a new license for Forge, replacing the current license. Forge will remain free for non-commercial use. Commercial use of Forge will be linked to the Simplifier pricing plans, which also give access to our support team and advanced features on Simplifier.
We posted a blog to explain the why and how: https://blog.fire.ly/2019/10/03/securing-the-future-of-forge/. Please send me or @Ward Weistra a PM if you have questions.
