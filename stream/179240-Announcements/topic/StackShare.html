Abbie Watson (Jun 11 2020 at 18:48):

After probably 6 months or more of waiting, StackShare finally approved my feature request ticket to add FHIR as a stack technology. Teams can now document their tech stack components, including the use of FHIR.

Screen-Shot-2020-06-11-at-1.43.03-PM.png

Documentation of the overall tech stack is, in my opinion, a very helpful activity for interoperability, similar to publishing StructureDefinitions for any extensions that you might be using.

Anyhow, that required navigating some red-tape, and is now available to everybody, so probably worth an announcement. Enjoy!
