Lloyd McKenzie (Apr 14 2016 at 04:03):

Just a reminder that the FHIR North connectathon will be running in Hamilton, Ontario, Canada on Wed. April 27th. We still have some seats left if you're interested. We're running 3 of the tracks from the Montreal connectathon: Patient (entry level stream), Structured Data Capture (questionnaire stuff) and SMART on FHIR - CA (using SMART with Canadian profiles). If you want to do something else, there's an experimental track too. In addition to hacking, there will also be tutorials and networking opportunities. You can find more information here: http://www.fhirnorth.ca.

(And yes, we're happy to welcome our southern neighbors from the U.S. Or from anywhere for that matter :>)
