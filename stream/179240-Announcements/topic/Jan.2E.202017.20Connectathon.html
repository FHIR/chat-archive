Subject: Call for track proposals for the January 2017 FHIR Connectathon in San Antonio, Texas

The January 2017 FHIR Connectathon is fast coming upon us.

It will be held in San Antonio, TX on Saturday, Jan. 14 and in the morning of Sunday, Jan. 15.

This event is held in conjunction with the HL7 Working Group meeting that runs from Jan. 14 through Jan. 20.

This will be the 14th FHIR Connectathon and will allow for hands on exploration of this rapidly developing standard.

So far the following tracks are scheduled:

*Care Plan

*Patient track (recommended for beginners)

*Terminology

The FHIR Management Group is asking interested parties to submit track proposals for inclusion at the Connectathon.

Please send track proposals (or questions) to one of the individuals below:

Grahame Grieve: grahame@healthintersections.com.au

Brian Pech: brian.pech@kg.org

David Hay: david.hay25@gmail.com

Information about the Connectathon can be found at: http://wiki.hl7.org/index.php?title=FHIR_Connectathon_14.

Information about the Working Group Meeting can be found at: http://www.hl7.org/events/wgm012017/index_7.cfm.

On behalf of the HL7 FHIR Management Group, thank you for your attention.

Brian Pech
