Passing this on from our HL7 Education Director:

Greetings:

I joined HL7 in August of last year as the Director of Education. This year we have been offering several new online classes and trying out different pricing schemes. I thought you might be interested in a subscription pricing series that is being offered for FHIR online classes. Student discounts are also available. The subscription price is a nice discount for 5 online classes. These webinars are being recorded and you will have free access to the recordings for 6 months once you are registered. Access to the recordings is a helpful feature if you are concerned that you may not be able to attend any class. You can register for this series here:

http://www.hl7.org/events/webinar/fhir/summer-school/2018/07/

Please forward this information to anyone who you think may be interested.

The FHIR Proficiency Certification exam is very new – you may also have an interest in getting certified. There is a review course, a study guide, and a practice test available to help prepare and again a student discount is available on the test itself. The test does not require any hands-on demonstration of ability. The student is tested on core knowledge of the STU3 standard. You will find the information on the test and study guide here:

http://www.hl7.org/implement/certification.cfm?ref=nav

If you pass the certificate exam you can get a digital badge that you can list on your business signature, LinkedIn profile etc.

The FHIR Fundamental's program is also being offered. It spans a full month and blends hands-on learning with didactic material: http://www.hl7.org/events/fhir_fun.cfm?ref=nav

You may also be interested in recommending others for training. Please reach out to sadhana@HL7.org if you have any questions.

Thank you very much.
