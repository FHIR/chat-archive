Igor Sirkovich (Jul 06 2017 at 14:10):

I just wanted to let all Canadian FHIR implementers know that we have an active Canadian FHIR Community with very productive monthly calls. We also have 5 work streams (Solution Architecture, eReferral, Provider & Client Registry, Business Case Development, Tooling), who meet weekly or bi-weekly. Please register and subscribe to topics of interest at https://infocentral.infoway-inforoute.ca/en/forum/266-fhir-implementations. You can find information on all the upcoming meetings at https://infocentral.infoway-inforoute.ca/en/news-events/event-calendar.
