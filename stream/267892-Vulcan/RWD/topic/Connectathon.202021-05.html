The main May 2021 Connectathon Vulcan/RWD goal, as stated on https://confluence.hl7.org/display/FHIR/2021-05+Vulcan+-+Real+World+Data+%28RWD%29+Submission+to+FDA, is to focus on FHIR->SDTM conversion for submitting patient medication (e.g. to FDA for the CDISC concomitant medications reporting - CM domain).

Kees van Bochove (May 17 2021 at 17:26):

The reason I'm joining is actually to learn more about the issues that arise and try and infer lessons learned for the upcoming FHIR -> OMOP conversion work where we will be facing many of the same challenges of mapping data from the operational healthcare data context to in that case observational medical research.
