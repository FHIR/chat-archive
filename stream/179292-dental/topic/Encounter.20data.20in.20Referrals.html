Russell Ott (May 14 2020 at 21:09):

The information in the Encounter row is intended to provide context on the visit that led to the Referral.In most circumstances, the referring system will not track planned encounters at a granular level, particularly if they are occurring in a different organization.
DECISION: The visit resulting in the Referral should be documented in the componentOf/encompassingEncounter in CDA (and appropriate constraints should be included in the design). This encounter information would be referenced in the ServiceRequest resource in FHIR, and included in the bundle as an Encounter resource.
