To accelerate interoperability, eHealth Exchange will be offering up to three $100,000 innovation grants, plus waived annual fees to organizations trying to accelerate interoperability via innovative methods (e.g. via FHIR, exchange with first responders, etc). Grant applicants must submit an intent to apply by 10/1/2021.

The grant is highlighted in the press release posted at https://ehealthexchange.org/ehealth-exchange-announces-550000-innovation-grant-program/. Grant details are posted at https://ehealthexchange.org/innovation-grant-program.

Could you please share this opportunity with your community as appropriate?
