Bryn Rhodes (Jan 30 2021 at 17:17):

Hi @Tien Thai , that error is saying that your Measure Observation function takes an argument that isn't consistent with the type of elements in the Measure Population. So if your population is made up of "Encounter, Performed", then your Measure Observation function needs to take an argument of type "Encounter, Performed". Does that help?
