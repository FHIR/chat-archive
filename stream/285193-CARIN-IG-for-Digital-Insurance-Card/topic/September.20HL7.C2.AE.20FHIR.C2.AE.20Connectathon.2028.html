Topic: September HL7® FHIR® Connectathon 28

Mark Roberts (Sep 08 2021 at 22:00):

The 28th HL7 FHIR Connectathon will take place September 13-15, 2021. In advance of the Connectathon, the CARIN Digital Insurance Card track will hold an information session, which is scheduled for Thursday, September 9 at 3:00 pm ET. We will provide an overview of the track and will answer any questions that might be helpful as you prepare for the Connectathon. The track will be purely educational. There will not be any testing, however we will review the scope of the IG, review draft data elements, and review other materials and would welcome anyone who is interested to participate.

The zoom information for the information session is: https://leavittpartners.zoom.us/j/98523019710?pwd=VzROUDcxRG8veWpQUy82RWhlT05YQT09 Password: 529183
The track page can be found here: https://confluence.hl7.org/display/FHIR/2021-09+CARIN+IG+For+Digital+Insurance+Card+Track

We look forward to speaking with you all tomorrow.
