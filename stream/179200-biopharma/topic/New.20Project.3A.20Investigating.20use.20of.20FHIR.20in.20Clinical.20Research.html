Topic: New Project: Investigating use of FHIR in Clinical Research

Geoff Low (Feb 15 2017 at 23:28):

Hey there. The PhUSE Steering committee has approved the New FHIR project to look at use cases for adoption and utilisation of FHIR as part of the Clinical Trials process. I appreciate there may be some overlap with existing projects/initiatives within the FHIR community, however we want to work together so we are open to anyone who wishes to participate. There are two scheduled F2F (one in Washington, DC and another in London, UK). We have been promised at least one representative from the FDA. If you are interested in this, please ping me.
