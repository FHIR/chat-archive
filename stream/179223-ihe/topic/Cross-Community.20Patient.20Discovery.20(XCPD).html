Elliot Silver (Mar 05 2018 at 17:24):

No, not yet. We haven't looked at any of the cross-community profiles. It may be that the same set of problem don't exist with FHIR (or can be solved trivially). I think most people are still having trouble getting their minds around FHIR-enabled distributed systems within an organization, and haven't though cross-organization yet.
I imagine a multi-destination forwarding proxy for facade for FHIR could solve the problem more generally than just XCPD.
If you have a use case, please let us know. We take submissions for new work items in August/September, and could potentially take this on for next year. Alternatively, we might have an idea that allows you to move forward before then.
