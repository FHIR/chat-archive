For applications implementing MHD or other FHIR based Profiles, I think it would make sense to implement ATNA using the AuditEvent Resource. John Moehrke (Nov 27 2017 at 13:40):

Hi Simone. The ITI committee has had too many work items to work on, so this one continues to fall off. A strong reason for us to not find priority to documenting it is that it seems very obvious to us what a predominant FHIR based application would use to record an AuditEvent. Thus we figure that the audience that needs to hear about a FHIR based method of recording an AuditEvent using http POST is likely to already have figured it out without us having a dedicated profile/option/transaction for it. Right?

I would be compelled more if I had any belief that Apps wanted to log audit events.... They tend to not want to log audit events, leaving all that kind of Accountability responsibility to the server. I would welcome someone taking this on as a Change Proposal, however the likely historic authors are not available.
