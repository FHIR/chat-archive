Elliot Silver (Feb 07 2019 at 19:13):

There was an attempt to define an overly-simplistic extension in ImagingStudy for radiation dose, but that has been removed in R4. The DICOM Dose SOP classes have been carefully designed with the the assistance of medical physicists and should be the best source of this information. We also want to avoid duplication of information between DICOM and FHIR as duplication introduces all sorts of questions related to source of truth, and proliferation of locations/mechanisms to find this information. I would hesitate to try to design anything in this space without the appropriate experts involved.

That said, I would support a variation on REM that replaced the v2 communications with FHIR, but this has not been tackled yet. If you feel it is a priority, you should submit a proposal when the IHE RAD domain issues a call for work items in the June/July time frame.
