Topic: use of externalDocument and XDS metadata

Jens Villadsen (Nov 13 2020 at 16:10):

XDS / CDA question for my CDA/XDS comrades: are you familiar with any use and coupling of externalDocuments and XDS associations in any XDS setups that uses this binding actively to group related documents - meaning that externalDocuments are part of the actual document set as well?

Joe Lamy (Nov 13 2020 at 19:13):

Hi Jens. not that I've seen in the wild, at least at this point. However, the 2016 HL7 Data Provenance IG makes use of externalDocument to capture provenance links, e.g. when an entry in one CDA is derived from one in another. And interest in provenance is increasing - we've started to add some requirements to production exchanges in the US, so I expect to see some uptake in cases such as reconciliation.

As far as its intersection with XDS, the mechanism by which you could group documents related this way is the XDS attribute referenceIdList (not through XDS associations). I don't believe this is being used at the national level in the US, although I don't have visibility into regional exchanges.

Hope this helps.
