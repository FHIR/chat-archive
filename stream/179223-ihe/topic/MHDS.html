I have converted the current IHE-MHDS to an IG publication. The MHDS - Mobile Health Document Sharing --- is the IHE Document Sharing solution with full FHIR stack. MHDS defines a Document Registry and it uses many other IHE-Profiles. Including MHD and PDQm that are also in IG format, but also some others that are not yet in IG format.

  • The github repo, for reporting issues and providing pull-requests, https://github.com/IHE/ITI.MHDS
  • The IG publication for review http://build.fhir.org/ig/IHE/ITI.MHDS/branches/master/index.html
