Stream: ihe

Topic: IHE MHD moving away from DocumetManifest resource

John Moehrke (Dec 03 2020 at 14:09):

IHE will be moving the MHD profile into the Implementation Guide publisher, and also moving away from use of DocumentManifest. We will be moving the functionality that we get from DocumentManifest to use of List resource. For more details on why and how this is going to be done and how to help, see https://healthcaresecprivacy.blogspot.com/2020/12/xdsmhd-community-input-needed-mhd-move.html

David Pyke (Dec 03 2020 at 14:23):

So the List profiles will be part of the MHD FHIR IG or will they get pushed as Core profiles and DocumentManifest be deprecated?

John Moehrke (Dec 03 2020 at 14:24):

Hard to ever predict the future.. but I expect the next version of MHD will not include any FHIR profiles of the FHIR core DocumentManifest resource.
