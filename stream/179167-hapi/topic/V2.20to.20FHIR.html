Sahitya Reddy (Jun 21 2018 at 14:09):

@James Agnew , does HAPI have the capability to transform V2 to FHIR?

James Agnew (Jun 21 2018 at 14:16):

Hi @Sahitya Reddy : HAPI FHIR does not come with built-in mappings from HL7 v2.x to FHIR, but you could use the HAPI HL7v2 library in combination with HAPI FHIR to build these mappings yourself.

Smile CDR (the commercial version of HAPI FHIR) does include a number of adapters, including HL7 v2.x support: https://smilecdr.com/docs/current/hl7_v2x_support/protocol.html
