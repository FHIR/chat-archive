No worries, your English is far better than my German =). There is another type of artifact in the GitHub repo for that package. Everything Patrick and Jens describe work for the Profile MI-I Patient - I happened to click through to the source repository first and the logical model fit what I thought you were asking about.

Loading the profile into HAPI will let the server support it and even validate it (using code such as the great example from Jens).

If you want to access an element such as versichertenId_GKV in your code, it does not have a 'first class' way of accessing it, you need to look at the Patient.identifer values, search for one with the matching pattern, and go from there. That is the part of the project I am working on - to hopefully make that layer easier. It is unfortunately quite a bit of work to get that working though =).
