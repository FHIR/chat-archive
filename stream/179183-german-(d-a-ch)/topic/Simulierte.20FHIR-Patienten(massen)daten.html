Synthetic Patient Simulation
– Synthea is an open-source synthetic patient generator that
simulates the medical history of synthetic patients.
High Quality Health Records
– Our mission is to output high-quality synthetic, realistic but not real,
patient data and associated health records covering every aspect
of health.
Freely Available
– The resulting data is free from legal, cost, privacy, and security
restrictions for a variety of secondary uses in academia, research,
industry, and government where realistic data is sufficient
