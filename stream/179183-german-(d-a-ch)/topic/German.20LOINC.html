Grahame Grieve (Jun 19 2017 at 19:48):

all: I have added multi-language support for LOINC on tx.fhir.org. This includes german versions. Here's an example:

en: http://tx.fhir.org/r3/ValueSet/doc-typecodes/$expand
de: http://tx.fhir.org/r3/ValueSet/doc-typecodes/$expand?displayLanguage=de

Grahame Grieve (Jun 19 2017 at 19:57):

a couple of notes:
- there are multiple de versions. you can specify just de, or de-DE, de-AT etc
- the de versions are incomplete - as can be seen in that expansion above. The server will use de where available, and fall back to the tx default langauge (en for LOINC) when not available. Vocab has clarified that the expansion should be more explicit about this, so the server will change in due course

Grahame Grieve (Jun 19 2017 at 20:04):

  • if you ask for just de, de-DE is preferred, and then will fall back to de-AT etc
