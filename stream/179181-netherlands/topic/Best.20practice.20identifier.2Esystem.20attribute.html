Piotr Chabot Stadhouders (Nov 15 2021 at 15:20):

We want to store the patient id and "subtraject nummer" of our EPD into the identifier attribute
What is the value to fill into the identifier.system attribute?

Pieter Edelman (Nov 16 2021 at 20:17):

The system can be any URI that is unique and under control of the organization defining the identifier. A straightforward solution would be to make up an URL that uses the domain of your organization, e.g. "http://my.organization.com/identifier/subtrajectnummer".
