Topic: <<data,reference>> and <<context,reference>>

Yuri Bonanno (Jan 10 2019 at 13:01):

I found something in MedicationUse HCIM that is still unclear to me. Other HCIM's also have this bit will take this HCIM as an example.

Prescriber::HealthProfessional is indicated as <<context,reference>>,
while
ProductUsed::FarmaceuticalProduct is indicated as <<data,reference>>.

What is the difference between <<context,reference>> and <<data,reference>>?

Ardon Toonstra (Jan 11 2019 at 10:40):

<<Context,reference >> is used when a reference to a complete HCIM is made.
<<data,reference>> is used when a reference to a sub-HCIM or specific concept is made.
