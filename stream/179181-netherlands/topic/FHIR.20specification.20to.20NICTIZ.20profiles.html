Alexander Henket (Feb 10 2021 at 14:56):

I don't think I follow the question. Where and sometimes how you map zib related data into FHIR is contained in the mappings for the existing set of zib related profiles. You may find that set here: https://simplifier.net/NictizSTU3-Zib2017/~resources. Mappings are defined and visible on the mapping tab of any profile, like Patient.

How you get stuff out of FHIR back into your EHR depends highly on the technology stack. Relevant strreams could include #hapi and #conformance. Does that help?
