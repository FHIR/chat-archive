Alexander Henket (May 10 2017 at 08:06):

In the initial phase we had projects without a FHIR version marker. This turned out to be unpractical as it is not advisable to mix versions in a project. Secondly we got a lot of implementer feedback that the distinction between nl-core-* profiles and the ZIB based profiles did not make sense to them.

So in cooperation with HL7 Netherlands we have decided to rationalize all that, hopefully just in time for major implementation. It causes a one time need to update your bookmarks.

The changes are as follows:

Actions GitHub:

  • Merged MedMij and NL-BasicComponents DSTU2 into Nictiz-DSTU2 (including history)
  • Merged MedMij and NL-BasicComponents STU3 into Nictiz-STU3 (including history)

Actions Simplifier:

  • Removed project NL-BasicComponents
  • Renamed project Nictiz to NictizDSTU2, added NictizSTU3, renamed HL7FHIR-NL to HL7FHIR-NL-STU3
  • Reconfigure webhooks to GitHub
  • Updated introduction/information on both projects to outline how NL-BasicComponents fits in
