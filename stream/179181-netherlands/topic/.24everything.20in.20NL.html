John Moehrke (Mar 19 2021 at 12:10):

I was reacting to the statements of the definition of $everything. Everything to one person is less than everything to another person. Consent allows the patient to restrict some data for some purposes. And patients are not the only source of restrictive policies. Medical practice has some safety reasons to restrict data. etc.... Thus the definition of $everything is.... all the data that the requestor is authorized to get. This might bluntly be everything, it might bluntly be nothing, it might be anything between.

John Moehrke (Mar 19 2021 at 12:11):

so the "not defined in FHIR core" is referring to the sub-set policy is not defined. The technical definition of $everything is there. Just not the policy.
