Topic: HCIM AllergyIntolerance - duplicative allergyStatus items?

Cliff Wu (May 28 2020 at 16:49):

In the HCIM for AllergyIntolerance, the status of an allergy (active, completed, and obsolete) is modelled in 2 elements--AllergyIntolerance.clinicalStatus and AllergyIntolerance.clinicalStatus.extension:AllergieStatusCodelijst. Is there a reason that there is a specific extension for this? Is there additional value (now, or in the future) that a Dutch-specific extension will provide for conveying allergy status? Otherwise, this seems like an opportunity to streamline the profile.

René Spronk (May 29 2020 at 09:28):

The HCIM probably mandates the use of a different coding system than the coding system used by the FHIR resource. Both may contain the very same concepts, but they're two different coding systems. For conformance reasons one has to have the extension.

Alexander Henket (Jun 09 2020 at 16:28):
