Richard Esmond (Jun 14 2017 at 16:41):

I will be posting more detailed information over the course of the week, but a group of people have begun meeting and having conversations about the possibility of starting a new HL7 work-group specifically targeting the authoring and balloting clinical models and FHIR profiles for the entire longitudinal care processes associated with Cancer. The group would be collaborative across all medical specialties associated with Diagnoses and Treatment, including Imaging, Pathology, Oncology, Genomics and Surgery. We have only had two meetings so-far, so it's very early days. But we have close to thirty individuals and two dozen organizations. So there is quite a bit of interest.
