Anterior, Posterior, and Middle are depth values, and are distinct from clock direction. (they are components of BreastAbnormality).

Should this be an extension to BreastBodyLocation instead (since it is a location property)?

The things we need to add to clock direction are
* direct under nipple
* axiel
* axiel tail

It is my understanding that these are mutually exclusive from clock locations. i.e. there is no direct under nipple at 1:00.
