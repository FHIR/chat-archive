## May 2018 Ballot Comment 78

Submitted by @cmoesel from MITRE

Chapter: CDS Service Response
Section: Source
Type: A-S :bulb:
In Person Requested? No

Existing Wording:

The icon returned by this URL should be in PNG format, an image size of 100x100 pixels, and must not include any transparent regions.

Comment:
Specifying exact dimensions and forbidding transparency seems it will not support pleasant display of icons / logos that are not square. Please consider making 100x100 max dimension size (to at least support rectangular icons) or allowing transparency (to support non-box-shaped icons).

## :de: Köln May 2018 Working Group Vote

@isaacvetter moved the following disposition, seconded by @kpshek.

Disposition: Persuasive with Mod
Disposition Comment:
We will change should to SHOULD (intent is to relax the restrictions that icons are always 100x100 PNGs without transparent). https://github.com/cds-hooks/docs/issues/34
