Alex Goel (Oct 21 2021 at 19:35):

Hi, question about representing pregnancy in the IPS. IPS has an observation profile for pregnancy: https://hl7.org/fhir/uv/ips/StructureDefinition-observation-pregnancy-status-uv-ips.html

but FHIR Condition says that pregnancy may be represented as a condition https://www.hl7.org/fhir/condition.html

Wondering if writing a query to retrieve pregnancy we'd need to account for the possibility that either might represent a pregnancy?

Richard Townley-O'Neill (Oct 22 2021 at 04:35):

Yes.
Whether a system supplies a pregnancy status observation for every pregnancy supplied as a condition resource is up to the system.

Rob Hausam (Oct 22 2021 at 13:52):

I believe that I agree with Richard on this. But I wonder if we should consider the use of the Condition resource (as an option) for this in IPS. Thoughts? @Giorgio Cangioli, and others?
