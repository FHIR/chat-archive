Yes
Please consider that the TII guide has not been maintained since the end of the project, some issues identified and fixed during the IPS guide development have not been applied to the TII guide.

  1. In the IG Builder IG profile, DocumentReference.context has been profiled out. Is the reasoning for this choice captured somewhere? I looked through some of the deliverables (e.g. "D3.5 Extending the Patient Summary with Imaging Results") and found some mentions, but am not sure if I am at the "true source".

The scope of this profile was to allow the identification and possibly the retrieval of external documents, for that reason the Clinical context of document was not considered relevant for this scope. However if I had to design the profile today I'd just leave that element unconstrained```
