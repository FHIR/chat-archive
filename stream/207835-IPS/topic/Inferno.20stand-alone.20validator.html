Michael O'Keefe (Sep 10 2020 at 14:37):

Hey all; I uploaded the FHIR IPS IG from Github (as of this morning) to the Inferno stand-alone validator. Now you should be able to validate resources against the IPS IG by going to https://inferno.healthit.gov/validator/ , putting your resource in, then opening the "advanced options" tab and selecting hl7.fhir.uv.ips as the IG, and your profile as the profile

Michael O'Keefe (Sep 10 2020 at 14:37):
