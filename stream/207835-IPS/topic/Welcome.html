Welcome to the IPS stream.
This stream aims to facilitate the growth of a global IPS (International Patient Summary) community of practice by sharing, exploring, and developing ideas around the IPS.

Giorgio Cangioli (Sep 01 2019 at 15:56):

It is intended to be open to any discussion and idea about:
1. the IPS ecosystems, including the IPS CDA implementers, the CEN EN 17269 standard (the reference IPS dataset), or the future ISO IPS standard and IHE IPS profile;
2. the implementation of the IPS and associated services
3. the relationship with other related initiatives (e.g. argonaut; CEN IPS; ISO IPS; IHE,..).
