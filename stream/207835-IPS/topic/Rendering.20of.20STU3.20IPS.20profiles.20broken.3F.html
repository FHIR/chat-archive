My advice would rather be that you should port back the current profiles to STU3 as closely as possible, if STU3 is absolutely necessary to use. There have been major changes after that version (like relaxing many 0..0 constraints and use mustSupport instead).

Alexander Zautke (Oct 10 2019 at 20:51):

And looking at the change log, that issue with missing elements in the differential got fixed in a subsequent version after porting the content to R4

Giorgio Cangioli (Oct 11 2019 at 08:29):

If you need to use an STU3 version of the IPS, I suggest not to look at the 2018 may ballot no maintained as STU3 based IG, but to the Trillium II project IG. http://hl7.eu/fhir/ig/trillium2 .
It includes some additional information not present in the current IPS (e.g. an Encounters section), but it is the closest thing to the IPS that uses FHIR STU3.

Giorgio Cangioli (Oct 11 2019 at 08:32):
