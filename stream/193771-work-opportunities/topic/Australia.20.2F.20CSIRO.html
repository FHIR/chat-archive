Grahame Grieve (Sep 03 2019 at 02:33):

The Australian E-Health Research Centre (AEHRC - http://aehrc.com/), part of Australia's National Science Agency, CSIRO, is a world class centre undertaking research and development across health and biomedical informatics and health services. We are seeking an someone with Australian Citizenship or Permanent Residency to join the Health Data Engineering team and contribute significant technical expertise in the use of HL7’s FHIR®, Medicines and Clinical Terminology including both SNOMED CT and LOINC. The individual will be responsible for leading and managing the development and deployment of Clinical Terminology and FHIR technology across multiple projects in partnership with key stakeholders. The projects will involve information modelling, the development and deployment of cloud-based applications and services to support our research across the Health Informatics group, and the further development and support of our clinical terminology tools.

Note about location: We have a preference for BNE for the core role, but other roles may be available & not tied to BNE
