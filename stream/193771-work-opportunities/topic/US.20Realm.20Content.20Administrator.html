The US Realm Content Administrator is a funded, non-volunteer contract position that has primary responsibility for managing databases, tools and processes to improve visibility into and provide support for HL7 standards development projects in the US Realm to improve the timeliness and quality of critical project-relevant specifications and related content.

Requirements: Work maybe remote but is US centric, so will be in USA timezone, and ability to travel around USA is important
