Grahame Grieve (Apr 01 2021 at 18:28):

The Vulcan HL7 Accelerator brings together stakeholders across the translational and clinical research community in order to bridge existing gaps between clinical care and research, strategically connect research collaboratives, maximize collective resources, and deliver integrated tools and resources. The Program Manager will serve as a critical force bringing collaborators together to drive delivery of innovative solutions in the operation of Vulcan. This position is available for immediate staffing and will be reviewed and renewed on an annual basis. It will initially be a part-time, remote-based contract position with the possibility to expand to a full-time position.

For a full job description please eMail Vulcan@HL7.org
