Topic: Field for CMS 1500 place of service

Nick Radov (Feb 05 2021 at 18:41):

Is there a recommended field anywhere in HL7 V2 for the CMS 1500 24b place of service code? We primarily need to distinguish between office "11" codes and inpatient hospital "21" codes for calculating certain care quality measures. The PV1-2 (patient class) field sort of fits, however I am hesitant to use it because the recommended values in User-defined Table 0004 don't align with the CMS value set. I understand some organizations are also using PV1-10 (hospital service) but that doesn't seem right either. Has anyone ever written a US realm implementation guide covering this topic?

Craig Newman (Feb 08 2021 at 16:02):
