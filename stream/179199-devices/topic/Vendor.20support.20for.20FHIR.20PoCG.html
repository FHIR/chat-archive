Todd Cooper (Mar 29 2021 at 12:28):

Best way to get that information is to dial into one of the Devices on FHIR PoCD calls (every Monday at 0600 Pacific) and ask - the context of your question is (I think) the interface between 11073 SDC ("from the device interface") and FHIR (integration with everything beyond the SDC networked systems & devices) ... that will be directly addressed in the IHE-HL7 Gemini SDPi+FHIR profile specification (specifically a SOMDS FHIR Gateway) that will leverage this version of the PoCD DoF IG w/ SDC mapping ... specifically for implementation in products. That should be out for public review in the June timeframe.

All to say, I've heard that YES - vendors are starting to implement this in products ... or at least have support included in their "near term" plans (i.e., later in '21, definitely '22). BUT this all depends on market demand ... and currently, the vast majority of demand is directly addressed by the IHE Devices V2-based profiles. that's starting to change, but remains more in the "noise" vs. a clear need for product support.

And of course, that same SDPi profile has "HL7 V2 Gateway" & "IHE DEC Gateway" actors defined also. :-)

Hope that helps ...
