Todd Cooper (Jun 21 2016 at 18:59):

@Ioana Singureanu OK ... I give: What are the 8 Rights?!

Ioana Singureanu (Jun 22 2016 at 01:26):

right PATIENT
right MEDICATION to be given
right REASON (this may be a diagnosis/symptom) - the nurse has to verify that the patient should indeed receive the meds intravenously
right DOSE - what is the patient's weight
right ROUTE
right FREQUENCY
right TIME/ DAY of order
right SITE

We could ease the verification if we could use an Encounter resource to specify that a patient is associated with a pump. We could query for outstanding medication orders and pick the order that is appropriate. That may be something interesting for *new* functionality not currently supported by IHE PCD.
