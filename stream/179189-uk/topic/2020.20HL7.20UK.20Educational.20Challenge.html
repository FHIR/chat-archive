Ben McAlister (Nov 06 2020 at 16:15):

Just a note to raise awareness of approaching application deadline for 2020 HL7 UK Educational Challenge.

The 2020 HL7 UK Educational Challenge has now been relaunched in a COVID secure form. If you’re an undergraduate at a UK university and interested in clinical informatics, this is the chance to win a cash prize and a ticket to take part in the FHIR DevDays 2021.

The prize for the winning team will be £1000 per student plus free online attendance at DevDays 2021 run by Fire.ly. DevDays is an outstanding opportunity to develop, learn, network and explore opportunities with the world’s leading FHIR specialists. This is a marvellous chance for computer science and software engineering students who want to acquire new skills and demonstrate competitive success in an exciting field. Excellent for the CV!

The sponsors are InterSystems and the computational platform is kindly provided by AWS Educate.

  • Application deadline: 2300 GMT, 10 November 2020
  • Accepted teams notified: 13 November 2020
  • Teams complete preliminary work: 13 November – 9 December 2020
  • Hack day and awards ceremony (online): 10 December 2020

To apply or raise any questions, please complete this form.
