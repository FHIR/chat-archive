What if there are some optional extensions in profile(like extension "EthnicCategory" in "Patient" profile) and I don't want to include in my profile (local profile with my own use case) ? Thanks

Rik Smithies (Jun 21 2020 at 16:59):

Hi Kailash, it depends what you are doing in the UK and the context. Not all projects in the UK are related to CareConnect. If you have a business agreement or requirement to support CareConnect (or a similar scope), that is one thing, but you may have other requirements completely. If you need to convey EthnicCategory, which is a commonly collected data item in the NHS in England, with a well established value set, then it would certainly be good to use that value set and that standard extension.

Kevin Mayfield (Jun 21 2020 at 18:20):

As Rik says. If you want to use ethnic category I would try to use that CodeSystem. Some of the extensions are for national (England) services rather than hospitals or systems.
