Chris Grenz (Jul 20 2016 at 15:55):

Bundle.entry.resource has type Resource which has children of id, meta, uri, etc. By this definition, the bundle should look like this:

<Bundle>
...snip...
  <entry>
    <resource>
      <id value="12345" />
      <identifier>
       ...etc...

rather than

<Bundle>
...snip...
  <entry>
    <resource>
      <Patient>
        <id value="12345" />
        <identifier>
       ...etc...
