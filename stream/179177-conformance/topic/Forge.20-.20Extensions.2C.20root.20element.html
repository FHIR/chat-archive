Jonny Rylands (Mar 15 2016 at 10:23):

@Michel Rutten Noticed a change in Forge this morning, I suspect it is correct (and intentional)- the root element for an extension (path == "Extension") is no longer included in the differential, unless the properties of it have changed.

Michel Rutten (Mar 15 2016 at 10:35):

@Jonny Rylands indeed, although this is not an intentional change. Thank you for pointing this out. I'll discuss this with Ewout to verify if the current behavior is valid.

Jonny Rylands (Mar 15 2016 at 10:36):

Thanks.

Michel Rutten (Mar 15 2016 at 10:49):

@Jonny Rylands Ewout confirmed that in DSTU 2, the differential component must ALWAYS contain a root element definition. In DSTU 3 this is no longer required. I will update Forge to ensure that the root element definition is always serialized to the output Xml, regardless of any changes.
