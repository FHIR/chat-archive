Patrick Werner (May 17 2018 at 16:07):

Patrick Werner (May 17 2018 at 16:14):

Lloyd McKenzie (May 17 2018 at 19:02):

Unless someone can provide a usecase for allowing strings to start or end with whitespace, I have no issue with enforcing its removal. It makes processing simpler for everyone downstream (where either removal will have to happen or things will render incorrectly)
