@Michel Rutten Think I might have found a bug in Forge re complex extensions. If you edit a complex extension with a valueset binding and save, Forge loses the valueset binding. It also loses max cardinality values.

Jonny Rylands (Sep 16 2016 at 11:08):

Seem to have found a weird workaround for the valueset binding - if you add a valueset URL then save, the valueset doesn't appear in the XML. Then add one character to the URL and re-save. Then remove the character and re-save. The final XML will have the valueset URL in it!
