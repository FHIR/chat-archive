Topic: GraphDefinition and contained vs bundled

Eric Haas (Jan 02 2020 at 19:59):

Does GraphDefinition need to replicate the SD aggregation list of contained vs bundled? How would an edge be represented to allow one vs the other or both?

René Spronk (Jan 03 2020 at 10:57):

Given that a contained resource can only reference another contained resource, and not to a non-contained resource, it's probably sufficient to have a mechanism in StrucDef. Unless one would like to be able to make a statement like "this node shall have an inbound reference from a contained resource". In my proposed new GraphDef I haven't distinguished between contained and non-contained resources, from a pure Graph perspective that difference is irrelevant.
