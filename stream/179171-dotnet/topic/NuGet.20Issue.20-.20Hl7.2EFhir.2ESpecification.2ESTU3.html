Richard Kavanagh (Nov 12 2018 at 17:22):

I've just been uodating the NuGet packages on some of my projects. It appears that after 0.93.4 the 'specification.zip' file no longer gets copied into the root path and is not present when the app is "built".

Anyone know what changed?

Yunwei Wang (Nov 12 2018 at 18:32):

I asked this question before. Here is the reply form @Michel Rutten

The specification.zip file is included in the Hl7.Fhir.STU3.Specification library NuGet package. However as Ewout mentions, NuGet now installs this dependency in the global NuGet cache. For example:
%UserProfile%\.nuget\packages\hl7.fhir.specification.stu3\0.94.0-beta2\contentFiles\any\any
