Tone Southerland (Feb 16 2022 at 15:20):

This highly depends on the clinical workflow and implementation, and I don't believe it to be a simple problem to solve as a one size fits all solution. We did some work in IHE PCC several years ago around this creating a base concern entry, and derived entries for problem concern and allergy concern that may provide some addition understanding. These entries were then used in Profiles to provide further usage/use case constraints as appropriate. Check out section 6.3.4.11 in the PCC TF Vol 2: https://www.ihe.net/uploadedFiles/Documents/PCC/IHE_PCC_TF_Vol2.pdf
