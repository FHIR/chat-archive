Topic: 20211025-01 Context Conduction inside entries

Lisa Nelson (Nov 01 2021 at 00:41):

Does ContextConduction apply inside a complex entry that has entryRelationships to nested acts?

Based on the entryRelationship participation having a contextConduction element, we believe context conduction does apply within a complex entry.

We are answering the question of what to do with the allergy Concern act based on how we understand ContextConduction to be working.

Decision 10/25/2021: The ConcernAct may offer (author, informant, subject) information that will conduct into lower levels of the clinical statement.
