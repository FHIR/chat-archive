Benjamin Flessner (Jan 12 2019 at 15:17):

For the Connectathon, we have an open endpoint to which you can just post CDA XML documents and receive a FHIR bundle back. I'd gladly welcome questions / comments / feedback on our mapping decisions at any time this weekend. I'll be splitting my time between the Documents track (table 26) and the C-CDA Implementation-a-thon (in Pecos).

Post your CDA XML to:
http(s)://redox.ngrok.io/fhir
with a Content-Type header of text/xml (no other headers necessary)
You'll receive a FHIR Document Bundle back in JSON format.
