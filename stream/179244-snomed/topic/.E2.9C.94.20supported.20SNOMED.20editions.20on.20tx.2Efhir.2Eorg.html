Michael Lawley (Nov 06 2021 at 23:27):

The spec here https://confluence.ihtsdotools.org/x/vgTJAQ is quite clear - there are Edition-relative and Version-relative forms of the SNOMED component http://snomed.info/id/... URIs. It's up to SNOMED International to deal with resolving them (or not).

Jens Villadsen (Nov 07 2021 at 00:58):

Michael Lawley (Nov 07 2021 at 02:01):

There is no requirement for them to resolve at all, or for them to resolve to precise or more general -- they are Identifiers not Locations.
https://confluence.ihtsdotools.org/display/DOCURI/3.1+Resolving+SNOMED+CT+URIs

However, given that the shorter unversioned forms resolve to the browser, it would seem like a useful thing for such resolution to be more consistently supported.
