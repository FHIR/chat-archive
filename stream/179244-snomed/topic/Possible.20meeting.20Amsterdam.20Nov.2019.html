I've just been speaking to Jane Millar about Snomed's analysis of the FHIR value sets for

  • Condition.clinicalStatus
  • AllergyIntolerance.clinicalStatus
  • AdverseEvent.outcome

(see https://confluence.ihtsdotools.org/display/FHIR/Free+SNOMED+CT+set+for+FHIR and sub-pages)

The Snomed analysis of these is troubling - they don't have lots of matching concepts, and they asked some hard questions of the HL7 design. It seems to both Jane and I that these elements should have strong mappings between FHIR and SNOMED CT, and that to progress this we need a solid focused discussion between HL7 (PC/Vocab) and SNOMED.

We cast around for options to do a face to face to this, and are proposing to our respective communities that there's a window of opportunity for us to meet in Amsterdam on Nov 19 (day before DevDays Amsterdam) to discuss this - there's a realistic possibility that enough of us from both communities could be present to have a meaningful discussion.

Any thoughts about this?
