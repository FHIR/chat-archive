Rob Hausam (Nov 14 2019 at 14:19):

There has been some work on mapping the US ICD-10-PCS procedure coding system to SNOMED CT, but I don't know how complete it is (I suspect not very). Kin Wah Fung at the NLM has been involved in this and the other ICD-10 to SNOMED CT mapping projects and has made several presentations and written papers on this, and I expect he may be in the best position to know the current state of it at the moment. His email is: kfung@mail.nih.gov
