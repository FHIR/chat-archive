An alternative would be the version specific URI: http://snomed.info/sct/731000124108/version/20180301/id/428071000124103 as defined here https://confluence.ihtsdotools.org/display/DOCURI/2.3+Version-Relative+Component+URIs
It looks like there are two issues with this approach:
