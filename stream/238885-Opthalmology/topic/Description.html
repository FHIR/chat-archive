Ashley Kras (Aug 13 2020 at 21:33):

This channel is for discussing topics related to ophthalmology and FHIR. Currently, there is a working group within Patient Care (guided by @Stephen Chu ) that meets weekly at 0800 AEST (1800 US ET). The confluence page link is here - https://confluence.hl7.org/display/PC/Ophthalmology+FHIR+Project. The goal is to publish IG/s based on defined use cases and drive adoption of FHIR/interoperability within the ophthalmic community. We address technical, clinical and political matters and interface with other groups (eg device / EHRs as required). All welcome to join mettings / post questions / submit ideas
