Topic: Forge 19.7 for STU3 - FHIR DevDays 2018 Amsterdam Edition

Michel Rutten (Nov 13 2018 at 10:46):

Forge 19.7 for STU3 is now available for download from https://simplifier.net/forge.

This is a major update that introduces a couple of new features, to celebrate FHIR DevDays 2018 Amsterdam Edition.

  • NEW: Package Manager
  • NEW: Support for JSON

Both new features are still experimental and will be improved in future Forge releases. Feel free to submit feedback!

Full change log: https://simplifier.net/organization/firely/news/60

If you want to submit a comment or report an issue, please contact us at forge@fire.ly.

See you at FHIR DevDays in Amsterdam!
