Grahame Grieve (Oct 21 2021 at 06:15):

http://tx.fhir.org is the default terminology service for FHIR tools - specially the validator and the IG publisher. It's never been our intent that tx.fhir.org be the only terminology service in town, and there's a set of other services that would like to make their content available within the FHIR eco-system - that is, to the FHIR tooling.

Tooling in this case includes:

  • the FHIR validator and IG publisher
  • Sushi
  • Simplifier
  • any other design time tooling

I've long had an action item to do something about this. So today, I've started on that. Here's a draft of a page that describes the process of making a terminology service available to the FHIR community through the tools: https://confluence.hl7.org/display/FHIR/Publishing+terminology+to+the+FHIR+Ecosystem

I know that at least the HL7 Australia would like to make the Australian NCTS available this way, and I will be making VSAC available this way, and probably art-decor.

Comments are welcome and sought from toolsmiths, terminology service provides, and other interested business parties. Note: obviously this process is extremely draft; I expect it to firm up as we discuss. I will direct all discussion to here.
