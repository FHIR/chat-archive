Topic: Forge on MacOS/Wine

Michel Rutten (Jun 18 2019 at 15:20):

The latest Forge releases for STU3 and R4 require .NET Framework version 4.7.2 (originally 4.6). This updated dependency may complicate or prevent the installation of Forge on MacOS/Linux & Wine. We are aware of and looking into the reported installation issues.

Fortunately, the latest (development) version of Wine seems to solve the newly introduced issues. Our team is currently testing an improved installation procedure for the Wine (MacOS/Linux) platform. Once verified, we will update and improve our online installation documentation accordingly.

Stay tuned!
