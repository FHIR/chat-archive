Jason Walonoski (Nov 30 2020 at 14:52):

It has been a long time since I've tried this, so this information might be out of date. If the FHIR server has the proper SMART-on-FHIR extensions in their capability statement, then they will be detected by Crucible. In which case, a padlock icon will appear in the screen when you go to execute some tests. At that time, you can click the padlock and enter some credentials. Things may have changed.
