Pat Taylor (Apr 09 2021 at 22:32):

@Caitlin Ryan The Benefit Payment Status indicates if a service was paid in or out of network. Institutional and Pharmacy are paid at the claim level. For Professional and Non Clinician, individual lines may be paid as in and out of network. For example, an orthopedist office visit may be paid as in network while crutches may be paid as out of network.
