Topic: Which version of CARIN IG to use for 2021

Art Huston (Oct 15 2020 at 17:48):

CARIN BB and other IGs are continuing to evolve. For example, http://hl7.org/fhir/us/davinci-pdex/StructureDefinition/hrex-coverage
was recently added, and it's unclear how this will co-exist with the CARIN Coverage http://build.fhir.org/ig/HL7/carin-bb/StructureDefinition-C4BB-Coverage.html.

With issues like this in mind, what is the recommended version to meet the CMS Interoperability rules and meet market expectations for 2021? It's obviously best to offer full support, but if means the target is constantly moving then implementation becomes difficult or impossible.
