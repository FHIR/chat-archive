Are there any specifications about Patient Consent while sharing the Patient data using the API
How do we take care of Consent with respect to Patient data access.

Ryan Howells (Nov 18 2020 at 19:37):

An individual who requests their access using SMART and the OAuth flow is making a specific decision to send their data to an application of their choice. As such, during the OAuth flow they can specifically indicate they would like their data to be sent to the app which meets the requirements associated with an individual's right of access under HIPAA.
