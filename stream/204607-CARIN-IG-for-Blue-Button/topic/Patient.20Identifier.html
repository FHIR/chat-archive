Pat Taylor (Sep 30 2020 at 11:53):

@Jason Teeple Member Number is the number payers assign for a contract; it may be different for various lines of business; ie. PPO vs HMO. Unique Member Number is a mastered identifier across all lines of business we're updating the value set. @Amol Vyas

Bryan Briegel (Feb 19 2021 at 23:18):

@Pat Taylor looking at the cardinality of Unique Member Identifier, its 0..* I understand optional, though wondering when there would be more than one unique member ID for an enrollee, if I am tracking the definition properly. Thanks -

Pat Taylor (Feb 21 2021 at 13:39):

@Bryan Briegel ideally, there is only one unique member ID for an enrollee. However, there may be situations which the unique member ID was assigned in error, meaning an ID identified two people and needed to be split or vice versa; i.e., two IDs are really one person. the * accommodates those situations. @Amol Vyas @Corey Spears @Mark Roberts
