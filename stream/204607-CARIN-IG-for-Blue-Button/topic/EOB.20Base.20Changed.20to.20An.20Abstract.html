Josh Lamb (Aug 28 2020 at 19:47):

@Courtney Bland The EOB base profile is only meant to act as the base for other profiles and should not be directly instantiated. The purpose of the base profile to is hold the fields that are common across all of the CARIN EOB profiles. The intent of the profile has always been to serve as a base profile, rather than be directly instantiated (as per my understanding).

Josh Lamb (Sep 01 2020 at 11:31):

As of the last time I asked, about a week ago, the intent is to incorporate the dental and vision profiles as part of the STU1 version of the IG. The regulatory deadline is 1/1 but enforcement will not begin until 6/1. If you needed to produce a Dental or Vison EOB outside of what is defined within the CARIN IG then you would just omit the CARIN profile on EOB.Meta.Profile.
