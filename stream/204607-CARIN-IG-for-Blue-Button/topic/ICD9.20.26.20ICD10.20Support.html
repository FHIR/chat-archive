Ryan Howells (Aug 06 2020 at 18:34):

@Jason Teeple We did not account for ICD9 codes since the CMS rule only requires claims to be sent with a DOS 1/1/2016 or later. When we spoke with CMS early on, we learned one of the reasons they chose that date was because they wanted a clean break between ICD9 and ICD10. I don't think anyone (including CMS) contemplated this use case. What percentage of your total claims would fall under this scenario?
