Melanie Davies (Aug 25 2020 at 16:25):

Hello, I'm looking at the IG for the ExplanationOfBenefit resource and there is a procedure BackboneElement and also item.productOrService. The former specifies ICD-10, the latter CPT and HCPCS. Other than the difference in coding systems, I'm having trouble differentiating these in a meaningful way. How are each of these fields intended to be used/what do they represent?

Michele Mottini (Aug 25 2020 at 16:49):

procedure is the what has been done clinically - eg hip replacement surgery - item is the list of what has been claimed for it - eg the details of all consumables and services used in the surgery
