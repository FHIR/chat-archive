Josh Lamb (Sep 02 2020 at 00:19):

The CARIN IG only covers adjudicated claims, which translate to the statuses you indicated (active and cancelled). A payor may choose to include EOBs with other statuses but they would fall outside of scope of the IG. @Pat Taylor for confirmation.

Pat Taylor (Sep 08 2020 at 16:00):

@josh lamb yes, Josh is correct. The IG does not prevent inclusion of EOBs with other statuses but does require provisioning of adjudicated claims.
