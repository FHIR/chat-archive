Corey Spears (Jun 03 2021 at 00:58):

The STU1 Update will be coming out shortly. We have gone through the Peer Review process and made a publishing request. This will be version 1.1.0, which is currently available on the CI build (https://build.fhir.org/ig/HL7/carin-bb/).
STU2, which covers Vision, Oral, and Jira tracker items, is in development now and we plan on balloting during the next ballot cycle in September. Depending on the number and nature of the comments received, it may take 3+ months to publish from there.
