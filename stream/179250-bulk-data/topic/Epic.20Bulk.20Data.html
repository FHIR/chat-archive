Cooper Thompson (Feb 04 2022 at 19:42):

The issue is less about actual group creation, and more that our Bulk Export requires an admin grant a client access to a specific group. We don't currently have a process to grant group access to arbitrary clients on the fhir.epic.com sandbox. It is something we have on our backlog of sandbox features, but no promises on if/when we'll get to it.

I don't really want to sound like I'm marketing App Orchard, but since you get a dedicated Epic admin in AO, they can set up group access for you in the AO sandbox. That does require AO membership though. The other option is to work with the specific health systems you'll be connecting to (since you'll need to do that anyway), and test in their TST systems rather than the fhir.epic.com sandbox (which also has the advantage of testing with representative data from the actual health system).
