Peter Bernhardt (May 03 2021 at 15:17):

Josh Mandel (May 03 2021 at 15:21):

There's no formal standards work happening around an $import operation, but we've had consistent community interest around a cluster of related use cases. @Dan Gottlieb has been working on some proposal's for import that better leverage existing export capabilities.

Dan Gottlieb (May 03 2021 at 15:28):

Yup, good timing on the question - I'm hoping to have a draft of a new proposal posted in the next week or two to start to collect early feedback. As Josh pointed out though, the standards work recently has been around getting v2 of bulk export finished.
