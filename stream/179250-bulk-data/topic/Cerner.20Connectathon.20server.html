Bradley Strecker (Sep 10 2020 at 15:04):

Cerner's secure Connectathon bulk data export endpoint is currently available at https://fhir-ehr.stagingcerner.com/beta/0b8a0111-e8e6-4c26-a91c-5069cbc6b1ca (this is likely to change at some point during the day)

The https://bulk-data.smarthealthit.org/keys/RS384.public.json JWK is preconfigured and the client id "smarthealthit_rs384" can be used when creating the client_assertion token when working through the backend authorization workflow. Alternate JWKs and client ids can be imported if you'd like to bring your own.

Our available bulk data content is pre-generated so filtering options are limited. We have content for Condition, Observation, Patient, Procedure, and DocumentReference
