Lisa Nelson (May 13 2020 at 21:44):

@Karie Nicholas

Unfortunately, if the screening tool has not yet been coded by LOINC, you will need to craft your own Questionnaire Resource by hand. You can retrieve a Questionnaire Resource created by the LHC forms tool (or some other Form Builder tool) and then use that one as a pattern. Use the temporary codes established by SDOH-CC so others working on this IG will be able to make sense of the coded questionnaire.
