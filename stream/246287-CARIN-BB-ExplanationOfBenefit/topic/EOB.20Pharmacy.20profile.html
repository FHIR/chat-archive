Corey Spears (Apr 12 2021 at 16:03):

The IG shows 0 codes in the valueSet because HL7 has not provided an expansion of the codes included within. Usually this is the case where HL7 does not have the rights to reproduce the codes and descriptions in the IG. An implementer will have to acquire the appropriate license for a copy of the relevant specifications. The IG includes copyright information and links for each terminology.
