Good time of the day. I was well redirected to the correct channel. Maybe someone may help me on 'Coverage' actors, and correct definition of who's who.
As far as my understanding go:

  • policyHolder - is the party through which the policy is provided? In my case that`s an employer
  • subscriber - patient\person who is 'paying' for the insurance uphold
  • payor - the party that will fulfill the obligation to pay insurance costs in case it will happen
  • beneficiary - whoever is insured under the Coverage, and in case insurance event comes - will be paid according to contractual obligations

Paul Knapp (Oct 28 2021 at 19:43):

@Lopatin Dmytro Correct on all counts. Commonly: Policyholder=employer; subscriber=employee; payor=insurance company; beneficiary=the insured. For simple coverage such as when each beneficiary has their own member ID and the coverage is state-wide or national you may only have the payor and beneficiary. Member ID goes in the Coverage.identifier.
