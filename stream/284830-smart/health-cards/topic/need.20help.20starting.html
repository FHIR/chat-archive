Reece Adamson (Nov 24 2021 at 14:38):

For DIY I'd recommend reading through the specs if you haven't already:

  • SMART Health Cards Framework
    • Framework for enabling individuals with verifiable health data
  • SMART Health Cards: Vaccination & Testing IG
    • Applies SMART Health Cards to vaccinations and testing, including for COVID-19

The SMART spec also has links to developer tools to help you get started including example code, libraries for various languages, Jupyter notebook and demo portal walkthroughs, and validation tools.
