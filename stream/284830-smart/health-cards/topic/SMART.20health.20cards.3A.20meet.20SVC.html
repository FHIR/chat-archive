Josh Mandel (May 19 2021 at 23:11):

One of the core goals that differentiates SMART Health Cards today is representing any clinical data (bound to identity) in a standardized way, not limited to COVID use cases or to vaccines / lab results. It's the "use FHIR for what FHIR does well" approach (but to be fair, there are trade-offs here; our QRs are larger than they would be if we relaxed this constraint).

Josh Mandel (May 19 2021 at 23:13):

My hope is that this work is part of a broader story about consumer access and empowerment, building on the success we've seen with SMART on FHIR for access.
