Our every-two-weeks conference call focused on SHR Vaccination & Testing FHIR IG content is tomorrow, June 2 at 12pm EDT. More info here.

Agenda: We will provide an update on our int'l terminology support, and then have a working session on our experimental profiles. These include the vaccination-related adverse event profile that's been in the IG for a while, as well as the new profile for represting a past COVID-19 infection that @Neelima Karipineni has been prototyping.

Some other programming notes:

Our primary channel will still be this Zulip stream, but we will cross-post important messages there and will encourage implementers who are not already on Zulip to join the listserv instead.
  • Our next every-other-week meeting on June 16th will include discussion of the lab results profiles.
