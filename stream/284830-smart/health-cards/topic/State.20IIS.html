Nathan Bunker (May 12 2021 at 22:49):

I'm not aware of any that have implemented VCI, but some do have consumer portals that allow access to download a PDF. The states that have these have had them for years. States will look to implement things such as VCI, but they are waiting for (1) national strategy and (2) time/money to implement. States are doing all they can to keep their current HL7 v2 infrastructures operational with traffic that is 5-10 times higher than normal. But now that the initial rush of data flows is starting to ebb, a little, the states are starting to look at what is happening and see how they can be involved.

Nathan Bunker (May 12 2021 at 22:56):

The LA state implementation is very interesting. They build on a state digital drivers license to allow residents to display/share a green check mark. Different than VCI, this standard does not transfer vaccine information, just an indication if enough doses were given of the right product for the patient to be considered immune.
