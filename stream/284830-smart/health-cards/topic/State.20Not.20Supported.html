Nay Won Lett (Nov 11 2021 at 21:27):

Hi. I would like to obtain a Smart Health Card but my state (Massachusetts) is not on the list of states that provide the Smart Health Card. How can I do this? What are the first steps?

Josh Mandel (Nov 11 2021 at 21:44):

MA doesn't yet have state-wide support for SMART Health Cards, but you may be able to get a SMART Health Card from a provider in MA. See https://vci.org/issuers for a list of issuers, to see if there's one where you may have access.

Josh Mandel (Nov 11 2021 at 21:45):

For example, if you have access to a MyChart account from any of the following MA-based health Epic systems, that might be a good source:

  • Atrius Health
  • Boston Medical Center
  • Harbor Health
  • Mass General Brigham
  • NYU Langone
  • OCHIN
  • Pediatric Physicians' Organization at Children's
  • QuadMed
  • South Boston Community Health Center
  • South Shore Hospital
  • UMass Memorial Health Care

Paul Denning (Nov 12 2021 at 14:46):

My PCP at MGH (Mass General Brigham) pulled in the immunization info from the MA IIS, so I can get an SHC from the MGH Patient Gateway (Epic MyChart).
