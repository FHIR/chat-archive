Topic: Holder VCs in iCloud/Google Cloud?

Ashier De Leon (Jun 03 2021 at 06:57):

For the wallet holders, the credentials are usually stored on their phones. Is there any discussion or guidance on whether the VCs are allowed to be stored on the user's iCloud (iOS) or Google Cloud (Android)?

Josh Mandel (Jun 03 2021 at 11:12):

No guidance at the level of the spec. For many use cases there's a strong benefit of local control and storage, and in that context you'd think about cloud storage only for managing encrypted backups / synchronization across devices.
