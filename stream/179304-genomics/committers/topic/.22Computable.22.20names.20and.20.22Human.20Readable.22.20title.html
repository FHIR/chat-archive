Kevin Power (Sep 24 2019 at 16:43):

Hey @Lloyd McKenzie - You mentioned we can do title and a computable name in different places. Can you provide some specific guidance for me?

Lloyd McKenzie (Sep 24 2019 at 17:02):

The 'ProfileName' for the root element should show up as the 'Name' of the resource. The 'short name' for the root element should be the title of the resource. For extensions, you'll need to add a 'Name' column to capture the 'Name'. Title should be grabbed from the 'short name'.
