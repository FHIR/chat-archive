Bob Milius (Feb 22 2022 at 19:28):

@John David Larkin Nolen replied in email. His process is:

  1. Download the master
  2. Make a branch with my username and the JIRA number
  3. Make changes and run the publisher locally.
  4. Expect some errors and make fixes. Usually a change in the resource will break either an example or two and/or the search parameters.
  5. Fix that and get the build to work locally.
  6. Commit and push
  7. Fire off a pull request and cross your fingers. Sometimes the CI build publisher finds things that are not found locally.
