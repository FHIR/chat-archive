Marc Duteau (Mar 09 2022 at 01:04):

As I understand it, part of the goal of using steward in THO is to know which WG to go to for approval to make changes. So for example if a WG had a value set in THO for an ig, they’d be the ones voting to approve the ticket before it goes to consensus review rather than every change requiring a vote from vocab.

Robert McClure (Mar 16 2022 at 15:06):

@Grahame Grieve Vocab is not the steward for much at all. @Marc Duteau has it right. With few exceptions that tend to be global things, the WG responsible for content, meaning the WG that sponsored the work that lead to the content, SHALL be the steward for the code systems created.
