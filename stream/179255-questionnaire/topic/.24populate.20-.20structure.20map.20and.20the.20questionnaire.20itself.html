Topic: $populate - structure map and the questionnaire itself

Alexander Kreutz (May 18 2021 at 09:57):

One question about performing the StructureMap based $populate operation: Does the Questionnaire resource itself somehow go into the population process? Is it part of the bundle that is passed to the source StructureMap? Or is the source StructureMap actually the lone source of the structure for the generated QuestionnaireResponse?

Paul Lynch (May 18 2021 at 10:27):

The $populate operation is either Questionnaire/$populate or Questionnaire/[id]/$populate. In the latter case you have the Questionnaire instance indicated in the URL. In the former case, "one and only one of the identifier, questionnaire or questionnaireRef in parameters must be provided". So the $populate operation has access to the Questionnaire instance either way. Whether it has to use it, I am not sure, but the output QuestionnaireResponse must contain a reference the Questionnaire and makes use of its linkIDs.

Alexander Kreutz (May 18 2021 at 10:35):

Yes, it is clear which Questionnaire should be used for the $populate operation. But how does the Questionnaire go into the processing? Must all linkIDs etc. have to be replicated in the StructureMap used?

Lloyd McKenzie (May 18 2021 at 15:40):

The Questionnaire isn't needed by the processing. The Questionnaire simply indicates the queries to be performed to produce the 'source' Bundle and the StructureMap to execute. The StructureMap then generates a QuestionnaireResponse that references the Questionnaire.
