Lloyd McKenzie (Mar 25 2020 at 14:28):

Comments against something approved but not yet applied are more likely to be looked at, though there's a significant risk that someone going through and applying changes will look only to the resolution and not subsequent comments. Can you raise this on the committer's stream? I'd like a wider set of opinions on what we should do.
