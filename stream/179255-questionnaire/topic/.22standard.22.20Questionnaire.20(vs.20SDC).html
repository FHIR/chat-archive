Topic: "standard" Questionnaire (vs SDC)

Paul Lynch (Feb 20 2019 at 16:06):

Several of our tools provide an option for viewing/saving both an SDC Questionnaire or a plain, regular, standard Questionnaire. If the user picks the second option, they get a Questionnaire without any extensions whatsoever. I am having doubts about this behavior, and am wondering whether we can safely drop this non-SDC option to reduce confusion for the user. In particular:

1) Is there such a thing as a "standard" questionnaire, and does including extensions make it non-standard?
2) Are there scenarios in which an SDC Questionnaire loaded with its extensions would cause a problem that would be solved by our plain no-extension Questionnaire?

Lloyd McKenzie (Feb 20 2019 at 16:09):

I don't see any benefit in stripping the extensions. Any client should be able to consume an SDC Questionnaire and ignore any extensions they don't care about. If they don't, they're a pretty poorly-behaved client.
