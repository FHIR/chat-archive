Paul Lynch (Feb 27 2020 at 23:10):

I recently submitted J#25949 for the purpose of removing confusion and explicitly stating in the spec something I think is implied already, that QuestionnaireResponses do not contain items without answers. Does anyone have a concern about that? It was suggested on today's Questionnaire call that we might, for instance, want to say that QuestionnaireResponses in memory could have items without answers, but persisted ones can't. My concern with that is then if you ran FHIRPath on the memory version and the persisted version, you might get different results.

Kenneth Myhra (Feb 28 2020 at 12:17):

We just had such a discussion in-house a week ago. As far as I know our implementation builds up all the items without answers and also persists those items which are without answers at the moment. Clarifying or stating it explicitly that these items should not be part of the QR is a thumbs up from me.
