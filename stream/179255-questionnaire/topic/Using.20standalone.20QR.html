Lloyd McKenzie (Mar 15 2022 at 01:51):

We can definitely chat about it. SDC currently demands that the Q's canonical URL be specified. However, in theory you could use an extension that just communicates the Q's identifier instead of canonical if that's all you have. Without having some sort of identifier for the Q, you have no way of knowing that 3 different completed QRs are all for the same form, nor any hope of finding QRs for a given form.
