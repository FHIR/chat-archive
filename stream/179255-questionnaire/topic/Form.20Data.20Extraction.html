Paul Lynch (Jan 24 2020 at 20:28):

http://hl7.org/fhir/us/sdc/toc.html is the old version. http://hl7.org/fhir/uv/sdc/2019May/extraction.html was the balloted version, which since has had various updates and improvements. The very latest version (which is sometimes broken or unavailable) is at http://build.fhir.org/ig/HL7/sdc/.

Paul Lynch (Jan 24 2020 at 20:30):

The history of versions page is at http://hl7.org/fhir/uv/sdc/history.html. Note that the version labeled "current" is the STU 2 (old) version, because the STU 3 is not (quite) published yet. We are still working on issues.
