Oliver Egger (Jan 31 2020 at 09:24):

As decided at the last TC meeting we want to perform two ballots for:
Normative ballot: CDA-CH V2.1 (2020)
STU ballot: FHIR CH-Core (0.1.0)
The voting procedure will start on 14.2.2020, the materials will then be provided online for the two ballots. The current intermediate status before publication is available at http://build.fhir.org/ig/hl7ch/ch-core/index.html and http://e-health-wiki.ch/index.php/Ehscda:CDA-CH_2017_(specification).

To participate in the voting procedure you need an account from http://www.hl7.org/.

Send me this account by email until 14.2.2020 so that you can participate in the voting procedure (for organisations as members 2 participants can participate). You need to be a member of the Swiss HL7 affiliate to participate. We need at least 10 participants to make the voting process successful!

On 14.2.2020 we will also inform you how to indicate the votes/comments. Enclosed the timetable and the ballot rules.

Please do not hesitate to contact me if you have any questions.

Oliver Egger (Mar 08 2020 at 11:15):

The voting procedure is through with the following intermediate result:

Normative Ballot: CDA-CH V2.1 (2020): Yes (15), Abstain (1), 0 comments STU-Ballot: FHIR CH-Core (0.1.0): Yes (13) , Abstain (0), No (4), 29 comments Both ballots have achieved the required quorum.

Since the CDA-CH V2.1 Ballot received no comments and achieved a greater yes share than 80%, the specification will be accepted.

The FHIR CH-Core Ballot has a lower yes vote than 80% and negative comments. I have listed the comments separately in the Google Sheet and opened respective GitHub issues: https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1QsmHHNBoa3XYPjkeIvR7TutcTRkQHzpVkaLM14eDRDM/edit#gid=492689224

We will start to discuss, resolve the issues on our Telco March 16h, from 4pm on, see https://github.com/hl7ch/ch-core/wiki
