Stream: genomics / eMerge Pilot

Topic: Back patching CG IG

Larry Babb (May 08 2019 at 00:08):

Question: will the CG IG back patch all changes that are made to Resources being used that are in the main spec. I'm trying to understand if the CG will keep pace with the main FHIR spec updates over time?

Patrick Werner (May 08 2019 at 13:37):

Most of our profiles are based on Observation which is already normative. So there won't be breaking changes to be expected.For other resources like Device/DiagnosticReport with less maturity there can be breaking changes. I expect that the IG will always be based on the latest FHIR release available. Do we have an official position on that? @Kevin Power @Bob Freimuth @ Bob Milius @Gil Alterovitz

Kevin Power (May 08 2019 at 13:50):

I don't think we have an official position, so this is just my opinion. I think it is reasonable to base our IG on major releases of FHIR at least until all of the core resources we profile become normative. Since you mention back patching, I suppose it is possible for us to just adopt a subset of features in a core resource by adding them to our profiles instead of basing on future release.

Bret H (Aug 21 2019 at 03:23):

The published guide should be versioned with releases of core FHIR resources - that being said the only guaranteed to be supported resources are the normative ones. However, I think the community has a right to expect that the CG IG will be kept up to date. PS see Patrick's response on Observation.
