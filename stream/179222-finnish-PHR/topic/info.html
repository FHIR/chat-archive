Pirjo Vuorikallas (Mar 14 2018 at 08:00):

Hi, next Tuesday 20.3.2018 12:30-15:00 is again (once in a month) FL7 Finland PH SIG Kanta PHR support meeting, agenda: http://www.hl7.fi/wp-content/uploads/HL7-20180320-OTV-skk-nro-3.pdf

In these meetings, you can get advices about how to use HL7 FHIR based Kanta PHR national data content in your integration / application. In addition, proposals for changing or expanding the current Kanta PHR data content are discussed and reviewed. Anyone interested in Kanta PHR / FHIR / Personal health can participate in these meetings. The language in meetings is Finnish, but we are ready to change to English if needed.

More information (in Finnish): http://www.hl7.fi/sig-toiminta/personal-health-sig/omatietovarannon-tukiprojekti/.

You are welcome to join the meeting and comment for example what kind of training you will need related to Kanta PHR!
