Giorgio Cangioli (Dec 07 2020 at 20:40):

The plan for the FAIRness for FHIR project (its first “product” is the FHIR4FAIR FHIR IG) is to start with regular calls begin of next year after the FAIR track at the next Jan FHIR connectathon.
The main goal of that track will be that of collecting inputs for the development of the FHIR4FAIR FHIR IG.
To better prepare the FAIR track and discuss the project planning I propose to have a preparatory call before Christmas.
This is a doodle (https://doodle.com/poll/nvvnvg35zbcukbz2?utm_source=poll&utm_medium=link ) for deciding the time for this first call.
