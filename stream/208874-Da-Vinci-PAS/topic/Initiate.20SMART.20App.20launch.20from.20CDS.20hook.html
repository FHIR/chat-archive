Eric Sornson (Dec 11 2019 at 19:49):

For any payer systems looking to integrate PAS workflows that launches a SMART app via CDS Hooks: We, Cerner, do not yet support developers in self-service registering and simulating CDS Hooks calls from our developer portal tools (https://code.cerner.com/developer/smart-on-fhir/apps). We can work with any developer to manually register your CDS Hooks services within our open EHR testing environment. A few things to keep in mind:

  • We support patient-view, order-select, and order-sign hooks.
  • Be sure your SMART app is running over port 443 or 80
  • Any appContext returned by the CDS Hooks Service should be a simple string. We don't have full support on it yet. Furthermore, the appContext value will be in the access token as cerner_appContext field. This is only for our current Connectathon server, and not intended as a long term solution.
