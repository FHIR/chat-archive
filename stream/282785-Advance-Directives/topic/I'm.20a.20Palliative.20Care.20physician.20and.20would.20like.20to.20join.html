Corey Spears (Sep 27 2021 at 03:40):

Hi Hank,
Thanks for reaching out. We would love to have you join our efforts. If you would like, we can look to arrange some time to have an introduction or orientation of sorts. Our continuing integration build of the Implementation Guide can be found here: http://build.fhir.org/ig/HL7/pacio-adi/ (Please note that we have some significant resource changes that will be happening in the next couple of weeks based on our findings from the Connectathon and Working Group Meeting.
We have several calls a week. You can find the call in details here: https://confluence.hl7.org/display/PC/PACIO+Project+Advance+Directive+Interoperability+%28ADI%29+in+FHIR
Currently we are looking to ballot in the January time frame. We are starting with a US Realm ballot, but have interest in expanding this to an international scope, so we definitely want participants from outside the US. (We only started with the US, because that represents the expertise of participants we had to launch the effort.)
Please let us know how we can best help get you involved.
