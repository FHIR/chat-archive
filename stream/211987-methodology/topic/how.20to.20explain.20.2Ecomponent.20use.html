Lloyd McKenzie (Jan 20 2022 at 14:22):

I don't know that there's standard language. People yelling that "you're doing it wrong" isn't a typical problem. And, as a rule, we try not to overload IGs with explanations of "why did we do it this way" unless it was unclear/controversial. (If that information was captured, it would typically be in ElementDefinition.rationale in the differential - so it shows up if you wade through the data dictionary, but not in the main profile page.)
