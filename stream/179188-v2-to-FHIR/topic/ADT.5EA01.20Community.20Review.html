Today we are starting the first Community Review of the ADT message map:
https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1kPOewgrlY4Mpi8W3HyLzb5J-tuZnUBAzgsXuhX5lcGo/edit#gid=0
-This is the first of the message maps we are putting out for community review
-This is NOT a ballot, rather the first opportunity to get wider input into the completeness and correctness of the proposed mapping.
The intent is to solicit any comments to:
-The overall organization of the message map
-The appropriateness of the segment to segment map associations
-The clarity and completeness of any comments associated with a segment
-The format and accuracy of noted resource references
Comments and suggested updates should be entered in the spreadsheet against the cell to which they apply (select the cell and click on the + icon in the short cut bar). We appreciate to include in your comment the problem (if the cell is not blank) and a proposed solution/wording.
The deadline for your comments is November 10th.
Any questions, please contact Hans and/or myself.
