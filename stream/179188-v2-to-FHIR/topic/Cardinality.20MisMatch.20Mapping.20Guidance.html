Topic: Cardinality MisMatch Mapping Guidance

Hans Buitendijk (Nov 03 2020 at 16:01):

There is a ballot item indicating that when v2 supports a higher cardinality vs. FHIR (e.g., 0..* vs. 0..1) that we should ask FHIR to change their cardinality. While generally that is agreed to start with that, the question is what to do when FHIR does not want to increase the cardinality as, e.g., it may not fit within the 80-20 rule.
One proposed is to (a) add an extension to support the repititions 2 and up or (b) to not map and let the implementation figure out whether they may need an extension.

We seek feedback on which approach to take.
