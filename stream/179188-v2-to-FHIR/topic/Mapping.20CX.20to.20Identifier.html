Topic: Mapping CX to Identifier

Jean Duteau (Aug 20 2021 at 15:28):

In my latest teaching on the V2 to FHIR project, a question was raised about the CX mapping - http://build.fhir.org/ig/HL7/v2-to-fhir/ConceptMap-datatype-cx-to-identifier.html

CX.4 (Assigning Authority) has two options with the notes: "If organization" and "If system". The question was if there was a way to know from the data whether something was an organization or a system. If there isn't, then this note is somewhat problematic.

Vassil Peytchev (Aug 20 2021 at 17:55):

I don't think that you can do V2-to-FHIR mapping with only knowledge from the data (in general). Such an assumption should be strongly discouraged.

Riki Merrick (Aug 20 2021 at 21:59):

You should be able to know what kind of identifier it is, but you will not
know if the assigning authority is an organization or a system from the
data, but I agree with Vassil, you should probably have more knowledge than
just the data in order to map correctly.
