Topic: v2.5.1 to FHIR mapping?

May Terry (Nov 04 2019 at 02:37):

I've been reviewing the v2 to FHIR mapping worksheets (https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1PaFYPSSq4oplTvw_4OgOn6h2Bs_CMvCAU9CqC4tPBgk/edit#gid=1930219638) and it seems that the mappings are to at least HL7 v2.7 because of the PRT segments. What is the recommendation of the v2-to-FHIR team for systems which have v2.3 or v2.5.1? Are these mappings backwards-compatible with both versions or is it expected that source systems support at minimum v2.7?

Hans Buitendijk (Nov 04 2019 at 19:17):

The v2-to-FHIR mapping is meant to be cumulative. Even if a field is deprecated, the spot is still there effectively. As soon as v2.9 is published, we will cover through that. But somebody using a v2.2 or 2.3.1 message would still be able to use this as all the slots are still there and we plan to map everything that is in use somewhere.

May Terry (Nov 05 2019 at 18:24):

Good to know. Thanks Hans!
