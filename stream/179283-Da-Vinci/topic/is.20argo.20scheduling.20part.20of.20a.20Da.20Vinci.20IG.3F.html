Richard Ettema (Feb 20 2019 at 17:10):

Isaac, the scheduling appointment demo in the HIMSS Interop Showcase Da Vinci vignette is part of the Clinical Data Exchange (CDex) use case. This demo @ HIMSS provided the audience with a glimpse into this particular use case which is still in a draft state within Da Vinci. The CDex IG is not yet published and is currently in a proposal state within HL7. The initial IG content should be available late March/early April.
