What's the ballot status of FHIRCast? Is FHIRCast maintained under any FHIR working group?

Isaac Vetter (Jul 10 2018 at 17:57):

Hi @Cindy Jiang !

Note that we've got our own zulip channel at: FHIRcast.

Yes, FHIRcast is a project sponsored by the Imaging Integration working group. There are fairly regular reports on the progress of the specification during this wg's regular calls.

We're hoping to work towards an initial ballot at the January HL7 meeting. There's still at least one major feature that needs to be added to the specification and tested at a connectathon. The next connectathon is in Sept, see: http://wiki.hl7.org/index.php?title=201809_FHIRcast

Some resources and feedback at:
- http://fhircast.org/
- https://github.com/fhircast/sandbox
- https://github.com/fhircast/docs/issues

Does that help? What's your interest?

Isaac
