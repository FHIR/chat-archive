Gino Canessa (May 06 2020 at 00:29):

  • Websockets use the same notification bundle as REST, so I didn't plan to make another set. Is there something you expect to see different?
  • Email uses the same notification bundle for attachments, with arbitrary content for email (edit: the message itself). I'm not sure what a useful example here would look like, but am open to adding content if you have a suggestion (e.g., emails are a supported format for proper examples, so they'd just be inline text).
  • SMS was removed as a standard channel, so I have no plans to add content around them right now.
  • Messaging examples are on the list, but I haven't had a chance to add them. An older example can be found here in the meantime (from the discussion in Feb - so a little out of date but correct in structure).
