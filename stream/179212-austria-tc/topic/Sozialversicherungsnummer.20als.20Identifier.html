Reinhard Egelkraut (Apr 04 2019 at 13:19):

@Florian Apolloner I am Reinhard Egelkraut from HL7 Austria. Sorry for the late response but I wasn't available for the last couple of weeks due to the after effects of a surgery.
Yes, you are in the right place here, although as @René Spronk mentioned, this stream is indeed not very active, since most of the conversation of the Austrian TC happens during our calls.
And yes, we do already have a definition for the Austrian "Sozialversicherungsnummer" - as identifier.system we are using the fixed uri value "urn:oid:1.2.40.0.10.1.4.3.1".
You will find a version of our base profile for patient datasets in Austria on our github repository, I will send you the according link. Feel free to take a look at it, you'll also find definitions for other identifiers like the Austrian bPK-GH or our latest approach to profile the specialities of an Austrian Address.
