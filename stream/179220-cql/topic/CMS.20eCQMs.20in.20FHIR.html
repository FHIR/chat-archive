Corey Sanders (Dec 07 2021 at 16:01):

Are there official versions of the CMS eCQMS written against the FHIR data model? When I look at the eCQI HealthIT.gov site the measures even for 2022 are still written against QDM. I have been using the Hackathon repository for my eCQM testing on FHIR, but I get occasional questions from business folks whether that is the best / "correct" place to get those knowledge artifacts.

JP (Dec 09 2021 at 17:29):

Hi Corey!

There are not yet published eCQMs written against FHIR. The best place for the draft content are these two repos. These are being maintained by a team working with CMS:
https://github.com/cqframework/ecqm-content-r4
https://github.com/cqframework/ecqm-content-r4-2021

Please see the introduction here:
https://build.fhir.org/ig/cqframework/ecqm-content-r4/

The connectathon repo contains draft Measures for prior FHIR versions and reporting years.
