Working on the CMS Data at the Point of Care (DPC) API. Currently, users who consume this API are required to attest that they have a legitimate purpose and/or relationship for pulling data for the given patient. We're looking at DaVinci PDex,HRex and ATR which are helpful but cover a lot of ground and use cases. Wondering if anyone can point us at specific areas within DaVinci IGs and/or provide guidance for this use case. Thanks!!
