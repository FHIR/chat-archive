Corey Spears (Aug 04 2021 at 21:45):

An autogenerated diff can be found from a link in the QA Report. For the particular version of Plan-net, you can find that here: https://build.fhir.org/ig/HL7/davinci-pdex-plan-net/comparison-v0.1.0/index.html

I am not sure a comprehensive set of change notes are usually kept between a ballot and a published version.

FYI, Plan-net has its own stream here: https://chat.fhir.org/#narrow/stream/229922-Da.2BVinci.2BPDex.2BPlan-Net
