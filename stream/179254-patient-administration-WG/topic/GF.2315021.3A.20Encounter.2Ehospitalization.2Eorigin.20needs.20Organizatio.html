GF#15021:
Encounter.hospitalization.origin should allow target resource type "Organization"

"The location from which the patient came before admission." ...may be known only by the name/identifier of the originating Organization (e.g. hospital, care home...) so it should be alternatively possible to point at the originating Organization instead of just the Location.

http://build.fhir.org/encounter-definitions.html#Encounter.hospitalization.origin
