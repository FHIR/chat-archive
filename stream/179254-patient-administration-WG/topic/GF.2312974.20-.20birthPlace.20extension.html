Cooper Thompson (Feb 22 2018 at 21:24):

12974 - birthPlace extension should be consistent with other Patient extensions

Looking at this tracker, are we sure we want to do what we have in the resolution? The birthPlace extension is in general-extensions not patient-extensions, which means it can be applied to any person-type resource (Pracitioner, RelatedPerson, Patient, etc.). If we update the name to be patient-birthPlace, it still can be used in all those places, but then it's super weird that you have patient-birthPlace on a Practitioner resource.

Also, the vote on that was May 9 2017, but all the zulip discussion was in December, so I think our initial resolution may be something we want to overturn.
