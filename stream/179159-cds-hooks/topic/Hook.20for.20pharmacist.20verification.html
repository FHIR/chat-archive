Topic: Hook for pharmacist verification

Steve Long (Dec 10 2020 at 21:44):

I was looking at the hooks that currently exist, and didn't see one that would be appropriate for CDS that is shown to a pharmacist who is verifying a medication order. The information I'd expect to be sent as context is similar to what is sent for the order-sign hook, except that instead of draft-orders, it would be the information for the order being verified. Does it make sense to use the order-sign hook for this workflow?
Thank you!

@Isaac Vetter , @Josh Mandel, @Dennis Patterson , @Bryn Rhodes - thoughts on this workflow / and whether it merits a separate hook or could use the order-sign hook? Note that the order being verified is an order that has already been signed.

I think order-sign's structure could be used to try out some use-cases, but correct there would be some tweaks to the hook context. Since they aren't draft orders, the CDS Service won't necessarily have all of the same types of guidance and a different hook would communicate that semantic difference in the workflow. I'm curious what kinds of decision guidance any services in question would be communicating (to help paint the picture).
