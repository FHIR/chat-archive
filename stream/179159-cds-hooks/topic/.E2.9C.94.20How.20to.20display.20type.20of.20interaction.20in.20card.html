Lloyd McKenzie (Sep 28 2021 at 15:52):

Why would you provide a card warning about drug-food or drug-alcohol? CDS Hooks isn't where you provide warnings to the patient, it's about where you try to drive a change to prescribing behavior. I.e. Every card should be something that could potentially cause the clinician to change their mind about prescribing. I suppose, a drug-alcohol warning might be appropriate if there are indications of current/recent alcohol consumption issues. However, even then, the text of the message should be enough "Drug X contraindicated due to alcohol consumption" or "Drug X reduces efficacy Drug Y". Stating drug-alcohol or drug-drug would be redundant and unnecessary.
