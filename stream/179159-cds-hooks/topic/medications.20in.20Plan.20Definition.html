Bryn Rhodes (Feb 01 2019 at 15:10):

Determining what decision support is evaluated at a hook is an integration decision. PlanDefinitions can help in that determination by surfacing what data is involved in the decision support, but ultimately it's a question of whether that decision support is applicable at that point in the workflow, and CDS Hooks doesn't prescribe that, it's a content question.
