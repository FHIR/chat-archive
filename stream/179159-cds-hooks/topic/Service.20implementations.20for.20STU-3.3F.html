Dave Carlson (May 24 2017 at 18:39):

Kevin Shekleton (May 24 2017 at 20:30):

Yeah, the FHIR sandbox that is wired to demo.cds-hooks.org is currently DSTU 2. We're going to enhance the sandbox to allow you to hook up any FHIR server (see #19) which will address your need here.

Kevin Shekleton (May 24 2017 at 20:31):

Something that is worth noting too on this topic is that no EHR that I'm aware of has STU3 deployed. Given that STU3 was just released a couple months ago, my guess is that it will be some time before we start seeing STU3 deployments in production.
