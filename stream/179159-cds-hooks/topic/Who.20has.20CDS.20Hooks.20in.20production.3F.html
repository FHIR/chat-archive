Matt Varghese (Sep 09 2020 at 17:07):

Lloyd McKenzie said:

Is there a policy to only roll out hooks that have passed ballot, or is it just that you haven't gotten to the others yet?

I wouldn't say there is a clear policy at this point. However, maturity level is something that will likely factor into hook implementation decision because there is less argument to justify an immature hook, and an immature hook has higher potential for breaking changes etc. Essentially, the hook maturity model is a proxy for how necessary and useful a hook is as well as how clearly the use-cases are understood.
