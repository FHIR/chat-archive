It build on our previous discussion about allowing 'simple' FHIRPath in search prefetch tokens, but adds one additional piece - the ability to convert relative references to simple ids. There's already a custom way of doing that for userId (you can use userPatientId, userPractitionerId, etc.), but this is a one-off and doesn't address the issue of doing it for other parameters (the new order-dispatch proposal has two cases where there's a relativeReference that could be a variety of resources and there may need to be a search with an include on that resource. It's also needed to solve the "MedicationReference.medication" issue.

Take a read, provide feedback, and hopefully we can discuss on an upcoming CDS Hooks call.
