Øyvind Aassve (Sep 08 2021 at 08:13):

Sorry @Mikael Rinnetmäki , I missed out on the part of the meeting where this was discussed last time. As you have already well documented on your page we have developed a number of national base profiles, and we are in the process now of increasing this number quite a bit. However, our base profiles are open without any use of "must-support" and therefore in a way somewhat different than the IPA profiles. Use of must-support will go under the umbrella of domain profiles and we still have a lot of discussion and trying out to do about the development of these - for example what is a good size and context for a domain . For now we are thinking that some will be more general (and IPA-like), while others can express quite complex reusable structures for more specific domains. As of which resources to develop here is still very much on the drawing board.

John Moehrke (Sep 10 2021 at 12:20):

My main point is that these other resources should be driven by other use-case analysis. where IPA should be driven by use-cases of access to clinical data. If we make IPA into an access to everything, then we have not really provided any Implementation Guidance.

These three are available in IG format

  • MHD - https://profiles.ihe.net/ITI/MHD
  • PDQm - https://profiles.ihe.net/ITI/PDQm

  • PIXm - https://profiles.ihe.net/ITI/PIXm
    The others have not yet been converted to IG format

  • mCSD - https://www.ihe.net/uploadedFiles/Documents/ITI/IHE_ITI_Suppl_mCSD.pdf

  • PMIR - https://www.ihe.net/uploadedFiles/Documents/ITI/IHE_ITI_Suppl_PMIR.pdf
  • SVCM - https://www.ihe.net/uploadedFiles/Documents/ITI/IHE_ITI_Suppl_SVCM.pdf

Lastly, to understand all of these one might be best to start with a higher level whitepaper

  • https://profiles.ihe.net/ITI/HIE-Whitepaper/index.html
