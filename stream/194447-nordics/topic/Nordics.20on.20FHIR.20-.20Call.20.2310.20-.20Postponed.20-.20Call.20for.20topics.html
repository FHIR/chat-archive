From Nordic eHealth Standardization group meeting summary 2020-09-17:

  • The Nordic countries recommend that the IPS standard (EN 17269) shall be used as a general information model for structuring or mapping patient summaries for national purposes, and for exchanging patient information across the Nordic countries.
  • The Nordic countries have agreed to work on standardization at national and European level so that the gap between national and cross-border exchange of EHRxF information is as small as possible.
