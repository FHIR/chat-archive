Viktor Jernelöv (Apr 15 2019 at 09:44):

Hi,
One "boring" but quite useful thing to decide on is some form of common guidelines when creating national profiles and extensions:

-pattern for naming them/setting the canonical URL, versioning etc
-formal policies when creating them (ie "don't create something that already exists" and "don't put use case specific stuff in extensions, do that in profiles instead" and similar things that would increase the overall quality)
-what metadata to fill in (status, publisher, useContext etc)

I assume a lot of this is something we could steal from work being done in other countries or work streams, but it's stuff that we all should have to deal with at one point or another and it's most likely quite country independent and would therefor be a suitable thing to solve together.

EDIT: It may very well be so that there is work done in Norway, Denmark and/or Finland that we could agree upon being the "nordic" way of handling these kind of questions.

The agenda topic could be to decide on IF and HOW we push this and similar topics forward.
