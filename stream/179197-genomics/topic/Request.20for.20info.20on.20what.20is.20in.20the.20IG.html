Bret H (Apr 01 2021 at 12:32):

The draft for May is still being confirmed, so too early to give all the specifics. The published version is DSTU 1, in May DSTU2 will be sent out for Ballot. There is not a specific diff page yet. However, some highlights are: consolidation in the pharmacogenomics space with medication-Transporter, medication-High-Risk, medication-Metabolism and medication-Efficacy begin moved to components in Therapeutic Implication. New Operation: $find-subject-variants
Retrieves variants from a specified genomic region. Changes to some examples: http://build.fhir.org/ig/HL7/genomics-reporting/artifacts.html#examples. But wait for more information in the coming weeks.
