Topic: Name of our IG

Arthur Hermann (Feb 08 2022 at 21:36):

Is anyone else becoming bothered by the name of our IG: Genomics Reporting Implementation Guide? I feel that the IG now encompasses much more than a focus on Genomics Reporting (I suppose it all depends on the definition of reporting).... As I write some document for GenomeX, I find myself stumbling on the name of our IG - as it reads to the public as if our IG is focused soley on Genomic Reports, rather than the discrete data which can be contained in these reports. Personally, I feel that we have moved far past the focus of simply moving report data.. instead, we are enabling the movement of machine-readable genomic data. This data may reflect a report, but it may also contain significant data which is not part of any current report.

Arthur Hermann (Feb 08 2022 at 21:37):

I don't know if changing the name of our IG is so onerous a task that we shouldn't even consider it, but wanted to put this out there in case changing it was possible if desired

Kevin Power (Feb 08 2022 at 21:43):

Nothing is impossible, and I am sure there is a process to change the name. I will say however that "Reporting" does NOT equal "Reports" so I am not sure it is necessary. However I would welcome any/all feedback - if others feel the same, we can consider it.

Arthur Hermann (Feb 08 2022 at 22:25):

@Kevin Power I understand that reporting does not equal reports, but I am afraid that the general public will likely read it that way. I am to am interested in what others think. Also interested in the history of why this name was selected. I keep seeing that IG on the transmission/exchanging of Genomic Data - but there must be history I am not aware of.
