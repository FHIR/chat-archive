Do we have official style guidance for profiling FHIR Parameters (e.g., naming conventions) for use with Operations?

Lloyd McKenzie (Oct 20 2021 at 18:12):

Naming conventions for the profiles? Naming conventions for the parameters? To my knowledge, both are a free-for-all

Josh Mandel (Oct 20 2021 at 19:11):

I meant for things like Parameter.name. We recently had an issue in FHIR-I where @Gino Canessa updated parameter names in our FHIR Subscription-related operations to better align with FHIR conventions -- which I think was good for overall clarity, but I don't think FHIR's editorial guidelines ever actually spell out or suggest that HL7-authored (even FHIR Core!) operations should follow any particular conventions.
