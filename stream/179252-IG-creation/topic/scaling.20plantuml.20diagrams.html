Thanks to @Jose Costa Teixeira for finding the key to scaling plantuml diagrams in your IG. Simply add to the top of your .plantuml source the skinparam svgDimensionStyle false

@startuml
skinparam svgDimensionStyle false
title My really big plantuml diagram that will scale inside the browser
