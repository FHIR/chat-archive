David McKillop (Jun 26 2018 at 05:05):

Hi @Grahame Grieve , @Brett Esler - I tried to add the additional information to tracker#17117, but I didn't see a way of adding more screen images/files to the Forge tracker.

The issue seems to be that the actual binding eg in the case of Practitioner.gender to "Value Set http://hl7.org/fhir/ValueSet/administrative-gender" the link is being replaced with a link to "unbound" (http://hl7.org/fhir/STU3/terminologies.html#unbound) .
For example when viewing Practitioner.gender in the Agency profile the binding to "administrative-gender" is present in Forge - refer to:
GForge-17117-Agency-20180626.JPG
And the correct binding is in the Au Base Practitioner which the Agency profile is derived from - refer to:
GForge-17117-Au-Base-20180626.JPG
But when the Agency Practitioner (the derived profile from the Au Base Practitioner) is run through the IG Publisher, the output has the link to administrative-gender replaced by a link to "unbound" refer to:
GForge-17117-Agency-IG-Pub-output-20180626.JPG
So the issue seems not to be an "Additional Unbound link", but a case of the terminology binding being replaced with a link to "unbound" when the profile is a derived profile and the binding is a type other than "Example".
