Lloyd McKenzie (Mar 16 2022 at 18:16):

Accelerator IGs are considered HL7 FHIR IGs. They'll go through balloting like any other HL7 IG

Lloyd McKenzie (Mar 16 2022 at 18:18):

We haven't set a firm rule prohibiting changes to color or other formatting, but my general leaning would be against it. Adding the accelerator logo is sufficient to differentiate. From a branding and consistency perspective, we want all HL7 IGs (whether developed within an accelerator or not) to have a consistent appearance and behavior. Also, the colors are intended to differentiate between FHIR, CDA and v2 IGs, so divergence loses us that benefit.

David Pyke (Mar 16 2022 at 18:52):

Grahame Grieve (Mar 16 2022 at 20:07):

technically, this is a product management issue that I rule on. And if I do, then it'll be current practice as described by Lloyd: colour is for product family, and accelerators add a logo for the accelerator
