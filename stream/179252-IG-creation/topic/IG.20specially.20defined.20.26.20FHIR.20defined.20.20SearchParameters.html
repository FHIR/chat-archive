Topic: IG specially defined & FHIR defined SearchParameters

Corey Spears (May 12 2021 at 18:40):

A question about common or preferred practice regarding SearchParameters in IGs. Specially defined or required SearchParamters need to show up in the capabilityStatement and the IG, but how about FHIR pre-defined parameters that an implementer may want to include in their implementation? Are those or should those be included in the capabilityStatement in some way? If it is included in the CapabilityStatement, what should the definition url be? Is there a canonical URL for R4 standard defined SearchParameters or does an IG have to "redefine" all of them?

Lloyd McKenzie (May 12 2021 at 18:44):

IG's must redefine any (referencing the original) if they want to assert constraints about modifiers or other aspects. Otherwise they can just reference the standard URLs.

Corey Spears (May 12 2021 at 19:32):

Thanks, and I found what I was looking for. For future reference, the canonical url is defined in the search parameter registry: https://www.hl7.org/fhir/searchparameter-registry.html
