Lloyd McKenzie (Jun 25 2021 at 04:19):

Creating a profile on CapabilityStatement would mean you're saying "these are rules for other systems that define CapabilityStatements". If you're wanting to publish your own CapabilityStatement, that'll be a CapabilityStatement instance, not a profile on CapabilityStatements in general.

Does your existing metadata.xml file have any narrative? If the narrative is omitted entirely, one should be generated by the publication process.
