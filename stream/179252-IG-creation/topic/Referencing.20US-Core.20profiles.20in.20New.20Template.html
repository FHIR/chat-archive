Nagesh Bashyam (Oct 11 2019 at 15:25):

@Lloyd McKenzie , In the new IG template I want to re-use the US-Core profiles without any modifications , how do I get them to show up on the Artifact page since it is auto-generated ?

Lloyd McKenzie (Oct 11 2019 at 15:33):

You can't, at the moment. The artifacts page only defines artifacts defined within the IG. All artifacts from all imported IGs are available but they're not listed, in part because we don't have any mechanism of telling the IG which ones are relevant (that typically won't be all of them). You can either have an external pointer to the artifacts page (or equivalent page(s)) in the target IG or you can manually define your own list of external links. We've talked about adding a mechanism to 'import' artifacts from a referenced IG and publish them as if they were local, but there's some tricky things with making that work and we haven't had time to fully explore it.
