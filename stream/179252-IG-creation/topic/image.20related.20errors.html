Chris Moesel (May 26 2020 at 21:21):

Based on a past conversation with @Lloyd McKenzie, images must go in the images folder. That is the only folder that the publisher will blindly copy files from. Files in pages and pagecontent must be processed by Jekyll to get copied over to the final IG, and binary files are not processed by Jekyll. See this sub-thread for the original conversation where I learned this: https://chat.fhir.org/#narrow/stream/179252-IG-creation/topic/Template.20changes/near/186949666

Jose Costa Teixeira (May 26 2020 at 21:34):
