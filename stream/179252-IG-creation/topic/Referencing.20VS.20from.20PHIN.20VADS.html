Rob Hausam (Aug 09 2018 at 02:31):

When I did the OccupationalData resource originally (I think Chris may be working on the profile?) I had the same question and decided to use the urls. The terminology service doesn't currently support the urls or urns for expansions, etc., and PHIN VADS doesn't provide a FHIR API. So I don't think there's a great answer, but it would be good to work out a more robust general strategy for it as working with PHIN VADS will be common in some contexts.
