Grahame Grieve (Aug 17 2021 at 04:57):

this thread is deeply confused. There's no reason to talk to Joshua Procious. There's two registries:

  • registry.fhir.org. There's multiple ways for any FHIR author to get content in there, and anyone is allowed to (and encouraged to) publish there.
  • a higher level summary of IGs at http://www.fhir.org/guides/registry/. You make a PR against https://github.com/FHIR/ig-registry to get in there.

In neither case is there any requirements around IP from HL7 to do that
