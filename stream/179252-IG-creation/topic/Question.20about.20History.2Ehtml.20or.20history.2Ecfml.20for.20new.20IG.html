I believe this gets created as part of the publication process. @Grahame Grieve ?

Grahame Grieve (Dec 18 2019 at 19:11):

yes. it is generated from package-list.json. When you submit for publication, the last thing you do is

  • download the current package-list.json from [canonical]/package-list.json
  • add an entry for your new release
  • save this in the root of your repo
