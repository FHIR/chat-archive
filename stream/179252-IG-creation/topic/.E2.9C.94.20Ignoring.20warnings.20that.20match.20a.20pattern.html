Lloyd McKenzie (Apr 05 2022 at 20:27):

Note that you need to be cautious about using wildcards. In general, you ought to check each of your display names and confirm they're correct. Even if most of the issues are caused by tooling, there's still a chance that one or two of them are wrong.
