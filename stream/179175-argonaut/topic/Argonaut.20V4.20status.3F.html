Topic: Argonaut V4 status?

Adam Flinton (Jun 07 2021 at 12:59):

Dear All,
We are looking at a FHIR Facade to serve up our data in a standard / interoperable way. For example an Observation or Procedure. The decision from those above me is to "go with International V4" which is a bit loose at the macro component level. So my basic question is how mature argonaut for V4 is (i.e. how not subject to change it is) & how comprehensive. i.e. we do not want to be creating our own macro-datastructures if we can avoid it as that clashes with the standard & interoperable aims. At the moment we are specifically restricting ourselves to "GET" with POST to follow once we have get done.

Brett Marquard (Jun 07 2021 at 13:08):

In the US Market place the US Core 3.1.1 (based on FHIR R4) is named in ONC Cures Update Certification regulation and is a good version to target. It contains the latest lessons from Argonaut...The latest continuous build is here: http://build.fhir.org/ig/HL7/US-Core/ . I would be careful to design in a way that's flexible for International and other use cases not currently covered US Core
