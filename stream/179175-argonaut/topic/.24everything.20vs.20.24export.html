Topic: $everything vs $export

Josh Mandel (Mar 06 2019 at 21:28):

Looking at where we landed in the bulk data project, our $export operation is very, very close to the (now evolved) $everything operation. In the current spec for $everything I see the following language:

In the US Realm, at a minimum, the resources returned SHALL include all the data covered by the meaningful use common data elements as defined in the US Core Implementation Guide. Other applicable implementation guides may make additional rules about how much information that is returned.

@Grahame Grieve how does the base spec make this kind of "SHALL" constraint about US Realm usage? Any why the tie to US Core, rather than Argonaut? Does this make sense, or is it just an oversight/mistake?

Josh Mandel (Mar 06 2019 at 21:32):

(This bit of language makes it much harder to profile the operation; I'm trying to understand whether we really need a separate $export operation, and this is maybe the only argument for it that I've seen so far.)

Lloyd McKenzie (Mar 06 2019 at 23:01):
