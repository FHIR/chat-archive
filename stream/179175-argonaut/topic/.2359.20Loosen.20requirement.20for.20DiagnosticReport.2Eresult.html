Eric Haas (Dec 06 2016 at 23:02):

#59 Loosen requirement for DiagnosticReport.result to allow for text, fax, etc
Need to follow up on whether need to require as an invarient either:

  • results ( references to Observations)
  • and/or presentedForm (attachment type – could include anything)

  • and/or Image (reference to Media)
    *

  • See example of this implemented here : http://hl7.org/fhir/us/core/StructureDefinition-us-core-diagnosticreport.html with results and/or presentedForm required.

  • Or is this too prescriptive and should we just leave it at DiagnosticReport.result 0..1 ?

Danielle Friend (Dec 08 2016 at 10:30):

I don't have a strong opinion either way. It is possible (though probably rare?) that an order receives a status of "preliminary" - for us that often happens if an LIS sends a V2 message with a preliminary status but without any results.
I'd be curious to hear what others think.

Eric Haas (Jan 03 2017 at 20:38):

I propose leaving out the invariant . making it a "SHOULD" but recognize that prelim reports may not have results. So is a must support + 0..* for results, images and presentedForm which covers lab. I've updated both ARgonaut DSTU and US-Core guides to reflect (undergoing QA.)
