Robert McClure (Oct 11 2020 at 15:20):

While Gender Identity is not in USCDIv1 and I lead the Gender Harmony project and encourage you to review that material as we head to a logical model ballot that should include some example changes we'd like to see in FHIR and elsewhere. I'd like to point out there is regulatory guidance from ONC on expectations for recording gender identity and sexual orientation. I'm not thrilled with this guidance but it does exist in the sections for Paragraphs (a)(5)(i)(D) and (E) in the test method certification guide. I'll note that many in the community working on the gender harmony ballot hope to see this improve once the work is completed.
