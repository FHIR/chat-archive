Welcome to #Forwarding Intermediaries.

Description: Discussions related to using FHIR on a forwarding intermediary with the intermediary being both FHIR client and server. Several national architectures employ a forwarding intermediary scheme (Sweden, Netherlands, ...) based on legacy protocols like SOAP WS-I. The intermediary may also perform aggregation of responses from multiple FHIR servers using a record/system/server locator.
