Topic: STU changes for Sept. ballot

Lloyd McKenzie (Jul 09 2018 at 22:17):

The FMG plans to put out a limited scope FHIR STU ballot. I.e. The ballot will not cover everything in the core specification that was part of the May ballot. Instead, it will be limited to those resources or pages that have undergone sufficient significant change that the responsible work group feels an additional ballot is necessary prior to publication of R4. Note that we will only have about 4 weeks from the close of the WGM to content freeze for QA for R4 (and you'll need to get reconciliation complete before you can make your changes). So it's in your interest to only ballot those things that really need it. Please reply to this thread if there is content in your area of responsibility that you feel needs to be included in the Sept. ballot - preferrably before Wednesday afternoon :)
