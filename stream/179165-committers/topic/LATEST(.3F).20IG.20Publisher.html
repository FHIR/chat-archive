Chris Moesel (May 17 2019 at 12:28):

The other approach (which I didn't think of last night) is to continue with SNAPSHOT but don't rev the version number (except maybe with major releases). For example, if you set the version to 1.0.0-SNAPSHOT, that would typically be interpreted as a daily build leading up to an eventual 1.0.0 release. You could publish 1.0.0-SNAPSHOT many times, and clients (or URLs) that ask for 1.0.0-SNAPSHOT would always get the most recently published 1.0.0-SNAPSHOT.
