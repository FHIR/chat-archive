Topic: FMG Publication requirements for Balloted IGs

Grahame Grieve (Jul 30 2016 at 04:51):

This is a candidate list of FMG Quality requirements for ballot Implamentation guides:

  • the home page must be 'index.html'
  • the canonical URL must be http://hl7.org/fhir/[realm]/[id]
  • the home page must have a link for 'IG publication history' near the top of the page. It must point at an empty page 'history.html '(FHIR product director maintains the actual history)
  • there must be no invalid xhtml or broken links (from the build)
  • the validation page (/qa/validation.html) must show no errors (hints and warnings are ok)
  • the home page must be clear about the license (creative commons 0)
