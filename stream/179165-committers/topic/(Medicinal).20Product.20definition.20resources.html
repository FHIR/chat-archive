Rik Smithies (Dec 21 2019 at 14:02):

The boundary is mainly one of level of detail. But the level of detail is closely related to the use case. Day to day prescribing (by far the most common use case) needs hardly any detail. Regulatory use cases need lots and lots of detail. So generally, unless you know that you need MedicinalProductDefinition, then you probably don't. We may need to make this clearer in the documentation. We've been busy getting the resources approved and the documentation is probably lacking. And yes, the level of detail issue could in theory be done using profiles, but this would be difficult in practice since the most common use case would need only a tiny small subset of the full model.
