For the purposes of creating an inventory, would you use a Group or a List? (not that I have a particular use case in mind)

Group is generally for a collection that can be acted upon (e.g. be the subject of an Observation, Procedure, etc.). That doesn't seem to be relevant for inventory items. So my leaning would be either List or just having the inventory reflected in what instances exist on the server
