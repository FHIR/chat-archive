Topic: Don't touch ballot/ballot-weight in gForge!!

Lloyd McKenzie (May 02 2018 at 19:29):

Just a reminder that the "ballot" and "ballot-weight" columns in gForge should NEVER be edited. They are set automatically by the process that handles ballot spreadsheets. That process does not change this ballot cycle, even though we're encouraging people to submit tracker items directly. The process for balloting using trackers is to submit your tracker items, then submit a ballot spreadsheet to the HL7 ballot website that only includes the columns for vote and tracker number.
