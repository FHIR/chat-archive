Grahame Grieve (Oct 02 2019 at 21:18):

The forth coming technical correction to R4 is now open for review until Oct-18 2019.

It is published for review at http://hl7.org/fhir/R4B (and the change list is visible at http://hl7.org/fhir/R4B/history.html#v4.0.1).

Please submit issues by email to grahame@hl7.org

Notes:

  • FMG wishes to draw your attention to https://confluence.hl7.org/display/FMG/Proposed+Policy+for+Breaking+Changes which describes policy for making breaking changes to normative content, and applies to this technical correction. Comments are welcome on this
  • The package infrastructure is being rebuild completely. if you want to see what the packages will look like after the technical correction, look at https://github.com/FHIR/packages/tree/master/hl7.fhir.rX
  • Each page in the technical correction includes a special 'compare Tech Corr to R4' - this will be removed once we post the technical correction
  • As a technical correction, there is no formal ballot, just this open review period
