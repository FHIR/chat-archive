Lloyd McKenzie (Jun 17 2016 at 04:56):

I've been reluctant to add the ability to filter by profiles because there's so darn many of them (and they keep changing). The way gForge works, the complete list of allowed values has to be maintained by an administrator. (And there's no API to load the contents from a file or something nice like that). So at the moment, they all get lumped under (profile) and the work group they're assigned to. In your case, given that CG doesn't own many profiles, that should hopefully be "good enough"?
