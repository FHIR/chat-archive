Ryan Howells (Aug 06 2020 at 19:04):

Most payers are brand new to the HL7 FHIR community and so DaVinci and HL7 wanted to hold an event focused exclusively on the requirements payers have to meet for the CMS Interoperability rule that includes the Patient Access API. They invited Argonaut and CARIN to participate since we are each working on different aspects of the rule (US Core/USCDI and CARIN IG for Blue Button). Everyone is invited to attend although the primary audience is the development teams (payers, apps, enablers) who are working to implement the CMS interoperability rule. . . and of course the technically-oriented patient advocates on this thread. The first two days are educational for the technical community and the third day will focus on testing. @Viet Nguyen is working with HL7 to organize.
