Topic: BMJ's Patient Partnership

Dave deBronkart (Sep 19 2019 at 12:36):

As I've mentioned, in 2014 the BMJ (nee British Medical Journal) initiated a Patient Advisory Panel, which interacts regularly with the editors. Further, the panel is part of a larger patient-public partnership, which includes the remarkable requirement that the authors of research articles must state how they involved patients in the design & conduct of the study!

So what we're doing is far from unprecedented. :-)
