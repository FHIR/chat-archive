Lloyd McKenzie (Jun 12 2019 at 21:08):

My notes from lunch:
- We need a "Patient Advocate/Support" work group at HL7 (name to be determined). Mission to include:
-- vetting content produced by HL7 work groups for appropriateness of their content for solving actual patient and caregiver requirements
-- providing an on-ramp for patients/advocates/caregivers who are interested in either using our standards or influencing our standards to help enable them to do so
- Grahame will look at where we might get sponsorship to help enable advocates to attend/participate at face-to-face meetings
- We (TSC and/or FMG) need to change our project/implementation guide 'proposal' process to specifically highlight how the project will alter/enhance patient experience (should be reviewed by above work group). It's ok if some projects don't expect any patient impact
- IG quality guidelines should change to mandate the inclusion of a section in each IG that is specifically addressed to the patient community that explains what the purpose of the IG is, how it will influence the care they will receive and how, when relevant, they can take advantage of it directly.
