John Keyes (Jan 05 2021 at 16:35):

It is now possible to submit feedback for the draft Patient Corrections Implementation Guide. From the footer of the IG, click on Propose a change, which will take you to Jira. Log in to Jira (if you are not already), and then click the Create button. On the Create Issue page, make sure that the Project field has FHIR Specification Feedback (FHIR) chosen, and then you should choose Patient Corrections (FHIR) for the Specification field. You can also make appropriate selections for the Raised in Version (currently 0.2.0), Artifact(s), and Related Page(s) fields as needed. Then provide your feedback in the Summary and Description fields, and press the Create button. Thank you! (cc @Debi Willis , @Virginia Lorenzi )
