Virginia Lorenzi (Jan 06 2021 at 07:54):

I am not sure how a denial or a disagreement are stored in the EHR or communicated. It would make sense that both are communicated as documents (DocumentReference). @Cooper Thompson I know you have been looking at this - any idea? I am going to ask around.

Cooper Thompson (Jan 07 2021 at 17:33):

We have a narrative section on the Chart Correction case where the reason behind the disagreement can be documented. So a disagreement/denial would show up as a Chart Correction case that had 1) a status of closed 2) no data changes made and 3) some narrative information about why nothing was done.
