John Moehrke (Dec 06 2018 at 17:18):

Hi @Dave deBronkart . IHE is a standards organization focused on 'profiling'. Profiling is taking a specific use-case that is identified by the marketplace as important, and specifying how that use-case would be best implemented. To do this IHE selects standards that best fit, and constrains those standards specific to that use-case. Their most well known standards are XDS, XCA, PIX, PDQ, etc. See http://www.ihe.net

John Moehrke (Dec 06 2018 at 17:20):

The 'this' I was refering to is 'patient empowerment'. As that is a high-level goal that is common to IHE and HL7. Gemini is an initiative between HL7 and IHE to cooperate.
