Breaking the ice in our new Patient Care WG stream by kicking off a discussion in response to http://community.fhir.org/t/resource-for-oxygen-administration/2972
Looking for implementer feedback on what FHIR resource is used to 1) order, 2) administer, and 3) measure oxygen.
Specific to Cerner, our answers would be 1) ServiceRequest, and 2&3) Observation.
Seeking input from other EHR vendors (e.g. @Emma Jones @Amit Popat @Danielle Friend @Daniel Rutz ) to see if they are also using ServiceRequest and Observation -- or if anyone, by chance, is using MedicationRequest/Admin or Procedure instead.
