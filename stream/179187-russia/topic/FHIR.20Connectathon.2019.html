Lloyd McKenzie (Jun 30 2018 at 14:17):

There's also the FHIR Applications Roundtable on the 27/28. It's in a different venue, but only about 45 minutes away.

  • The Roundtable is a whole bunch of short presentations about what different groups are doing with FHIR. (You'd be very welcome to present too.)
  • The Connectathon is 2 straight days of working with FHIR on a specific "track". (You can see the candidate tracks here: http://wiki.hl7.org/index.php?title=Category:201809_FHIR_Connectathon_Track_Proposals)
  • The WGM is where we will work through issues with the specification. There are also tutorials and a receiption on Wed. night. You can see the schedule from the May WGM here: http://wiki.hl7.org/index.php?title=FHIR_Agenda_201805_WGM. (Unfortunately, the schedule for Sept. won't be finalized until about a week before the meeting.) Nothing much happens on the Friday, so that might be a good day to tour around Washington DC.
