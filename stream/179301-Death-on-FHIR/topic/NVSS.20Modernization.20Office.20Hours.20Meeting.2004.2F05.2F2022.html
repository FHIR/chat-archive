NCHS Updates

Monitoring Calls starting in Q4 (May to July , 2022)
Starting in May, NCHS will be participating in Q4 calls hosted by the ELC Program get updates on where vital records offices are with their modernization efforts. Jurisdiction will be hearing about participating in these calls most likely from their state ELC Program Coordinators. Ideally someone from the program and technical side of the VRO will participate in these calls which will focus on understanding progress made in meeting TIER III objectives, technical and programmatic challenges and areas where NCHS/COP can assist.

Upcoming opportunities for working towards FHIR certification-
The NVSS COP is inviting jurisdictions to partner with NCHS and work towards FHIR certification by December 2022/January 2023. Once a jurisdiction is FHIR certified, they will be able to send and receive FHIR data to NCHS in production. Ideally jurisdiction that have already successfully participated in one or more Connectathons and/or NCHS organized testing events and are familiar with the VRDR IG and supporting tools (e.g. libraries , canary ) may be well positioned to participate. If interested please send in your name to the NVSS modernization mailbox.

Even if you are not interested in working towards FHIR certification for your systems but are interested in working with NCHS with pilot testing efforts, please send a note the NVSS modernization mailbox:.
