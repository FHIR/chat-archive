Saul Kravitz (Mar 08 2022 at 20:50):

Regarding #4:
We are working to update the VRDR STU2 and FHIR Messaging Implementation guides, along with all associated software (canary, VRDR dotnet, etc) in time for the next testing event.

Both IGs are in final review after completing ballot reconciliation. We are dotting the i's and crossing the t's on the VRDR IG, so its current state should be very close to the version published as STU2. There has been significant addition of documentation. See for example the Decedent profile.
We expect a release of IGs and associated software around mid April 2022.

We invite your feedback on the latest builds of the two IGs.

Prachi Mehta (Mar 09 2022 at 15:37):

Regarding # 4
The version of the IG and associated tools released in mid April 2022 will be used for the NCHS testing event organized in June, 2022. Note that the new proposed date for the NCHS testing event is June 27-28th (and not May 16-17th, 2022) This is to give jurisdictions and their vendors more time to update their systems and work with the tools leading upto to the testing event. NCHS will organize TA calls with jurisdictions that want to participate in the June testing event approximately 4 weeks prior. If you would like to participate, please send an email to nvssmodernization@cdc.gov
