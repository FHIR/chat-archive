These questions came up at the July 27 COP Office Hours. Answers will be forthcoming.

Q1) If we submitted a record initially as an IJE and we have transitioned to FHIR, would an update message be a new submission or an update?
A1) Regardless of what format was used for the original submission and the update, an update is an update.

Q2) If we submit a message an an error message is returned, is the resubmission a new submission or an update?
A2) New submission.
