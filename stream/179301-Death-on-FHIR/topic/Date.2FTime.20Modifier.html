Topic: Date/Time Modifier

Sirisha Sathyamurthy (Feb 03 2022 at 14:08):

@Pete Krautscheid
We are collecting date of death/injury modifier for a death record with values like Actual Date of Death, Approximate Date of Death, Presumed Date of Death, Court Determined Date of Death. I could not locate a property to map this value in VRDR death record.

Kate Brett (Feb 03 2022 at 14:43):

These are fields that will be defined in the MDI IG but are not in VRDR IG. Would it help to use the definitions from the other IG?

Pete Krautscheid (Feb 03 2022 at 21:53):

@Sirisha Sathyamurthy as Kate mentions they aren't currently in the VRDR IG, which is driven by the fact that they aren't reported to NCHS; given that, those fields can currently be skipped when creating a VRDR record (though this may change if at some future point NCHS becomes interested in that information or if it's used for inter-jurisdictional exchange).

Sirisha Sathyamurthy (Feb 04 2022 at 19:38):

@Kate Brett
It would nice if it can be included to the VRDR IG as an optional value. We are interested to hear if other jurisdictions collect this data.

Catherine Cheney (Feb 09 2022 at 14:31):

@AbdulMalik Shakir @Grahame Grieve @ Yes we should approach adding the Death Modifier (Actual Date of Death, Approximate Date of Death, Presumed Date of Death, Court Determined Date of Death) to the IG as part of the Death Record for the VRDR - agree with @Kate Brett , @Sirisha Sathyamurthy

Saul Kravitz (Feb 09 2022 at 15:13):
