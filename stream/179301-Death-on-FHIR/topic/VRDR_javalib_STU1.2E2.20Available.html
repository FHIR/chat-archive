Topic: VRDR_javalib_STU1.2 Available

Michael Riley (Aug 26 2021 at 20:21):

Hi Death on FHIR community, this is just a reminder that now that the STU 1.2 IG is released to the public, the VRDR_javalib library, which allows you to easily read, populate, manipulate and export VRDR documents has been updated to the newest release to support it. You can find the new versioning here. https://github.com/MortalityReporting/VRDR_javalib/releases/tag/v1.2.0-R4-STU1.2

Note: as a rule going forward we are going to practice a version scheme of "v<source_version>-<fhir_version>-<ig_version>" and we will try to mirror the source_version and ig_version when possible.
