With the profile that is built on top of another profile, do I need to include all elements from the source profile (e.g., extensions, slices) or only provide differences (additional constrains, secondary extensions, etc.)?

Lilian Minne (Jan 13 2020 at 13:53):

Hi Shamil, you can choose: the snapshot of a profile describes all elements including all elements of its parent, while the differential only describes the differences compared to its parent.
