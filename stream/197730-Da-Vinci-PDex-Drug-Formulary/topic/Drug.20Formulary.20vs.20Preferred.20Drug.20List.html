Kate Dech (Apr 08 2021 at 15:03):

Adding my two cents on this. Preferred Drug Lists may or may not be tiered. Generally this used in open formularies where only preferred products are managed on the "list" but any other product may covered as "non-Preferred" (given other exclusion rules like bulk chemicals, repackaged products, etc.). So being Preferred is a tier or a part of a tier.
