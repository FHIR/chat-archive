Saul Kravitz (Oct 30 2020 at 15:49):

  • The 'D' -> 'd' change is the key one.
  • There is an example of the DrugAlternatives extension that was missing from the 1.0.0 (see https://build.fhir.org/ig/HL7/davinci-pdex-formulary/MedicationKnowledge-FormularyDrugV3001.html)
  • There was an issue with the way the extensions were defined that bothered some validators that has been changed.
  • The IG has been rebuilt using FSH for future maintainability

Other than the 'D'->'d' there should be no impact on existing data or implementations.
