It has profile validation and a series of tests around the supported searches, etc. They can be found here: https://touchstone.aegis.net/touchstone/testdefinitions?selectedTestGrp=/FHIR4-0-1-PatientAccess/Pharm-Formulary&activeOnly=true&contentEntry=TEST_SCRIPTS
