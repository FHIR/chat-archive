Chris Moesel (Sep 29 2021 at 19:35):

That's ANTLR (our parser) speak for "I expected to see X in this part of the statement, but you put Z. Z is not a viable token to have in this spot." In your case, it's saying that after reading contactParty BackboneElement, so BackboneElement is the thing it did not expect to see. That's because it's expecting to see a cardinality there, not a type. You swapped the cardinality and type on that line.
