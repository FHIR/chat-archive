Nick Freiter (Aug 18 2021 at 13:39):

Here is an example of how to set the text.div: 

Instance: P
InstanceOf: Patient
* text.div = """<div xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml">
This is a simple example with only plain text
</div>
"""
* text.status = #additional
* name.family = "foo"

and a here is the link in FSH Online. The important note is that the content of the text.div must be valid xhtml.
