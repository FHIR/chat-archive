Chris Moesel (Oct 16 2020 at 12:31):

Yeah -- when it comes to Profiles and Extensions, whenever you ask "Is there a way to..." the answer is almost certainly yes, and often involves using a ^ rule. As far as I'm aware, the only things you can't do in FSH for profiles and extensions are the things that you also can't do in StructureDefinition or any other profile editing tool (in other words, things not supported by FHIR at all).

Chris Moesel (Oct 16 2020 at 12:34):

Unfortunately, I can't say exactly the same thing for CodeSystem and ValueSet. In theory, FSH does allow you to author any CS or VS and use their advanced features -- but in order to do that, in some cases you need to abandon the FSH CodeSystem/ValueSet syntax entirely and author it as an InstanceOf: CodeSystem or InstanceOf:ValueSet (which is much more tedious).
