Currently GoFSH ignores the .text element. Example: https://fshschool.org/FSHOnline/#/share/3yACorz

Chris Moesel (Dec 17 2021 at 13:50):

Hey @Patrick Werner. Thanks for including a FSHOnline link, but unfortunately the FSHOnline links only encode the FSH text. So when I click on it, I don't get the JSON you are trying to run through GoFSH. Can you attach a file here in Zulip w/ the JSON?

Patrick Werner (Dec 17 2021 at 13:52):

Hi @Chris Moesel i attached it to the issue: https://github.com/FHIR/GoFSH/issues/165

Chris Moesel (Dec 17 2021 at 14:02):

Ah. Perfect. Thank you. So... this is actually intentional. Since the narrative is marked as generated, GoFSH does not decode it into FSH. This is under the assumption that generated narratives weren't a part of the original source -- and that the IG Publisher will re-generate them on build. GoFSH also does this if the narrative is marked as extensions (since that also means it is generated). If the status is not generated or extensions, however, GoFSH will decode the narrative into FSH.

Chris Moesel (Dec 17 2021 at 14:03):

If there is a use case for reproducing the generated narratives, we could consider making that a configuration point. But if not, the FSH looks a lot cleaner without all that generated narrative included in it!

Patrick Werner (Dec 19 2021 at 14:23):

Makes a lot of sense, will remove the generated tag.
