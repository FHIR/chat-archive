Chris Moesel (Dec 01 2020 at 23:05):

As of SUSHI 1.0, SUSHI does not generate ig.ini anymore (except for the backwards-compatible "legacy" mode). Generating ig.ini was controversial and it's a very simple file that hardly ever changes -- so it didn't seem too much of a burden to have users maintain their own. So sushi --init will create one for you at the start, but after that you're on your own.
