Chris Moesel (Feb 09 2021 at 13:37):

Ah... you mean just the science of writing a SearchParameter in general? Yeah, I agree, there is not a lot of documentation I can find that addresses that directly. I think having a solid understanding of how search works (documented here) helps, along w/ the SearchParameter resource documentation. But after that, you still need to connect a lot of the dots on your own. For me, I find it most helpful just to look at a lot of definitions and learn by example. There is a page called Defined Search Parameters that lists all of the search parameters in the whole FHIR spec. One nice thing about this page is that it has a link near the top to download all the definitions as a FHIR Bundle (XML or JSON) -- so you can also see how each search parameter is actually defined.

Other than that, I don't have any other guidance, but perhaps someone else does.
