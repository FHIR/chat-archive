Mark Kramer (Sep 09 2020 at 16:00):

For those of you coming to the Shorthand track tomorrow and Friday, the track is a bit different than traditional connectathon fare. We will feature "office hours" (open questions and discussion, hands on assistance) and the following tutorials (with some exciting new content):

1) Introduction and Beginning FHIR Shorthand: Motivations, Overview, Basics of the FHIR Shorthand language
2) Advanced FHIR Shorthand: Producing IGs with SUSHI, extensions, slicing, instances, caret paths, rule sets,
3) Wicked FHIR Shorthand: Planned future power-user features: Macros, soft indexing, context paths, logical models
4) FSHing Equipment: Tools to help with FSH: FSH Online, Go FSH, FSH School

The schedule is as follows (times in Eastern US time zone):

THURSDAY:
9:00 am - 6:00 pm – Open Office hours

4:00 pm FSHing Equipment
