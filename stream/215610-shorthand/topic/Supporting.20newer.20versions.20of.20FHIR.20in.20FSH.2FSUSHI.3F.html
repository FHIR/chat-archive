I'm kind of stuck since my guide is all in FSH but I do need the 4.5 versions of some resources. @Chris Moesel do we (and by we I mean the royal we) have a timeline on when SUSHI/FSH might support 4.5.0 or current?

Chris Moesel (Jan 06 2021 at 22:43):

Hi @Jean Duteau -- it depends on what you need from 4.5. If you don't need native FSH support for integer64 or CodeableReference then you can probably get by with the next release of SUSHI (due in the next two weeks). That release will be the first release that allows you to specify your version of FHIR as 4.5 or current. That said, the 4.5/current stuff isn't heavily tested yet, so you should think of it as a beta experience.
