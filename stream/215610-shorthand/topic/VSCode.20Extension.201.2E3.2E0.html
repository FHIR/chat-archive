Topic: VSCode Extension 1.3.0

Mint Thompson (May 13 2021 at 18:01):

For all FSH authors who use the VSCode extension, version 1.3.0 of the extension has been released. This update improves the performance of the "Go To Definition" feature, so those of you with large IGs should be able to notice that feature going a bit faster. You can install the update by going to the "Extensions" sidebar and clicking the "Update" button under the FSH extension. If you don't have the extension yet, search for vscode-language-fsh in the Extensions sidebar to get it.
