Jens Villadsen (Apr 16 2020 at 10:41):

How do you state every wednesday in effectiveTime in CDA?

Matt Szczepankiewicz (Apr 24 2020 at 16:28):

Hey Jens--maybe you've already gotten an answer for this in the past week, but I think the upshot is to use a PIVL_TS with @alignment="DW":

<effectiveTime xsi:type="PIVL_TS" alignment="DW">
    <phase>
        <!-- Apr 22 2020 was a Wednesday -->
        <low value="20200422" />
        <width value="1" unit="d" />
    </phase>
    <period value="1" unit="wk" />
</effectiveTime>

Matt Szczepankiewicz (Apr 24 2020 at 21:05):

I'm not sure whether it's better to include the width or whether that would imply "all day every Wednesday" though.
