Peter Jordan (Nov 12 2019 at 07:55):

Either way, given a choice, I'd plan to holiday after the Connectathon/WGM to avoid the end of the School Holidays. Should you only have 10 days in NZ, you'd probably make better use of time by sticking to one of the two main islands. Central North Island offers quite a good variety of outdoor activities and Maori culture (Rototura, Lake Taupo, Tongariro Crossing, etc.) and it's an easy drive over to the Bay of Plenty if you fancy a day at the beach. The South Island merits more time and the main centres of Queenstown and Wanaka will still be overflowing in early Feb.
