Quick pedantic note on terms : there's a distinction between an absolute URL and a canonical URL; all canonical URLs are absolute, but not all absolute URLs are canonical URLs. So to be precise, it's better to talk about populating Refernece.url with a "relative or absolute URL" rather than a "relative or canonical URL".
