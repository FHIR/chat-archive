Lilian Minne (Apr 30 2018 at 07:53):

Hi Jessica,

That's sounds like a great idea. In the Netherlands we have the Medical Intelligence initiative, which is a collaboration between Dutch academic hospitals and Furore to build a generic architecture and share knowledge on clinical data collection for research and quality measurement. The first hospitals now started to investigate the possibilities of FHIR.

One of them is using Questionnaire and QuestionnaireResponse to automatically extract data from their EHR and send it to the auditing party. Another one has built a Vonk Facade server as a layer above their custom non-fhir data warehouse. The Vonk Facade server provides the mappings to FHIR resources, which are sent to an application researchers use for data collection of their patients. If you are interested, there's a tutorial online on how to build a Vonk Facade Server: http://docs.simplifier.net/vonk/facade/facadestart.html

Good luck!

Lilian
