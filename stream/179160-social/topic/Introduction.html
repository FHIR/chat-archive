Abbie Watson (Dec 09 2016 at 00:33):

Hello!
I'm Abigail. Grad student in the Masters of Science in Biomedical Informatics program at the University of Chicago. I have about 15 years of experience in the industry, as an MRI Technologist, RIS/PACS Admin, Cerner Analyst, HL7 Analyst, etc. etc. but never managed to get an advanced degree. So, eventually it was time to go back to school.

Anyhow, I've signed up for our Healthcare and Interoperability class next quarter, and will be using that course as an opportunity to develop out a full-stack javascript implementation of the FHIR standard I've been working on for the past year. The project uses the Meteor javascript platform, and is called "Meteor on FHIR", naturally. And for the next few months I'll be busy getting it connected to our Epic 2015 instance, validating that it conforms to Argonaut Sprint 3 and 4, implementing the rest of the base resource schemas, etc. etc. The plan is to dig deep into the FHIR standard in the upcoming months, so I may be commenting a lot and posting lots of questions. Hopefully I don't make a nuisance of myself, and can contribute to the overall discussion.

I found myself catching up on a bunch of posts today, and maybe contributing in a qualitatively different way than in the past; and it occurred to me that it might be a good idea to give an introduction. Happy Holidays and looking forward to working with everybody in the upcoming year!
Abby
