Topic: Hot Beverage FHIR IG is live!

David Pyke (Apr 01 2021 at 12:28):

Due to overwhelming demand to have a way to communicate with coffee machines via FHIR, the Hot Beverage FHIR IG is live. This IG implements RFC 2324 to give full morning pleasure to FHIR users and implementers alike. And, thanks to @John Moehrke it has full auditing so no worries about unauthorized coffee drinking will slip by. This desperately needed FHIR IG can be found at https://build.fhir.org/ig/DavidPyke/HotBeverage/ We will be looking to move it to HL7 and normative stats as soon as we get a good cup of espresso
