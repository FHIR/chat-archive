Andrey Fedorov (Feb 07 2019 at 19:30):

@David Johnson hehe ... If you create an HL7 login, and then navigate to "Home > Membership > My HL7 Dashboard > My Subscriptions", you should see "Want to see recent discussions from these lists?", where "see recent discussions from these lists" links to "Recent Listserv Messages All messages sent in the last 30 days to listservs you subscribe to are shown here." (http://www.hl7.org/myhl7/recentlistmessages.cfm)

Elliot Silver (Feb 07 2019 at 19:32):

To see older content, click on the "Lyris listserv interface" link on http://www.hl7.org/myhl7/managelistservs.cfm?ref=nav.
