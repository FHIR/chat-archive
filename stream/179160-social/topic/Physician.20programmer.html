@Michael Albrecht You would be very welcome on the FHIR-FLI Slack group which is run by @Grey Faulkenberry and @John Manning . Grey has also built a Flutter library called "fhir_auth" which can turn your native app into a Smart on FHIR app. I have baked this into a questionnaire filler that can send its questionnaire responses from protected FHIR endpoints (see https://github.com/tiloc/faiadashu). Check out https://github.com/MayJuun/fhir for details on Flutter FHIR libraries and info on how to contact Grey and the Slack group.
