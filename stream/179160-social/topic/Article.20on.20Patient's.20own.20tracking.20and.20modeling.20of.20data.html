I've often spoken about the Quantified Self movement, including at my 2018 Amsterdam DevDays talk. Its founder Gary Wolf (@Agaricus on Twitter) has just posted a really thought-provoking article on Medium about how he's cobbling together his own tracking and modeling of data for his Essential Tremor condition.
