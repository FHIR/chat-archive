Hi all, I'm a veterinary informaticist working for VCA (a large hospital and lab services provider in the companion animal domain). I understand that veterinary involvement in FHIR has been pretty light so far, and I'm hoping to promote FHIR use within the profession (as there is fairly poor standards adoption industry-wide, largely due to the lack of consumer or government pressure to do so). Incidentally, the Association for Veterinary Informatics (http://avinformatics.org) is the best single place to find informatics-savvy veterinarians, if that is helpful. Anyway, glad to be participating. Thanks for pointing me here, @Grahame Grieve.
