Andrea Pitkus, PhD, MLS(ASCP)CM, CSM (Mar 07 2019 at 16:12):

From a lab testing perspective for opiates, there are several aspects that might apply. O&O WGs include LIVD (Lab In Vitro Diagnostics), O&O, Service Catalog (for drug screen orders from lab catalogs), FHIR Workflow (order, specimen collection, testing, reporting); Specimen DAM (specimen info for testing), etc. However, not sure what FHIR implementations are in production as it hasn't permeated clinical labs quite yet as current regulatory and reporting requirements are HL7 v2 format.

For the public health aspects, you may wish to see the last CLIAC meeting slides and presentations on testing and what CDC and health departments are reporting.
