Topic: Pt Empowermt WG initial priorities announced

Dave deBronkart (Apr 24 2020 at 15:47):

This month our relatively new WG completed the process of defining initial priorities, out of the infinite spectrum of possible projects. The document is posted on Confluence here:
Patient Empowerment WG priorities

The Care Planning and Consents projects will interact with existing HL& WGs and projects.

Corrections to Errors in the Record and Patient-Contributed Data are "net-new to HL7" projects (as @Lloyd McKenzie put it) so we will create Project Scope Statements.

Project leads:

  • Care Planning: @Lisa Nelson
  • Consents: @John Moehrke @Mikael Rinnetmäki
  • Corrections: @Debi Willis @Abigail Watson
  • Patient-Contributed Data: @Jan Oldenburg @Maria D Moen

We intend to be involved in various tracks at the Virtual Connectathon, May 13-15, so if you have anything going on that touches on these issues please let us know!

Also be aware of the #patient empowerment stream here.
