Lloyd McKenzie (Jan 03 2020 at 15:20):

If you want the connectathon to 'count' from a FHIR resource maturity level perspective, there are a few criteria you need to adhere to. The criteria are here: https://wiki.hl7.org/FHIR_Connectathons (@Joshua Procious I couldn't find this page migrated to Confluence yet?) Essentially the event needs to be open, you need to make clear what the scenarios are in advance and the results need to be reported and shared with HL7's FHIR Management Group.
