Brendan Keeler (Mar 04 2021 at 06:36):

Carequality predates TEFCA by several years. TEFCA is in its infancy but the Sequoia Project is the recognized coordinating entity with Carequality as a sort of subrecipient.

Brendan Keeler (Mar 04 2021 at 06:37):

With the new administration, it will be interesting to see if TEFCA is finalized and if so, what it looks like in that finalized form

Brendan Keeler (Mar 04 2021 at 06:38):

For the time being, Carequality is the dominant trust framework for organization to organization exchange

David Pyke (Mar 04 2021 at 13:49):

TEFCA and Carequality are very different designs. Carequality is a point-to-point trust framework with a directory. TEFCA is a muti hop network with overarching trust framework. Both are run by Sequoia

David Pyke (Mar 04 2021 at 13:49):

TEFCA isn't active, the design of the Common Agreement and the Technical Framework should be out for comment in the next couple months
