Topic: Health track at the MyData 2019 conference

Mikael Rinnetmäki (Sep 06 2019 at 05:26):

The Health track at MyData 2019 tackles the myriad of issues related to highly sensitive health data in its various forms from clinical trial data and medical records to wellness data from wearables and mobile apps.

The MyData Health Group hosts a workshop around these topics and two further sessions include accepted presentations and panels such as
- “Keeping control and minimizing risk in secondary usage of health data”,
- “The Internet of Things and the future of the web: Privacy-enhancing techniques in IoT”,
- ”How can we make personalised medicine a reality while ensuring data protection?”,
- “SalusCoop: Citizen data cooperative for health research”,
- “Sharing Health Data Through Semantic Containers”.

https://mydata2019.org/event-track/health/
