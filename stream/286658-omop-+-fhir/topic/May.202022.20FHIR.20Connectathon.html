Connectathon Track proposal has been submitted for the FHIR-to-OMOP (Oncology) Use Case : https://confluence.hl7.org/display/FHIR/2022-05+FHIR-OMOP+Oncology
Please review and reach out with questions or feedback on how we can make this better.
Note that the use case is oncology-related but our work applies to any organization that focuses on translations from FHIR to OMOP. This is the only HL7/OHDSI partnership use case focused on translation from FHIR to OMOP - the others are converting from OMOP to FHIR. Therefore, we welcome any one that is interested, regardless of clinical domain, to join us in this exploration and effort.
Feel free to reach out to me if you have any questions or are interested in participating!
