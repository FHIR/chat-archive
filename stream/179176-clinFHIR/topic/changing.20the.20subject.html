I tried to change the subject of a medication resource in the scenario builder and it wouldn't let me change it. When I tried to update the changes in the change log, it ignored all the changes. am I doing something wrong? let me know if you need additional info.

David Hay (Sep 15 2017 at 20:55):

Hi Senthil - If you do mean the Medication resource, then that doesn't have a subject so you can't. If using one of the patient referenced medication resources (like MedicationRequest) then you should be able to add a new Patient resource to the scenario, then change the subject in the MedicationRequest resource to that Patient.
