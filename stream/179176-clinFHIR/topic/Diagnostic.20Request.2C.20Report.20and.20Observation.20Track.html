Joel Schneider (Jan 20 2017 at 17:13):

In case anyone's interested, here's a Patient/Specimen/DiagnosticRequest example recently assembled by one of my colleagues:
https://github.com/nmdp-bioinformatics/dash/tree/master/fhir/fhir_hla_examples
For the DiagnosticRequest, he used LOINC code 13303-3, for HLA-A+​B+​C (class I) [Type]. Apparently, there wasn't a code to fully cover our typical genetic typing activity which, in addition to genes HLA-A, HLA-B, and HLA-C, includes genes HLA-DRB1, HLA-DRB3/4/5, HLA-DQB1, and HLA-DPB1 (and may also include the CCR5 gene and genetic blood group predictions for ABO and Rh).
