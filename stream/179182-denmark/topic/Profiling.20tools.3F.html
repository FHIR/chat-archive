Topic: Profiling tools?

Thor Schliemann (Sep 26 2016 at 07:49):

Question from Lars Kristian Roland, Norway: What are your thoughts on tools for FHIR profiling? We’ve been looking at simplifier.net, but it has a rather high license fee. Do you see a point in coordinating how to do this within the Nordic countries, for example by using similar or the same solution?

Jens Villadsen (Sep 26 2016 at 17:51):

I would say it really is up to what the usage is all about. In its base, it is merely a place to store your profiles. If you can render the profiles yourself and don't need the fancy parts of simplifier, a regular git-repo will do it for you. And for national use, a single profile can be enough ... but that utterly depends on what the need is

Michael Johansen (Nov 10 2016 at 15:07):

We in MedCom are also looking at simplifier.net, and see the benefits in a common repository. We will be all ears in Amsterdam NeXT week
