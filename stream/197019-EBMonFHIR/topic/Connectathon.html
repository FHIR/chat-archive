Rob Dingwell (Mar 22 2022 at 15:33):

Great, @Brian Alper looking at the track it appears as though the notion will be to have participants take a trial from clinicaltrials.gov and map that into an EvidenceVariable, is that impression correct?

Brian Alper (Mar 22 2022 at 16:08):

@Rob Dingwell That is a way of doing it without having to determine your own set of "eligibility criteria" -- you can pick any trial from clinicaltrials.gov and you will find eligibility criteria as an unstructured text entry, then show how you would provide the same information as structured content with EvidenceVariable. You are also welcome to bring your own data to adapt the exercise to meet your specific needs.
