Care Planning topics this weekend include review and testing of the draft HL7 CPG-on-FHIR IG -- Clinical Practice Guidelines on FHIR, plus relationship with IHE profile for dynamic care plans. These IGs document patterns for using PlanDefinition, ActivityDefinition and CQL for practice guidelines, and using those definitions to create care plans and manage care for chronic conditions.
