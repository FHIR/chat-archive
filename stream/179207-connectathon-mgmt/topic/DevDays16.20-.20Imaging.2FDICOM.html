Brad Genereaux (Nov 09 2016 at 19:12):

Hi all - looking forward to DevDays next week, and looking at imaging / DICOM topics!

The Imaging/DICOM track focuses on the integration of medical imaging content and workflows into FHIR. Topics include the clinical workflow of a typical imaging department (from order to acquisition to report) and how FHIR complements it, a deep-dive of the imaging-specific FHIR resources, and an introduction to the DICOM / DICOMweb standard (international specification on medical imaging). Both radiology (X-rays, CTs) and enterprise imaging (photographs, point-of-care ultrasounds) concepts will be covered. In addition, IHE related imaging profiles that include FHIR (like MHD-I) will be reviewed.

Get Involved in DICOM / imaging standards - here's the relevant links -
- Individuals can be added to any DICOM WG as “observers” by going to http://www.dicomconference.org/contact/participation-in-dicom-activities/
- Intersection of FHIR and DICOM is covered by composite groups in both HL7 and DICOM
--Join WG-20 (DICOM) by e-mailing contact information to the DICOM Secretariat: dicom@medicalimaging.org
--Join Imaging Integration (HL7) by joining II mailing lists and teleconferences, find out more at: http://www.hl7.org/Special/committees/imagemgt/
- Join IHE Radiology by visiting https://www.ihe.net/Radiology/
