Ardon Toonstra (Nov 08 2019 at 12:43):

Hi All!

For the upcoming DevDays in Amsterdam, I am wondering if people are interested in a ‘birds of a feather’ session discussing how interoperable national programs, such as Argonaut and the Dutch/Finnish PHR program, are at an international level.

For example, think on how much effort it would take a USA based healthcare IT company, that has implemented US core, to start operations aboard. How easy or how hard is it for them to join the Dutch PHR program. Or the other way around. We could discuss where the bottlenecks are. At the data level or rather with regards to accessing the data based on different authorizations implementation? Should we do anything about it?

Who thinks this is a good subject to discuss? Even better: will you be at DevDays and able to add something to the discussion?
