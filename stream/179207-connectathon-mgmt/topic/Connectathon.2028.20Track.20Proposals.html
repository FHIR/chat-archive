Sandy Vance (Jul 14 2021 at 20:53):

The 28th HL7 FHIR Connectathon will take place September 13-15, 2021 and will be an all virtual event. Track proposals are due on Wednesday, July 21. (next week!) and final schedules for Whova will be due on July 30th.

If you would like to bring a track to the September Connectathon, please review the Connectathon Track Lead Responsibilities Page here: https://confluence.hl7.org/display/FHIR/Connectathon+Track+Lead+Responsibilities
and the Track Lead Check List of key dates on the right side of the Connectathon 28 Confluence Page here:
https://confluence.hl7.org/display/FHIR/2021+-+09+Connectathon+28.

Scroll to the bottom of that confluence page and click the “Create New Track” button to enter your track information.

*Tracks submitted not using this form or left incomplete will be removed from the Connectathon 28 Confluence Page on July 23rd. Please let me know if you have any questions!
