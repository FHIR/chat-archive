Aaron Seib (Feb 05 2018 at 02:02):

Jason - a week has passed since the connectathon and I am interested in the integration track. From the Consumer Centered Data Exchange Track perspective I am interested in figuring out how the consumer can be included in any instances where data is exchanged across enterprises. I think an emerging expectation is that if data can be made available to someone else on my behalf that it should also be made available to me to do what I want with it. If you refer me to a specialist and make my data available to him is there any reason I shouldn't be able to get a copy of that data too? I'd be interested in participating in an integration track because I am sure it is not as easy as that but at the same time I might be able to add some ideas about how it could be made easier to achieve as a goal.
