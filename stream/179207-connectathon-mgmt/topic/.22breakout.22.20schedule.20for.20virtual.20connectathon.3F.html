Sandy Vance (May 11 2020 at 02:24):

Hello all! The Main Connectathon Go To Webinar will have the typical "all hands" sessions. The kick off and wrap up, Richard's Intro to FHIR and Tooling sessions, and 15 minute "sharing" sessions for any track who wants to sign up. Each Track Lead will be given zoom credentials to run their zoom for their own track and you are encouraged to schedule at least two "status" sessions where people who are not on for the entire time can check in. All meeting credentials will be sent out Wednesday morning. We will publish the list of all tracks with their respective chat stream and zoom links listed as well. That will come out via email Wednesday morning also.

Sandy Vance (May 11 2020 at 02:26):

So in essence - since breakouts can be handled via zoom - there is not a break out session. However there is an opportunity for tracks to schedule time to engage the broader connectathon community with their track and share info for newbies.
