Isaac Vetter (Mar 27 2019 at 18:38):

And the FHIRcast STU ballot is starting in just a few days.

HL7's ballot process is an important method for requesting feedback on an interoperability specification and a successful ballot bestows a mark of maturity. FHIRcast is balloting at maturity level "Standard for Trial Use". HL7 folks will read the spec here: https://fhircast.hl7.org and submit comments here: http://www.hl7.org/dstucomments/. We'll update the specification based upon these comments and discussion around them. Once all comments are addressed, we'll update the HL7 FHIRcast site and will have an official and published specification!
