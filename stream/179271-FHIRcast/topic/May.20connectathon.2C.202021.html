Isaac Vetter (May 19 2021 at 17:31):

FHIRcast track report-out, any feedback?

FHIRcast
Summary
FHIRcast provides modern, simple, application context synchronization. As the number of production-grade releases of FHIRcast increases in 2021 tested interoperability of existing implementations using the STU2 specification.
Additionally, implementers tested prototyped functionality for an STU3 version of the specification.

Implementer Participants
Not including observers, implementers include: Siemens, Nuance, Vital Images, Philips, Epic

Topics of interest
Advanced UI integration beyond simple open and close of coarse context, enabling multiple disparate applications to communicate UI selection of finer-grained clinical concepts.
Improvements to synchronization error scenarios
Exchange of clinical content beyond simple exchange of coarse context information.

Now what?
Throughout 2021, the Infrastructure & Messaging work group has hosted FHIRcast design sessions investigating the above topics.

Design is continuing around these topics, likely to result in an STU2 version of the specification with the goal of preserving backward compatibility for the “simpler” context synchronization use-case well enabled in STU2.
