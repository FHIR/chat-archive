Isaac Vetter (Apr 14 2018 at 07:52):

Hi!

FHIRcast is a fledgling, modern, simple application context synchronization spec. It's built on top of the SMART app launch protocol to enable rich integration between full-featured healthcare applications. Think CCOW, but simpler and cheaper, using FHIR and http.

Following implementer feedback from the January FHIR connectathon and thanks to involvement from @Jenni Syed and @Niklas Svenzén , the FHIRcast specification moved away from FHIR Subscriptions to a simple set of web hooks interactions, with FHIR for data exchange, and modeled after WebSub. We'll be testing this iteration of the spec at the May connectathon.

Subscribe to the FHIRcast google group to converse, the fhircast-github stream to see all FHIRcast traffic (issues, wiki edits, commits) and the fhircast-committers stream to just see new commits.

Check out the current list of issues and consider lending your expertise to the help-wanted issues.
