Isaac Vetter (Jul 23 2019 at 14:39):

Tomorrow at 9a Central is our (hopefully) final FHIRcast STU1 ballot resolution call! We'll be voting on the block vote described above and discussing and voting on the proposed resolutions in these 3-5 issues:

  • #143 - Remove query for current context. Also resolves 116 and 142.
  • #126 - How to discover supported events?
  • #89 - rename cast-hub to hub.url

Once we have an approved resolution for all our issues, we'll begin working in earnest on updating the spec to implement these changes with a goal of publishing STU1 on https://fhircast.hl7.org by the end of the summer.

Once STU1 is published, we need to focus on two improvements:
1) Websocket support
2) enabling discrete measurement exchange
