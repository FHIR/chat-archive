Topic: Spreadsheets for terminology resources

Grahame Grieve (Aug 31 2021 at 02:20):

I've had several requests for IGs to contain spreadsheet versions of terminology resources. This is to help many people who want to do ad-hoc things with the content, and know how to work with excel. So from the next release, the IG publisher will start generating spreadsheets for CodeSystems, ValueSets, and ConceptMaps.

However, it's not immediately obvious what 'a spreadsheet' means for these resources. So they won't be published immediately- first, we'll hash out our expectations of what they should look like. I have a code system and a value set here:

Grahame Grieve (Aug 31 2021 at 02:20):

Grahame Grieve (Aug 31 2021 at 02:20):

from the next validator release, you can generate them yourself:

Grahame Grieve (Aug 31 2021 at 02:21):

run

-version 4.0 -spreadsheet [source-file] -output [output-file]

and you'll get the spreadsheet that the IG publisher would produce.
