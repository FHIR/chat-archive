Michael Lawley (Mar 06 2020 at 14:21):

Different servers will behave / support large CodeSystems differently. Ontoserver is known to work with very large CodeSystems (e.g., HPO, NCBI Taxonomy). Note that the main issue with SNOMED is not so much size, but the details of the associated semantics that a server needs to handle.
