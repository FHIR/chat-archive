Topic: $expand with displayLanguage

Michael Lawley (May 06 2019 at 19:46):

If I have a ValueSet v where v.language = "en" and I have a relevant CodeSystem supplement that provides translations to French and then I do a [base]/ValueSet/v/$expand?displayLanguage=fr then the display property for each code, if it exists, should come from the supplement.

But now I have a ValueSet with strings in multiple languages. How do I represent this?
The only way I can see is to leave the display field for the concept empty, but include a designation with a language="fr" and use="display", but I fear that most terminology server clients will expect the display field to be populated

Michael Lawley (May 06 2019 at 20:02):

It also occurs to me that this is an issue regardless of supplements. The language of a ValueSet (definition) may be different to that of the display fields in a referenced CodeSystem.
