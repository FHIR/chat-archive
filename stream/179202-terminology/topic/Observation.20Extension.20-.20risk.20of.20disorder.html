Lloyd McKenzie (Oct 17 2017 at 00:16):

Observation and RiskAssessment are your best candidates. RiskAssessment allows you to capture more detail, but a raw determination as an Observation would be acceptable. (RiskAssessment is a "tuned" Observation resource like AllergyIntolerance and Condition, so you should expect some overlap in practice.)
