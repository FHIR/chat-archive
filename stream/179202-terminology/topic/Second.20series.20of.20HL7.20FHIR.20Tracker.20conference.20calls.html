Reuben Daniels (Jun 07 2021 at 23:03):

Hi All

This is a brief note to advise that the Vocabulary WG have added a second series of fortnightly conference calls to work through the backlog of terminology-related FHIR Tracker issues. Details for the new call are here: https://www.hl7.org/concalls/CallDetails.cfm?concall=56228

The new series will have its first meeting on Wednesday, 9 June 2021 at 8:00PM UTC (Wednesday, 9 June 2021 at 4:00PM EST; Thursday, 10 June 2021 at 6:00AM AEST).
