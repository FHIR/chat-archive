David Pyke (Sep 10 2021 at 12:36):

The HL7 Terminology Services Management Group is now being formed and they're looking for people with a background in terminology (both expertise and implementation) to join.

If you have a background in one or more of the following, please email me, Rob McClure and Anne Wizauer with your interest and CV.

  • FHIR Standards
  • CDA Standards
  • V2 Standards
  • A FHIR Implementer
  • A CDA Implementer
  • A Version 2 Implementer
  • Up to 3 at large members to represent other perspectives.
    • Terminology (Service) Implementer Perspective
    • Terminology Vendor

At least 2 members should (also) be members of HL7 Affiliates.

Needs:

  • Tooling knowledge
  • Implementation knowledge
  • Terminology view of standards development
