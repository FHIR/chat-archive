Robert McClure (Jan 17 2018 at 19:02):

@David Teirney NUBC (the National Uniform Billing Committee) essentially defines a number of "code systems" - one for each encoded field in UB-04. We've had Field 17 -which is the "patient discharge" - for a long time because it's in C-CDA and elsewhere. NUBC is managed by the American Hospital Association (AHA) and they see the codes used for UB-04 as propritary and require a license to use them. NLM/ONC/VSAC has for years been working with AHA to obtain these codes and it's not happened but we've gotten closer - for that one FL-17 set.

What this means is that "publishing the system" is against NUBC's IP restrictions. Deciding on a FHIR url is a different matter, but I think you want access to the content, correct?
