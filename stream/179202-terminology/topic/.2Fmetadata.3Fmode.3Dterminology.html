Peter Jordan (May 07 2021 at 23:13):

@Lloyd McKenzie that's incorrect. The response to metadata?mode=terminology should be a TerminologyCapabilities resource, and that's been the case since R4. That resource remained at FMM Level 0 for quite some time, but is now at FMM Level 1 - see http://build.fhir.org/terminologycapabilities.html.

For an implementations example GET https://terminz.azurewebsites.net/fhir/metadata?mode=terminology

Lloyd McKenzie (May 08 2021 at 01:08):

Peter Jordan (May 08 2021 at 08:58):

It IS defined on the RESTful API page here... http://hl7.org/fhir/2021Mar/http.html#capabilities - and that page has a Maturity Level of normative. Also, the TerminologyCapabilities resource is owned by the Vocabulary WG, not FHIR-I, and I haven't heard any discussions in their meetings about deprecating it.
