Rob Hausam (Mar 29 2021 at 16:29):

@Alexander Dimitrov I don't want to suggest that you shouldn't map to and use the SNOMED CT codes from the GPS for this, but I will point out that the binding to the value set is required in the problemGPSCode slice, but the slicing rule itself is open. So you are actually free to use the ICD-10 BMG 2017 codes alone (without requiring additional mapped SNOMED CT codes) for Condition.code, and you will still be conformant to the Condition-uv-ips profile if you do that. With that said, though, it's still desirable for us (as a community) to provide a means of mapping the ICD codes (with any ICD variety) to SNOMED CT and to encourage sending the SNOMED CT code in addition to the ICD code whenever possible.

Alexander Dimitrov (Mar 30 2021 at 18:30):
