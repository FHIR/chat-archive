Topic: $lookup for "all properties"

Michael Lawley (Nov 23 2018 at 23:37):

Currently there are two ways to get a code's properties via $lookup:
1. A straight $lookup call and "the server chooses what [properties] to return", or
2. Use the property parameter and explicitly request the things you want

AFAICT, there is no way to ask the server "give me all the properties you know of for this code" without prior knowledge of what they may be.

What I'd like to do it support * (and probably lang.*) as special properties that represent "all (non-lang) properties" and "all lang.X properties". Does anyone foresee a problem with this?

Jim Steel (Nov 24 2018 at 01:01):

Might want to clarify the wording for lang.*, as that will only apply to designations, not to properties. Properties are not language coded (either in the declaration of the property on the CodeSystem or in the population of values on each concept). [Note: properties not being language coded could be its own issue, but its unrelated to the question of $lookup parameter conventions]
