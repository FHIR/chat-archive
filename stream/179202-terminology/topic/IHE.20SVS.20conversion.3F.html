Michael Lawley (Apr 23 2020 at 04:15):

Just wondering if anyone has looked at converting IHE Shared ValueSets to FHIR?

John Moehrke (Apr 23 2020 at 13:47):

IHE now has a profile that took the SVS use-cases (and some others), and re-imagined it using FHIR. -- It was in Public Comment last month, so should be out in Trial Implementation next month after our virtual face-to-face next month. -- See the IHE Profile "Sharing Valuesets, Codes, and Maps" (SVCM) -- https://www.ihe.net/resources/public_comment/#IT
FHIR conformance resources are not yet published, but not much for constraints. It is mostly endorsement of use of a subset of the terminology parts of FHIR.
