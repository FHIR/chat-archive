Paul Lynch (Jun 02 2021 at 15:42):

The desktop IE 11 app support is ending in just over a year, on June 15, 2022. Also, the Angular framework (on which we are basing our SMART app) has deprecated support for IE 11 and it will removed in the next version. Microsoft 365 apps are dropping support for IE 11 in August. That in itself is not a problem for SMART, but I think the number of development frameworks that support IE 11 will rapidly dwindle if not even Microsoft supports it.

I am wondering what the expected timeframe is for EHR vendors to update their embedded browser component from IE 11 to Edge.
