Topic: Use of smart styles

Mikael Rinnetmäki (Jun 10 2020 at 19:49):

On today's call it was mentioned that very few apps actually use the cool feature of SMART styles. In my experience, it's really hard to base the design of the app on just the specified SMART styles. We'd really need a suite of test harnesses, or even screen shots, of EHR implementations, to take all of that into account when designing the UI and UX of the app.
Sure, when you've built a few apps and integrated them to several EHR's, you get the full picture and are able to design your apps to better match the overall experience. But when you're just building your first app, there's currently very little visibility on how the app would actually behave within the EHR once integrated.
App developers would really benefit from exposure on the EHR GUI's where the apps will be integrated in.
