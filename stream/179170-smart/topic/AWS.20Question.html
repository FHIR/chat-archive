Ryan Howells (Sep 15 2020 at 01:33):

Yea, we just heard today @Grahame Grieve from our implementer community that AWS cannot support SMART in the following areas:

  • It cannot support the patient access CapabilitySet. CapabilitySet
  • It cannot support the launch scope or launch-based capabilities (i.e. most Capabilities)
  • But AWS can support the SMART on FHIR resource scopes (patient/<resourceType>.read) and identity scopes (openid profile).

Any help from the AWS folks or others on when these might be supported would be extremely helpful for our CARIN members.
