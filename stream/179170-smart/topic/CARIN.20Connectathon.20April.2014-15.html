Alan Viars (Feb 22 2021 at 20:15):

CARIN will host a Virtual Connectathon on April 14-15. Connectathon attendees will have the opportunity for hands-on testing of the CARIN Consumer Directed Payer Data Exchange (CARIN IG for Blue Button®) Implementation Guide, learn more about the IG, and have the opportunity to ask the IG developers questions. We expect many of the major payers in the U.S. will be participating. The event is free for all attendees.
