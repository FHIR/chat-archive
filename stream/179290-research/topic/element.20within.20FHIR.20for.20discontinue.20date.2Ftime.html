Lloyd McKenzie (Aug 23 2018 at 17:42):

If you're looking at an order that was already discontinued, the stop date would be in the relevantHistory (along with other relevant actions like suspending, resuming, etc. If you're wanting to communicate a request to stop a med, that would use Task. The units for does are inside the Quantity data type.
