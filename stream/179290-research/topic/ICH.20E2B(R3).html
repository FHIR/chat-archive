Jean Duteau (Jul 22 2020 at 14:14):

Yes, sort of. The guide is focusing on FDA submissions to FAERS and VAERS. FAERS currently uses ICH E2B(R2) with local extensions while VAERS uses ICH E2B(R3) with local extensions. I have mapped both R2 and R3 to FHIR but our profiles only handle the Transfusion and Vaccination use cases.
